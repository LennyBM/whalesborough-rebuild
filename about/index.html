A working farm turned five-star resort — where the land still works, the food still grows, and guests still feel like they've found somewhere genuinely rare.

One estate. Everything you need.

Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa is a privately owned 428-acre working farm on the Atlantic coast of North Cornwall, set above the cliffs between Bude and Widemouth Bay. It's been farmed for generations — the landscape, the hedgerows, the stone walls — and that working history is woven into everything we do.

Today the estate is home to 26 VisitEngland 5-Star self-catering cottages, 22 Arvor Suite self-catering suites, The W Club Spa, The Weir Restaurant, and an organic market garden that supplies the kitchen. All within one privately held Cornish estate — no neighbours, no traffic, no interruptions.

Every element of the resort has been built around a single principle: that the landscape is the luxury. The Atlantic cliff path runs through the estate. The farm animals are the activities. The valley with its two lakes is the spa backdrop. Cornwall is not a backdrop here — it's the whole point.

What we believe in

The land comes first

Whalesborough has been farmed for generations. The farm still works — the cattle graze, the garden still grows, the hedgerows are managed. The resort has been built around the land, not in spite of it.

Privacy as standard

One estate, one resort. There's no public road through the property, no passing trade, no strangers in the courtyard. When you're here, it's yours — whether you've taken one cottage or the whole farm.

No hidden extras

Spa access is included for cottage guests. Dogs stay free. The price you see is the price you pay. We'd rather earn your loyalty than win your booking with a misleading headline rate.

Cornish at heart

The food on the menu comes from here or near here. The building materials came from the estate. The paths follow ancient field boundaries. We're not a branded resort dropped into Cornwall — we are Cornish.

Five stars, no pretension

Every cottage carries a VisitEngland 5-Star rating. The W Club Spa, the Arvor Suites, the Weir Restaurant — all built to the highest standard. But the welcome is always warm, never stiff.

Sustainability without greenwash

Solar energy, organic growing, low-impact building, responsible sourcing — not because it makes good marketing copy, but because it's the right way to steward land you care about.

Plan your visit

Whalesborough Farm is located between Bude and Widemouth Bay on the North Cornwall Atlantic coast — roughly 2.5 hours from Bristol, 4.5 hours from London.
