The Facilities

Every room for relaxation.

01

The Indoor Pool.

Our indoor infinity pool is held at a constant 29–30°C — warm enough to melt the journey away, comfortable enough to swim lengths in. In keeping with our natural ethos, the pool is treated with UV light rather than heavy chlorine.

A dedicated children's pool sits alongside the main pool, ensuring even the youngest guests have their own safe, joyful space to splash and discover.

Complimentary access for all cottage guests · Rainbow lighting feature · Swim-in spa area

02

The Outdoor Pool.

Measuring 17 metres by 8 metres and heated to a balmy 26°C, our outdoor pool has a southerly aspect that makes it one of the finest sun traps in North Cornwall. Depth ranges from 1 to 1.5 metres.

Changing rooms, showers, and toilet facilities are available poolside. The outdoor pool is open throughout the summer months.

Open: summer months · 17m × 8m · Heated to 26°C · Southerly sun trap aspect

03

Sauna & Steam.

Our traditional Swedish sauna works with the deep, dry heat that has been the foundation of Scandinavian wellness for centuries. Tensions fade, muscles relax, the body detoxifies, and cardiovascular performance improves.

Alongside the sauna, our aroma steam room uses aromatic botanicals to create a deeply immersive and therapeutic experience — the perfect complement to a morning of swimming or an afternoon of treatment.

Swedish sauna · Aroma steam room · Complimentary for cottage guests

04

The Fitness Suite.

Our air-conditioned fitness suite runs a full line of biomechanically precise Technogym machines — the same equipment used by elite athletes and five-star resorts worldwide.

Alongside the machines, a traditional strength-training and free weights area caters to every training approach. And for those who prefer to take their workout outdoors, Wednesday morning circuits at 7am (£5 per person) offer something more energising still.

Technogym equipment · Free weights · Air-conditioned · Morning circuits: Wed 7am, £5pp

From the Land

The estate is in every treatment.

Gaia Natural Skincare is rooted in the same wild Cornish coast that surrounds Whalesborough. The botanicals in your treatment — sea kelp, wild rose, sea buckthorn — grow in the same landscape you can walk through from your cottage door.

I

Sea Kelp

Harvested from the North Cornwall coast

Dense in minerals, amino acids, and iodine. Deeply hydrating and gently detoxifying. The marine backbone of the Coastal Ritual facial and Deep Restore blend.

II

Wild Rose

Cornish hedgerows — including ours

Cold-pressed rosehip oil, rich in Vitamin C and essential fatty acids. Regenerative and visibly brightening. A cornerstone of the Gaia facial range.

III

Sea Buckthorn

Atlantic coastal dunes

One of nature's most complete plant oils. Anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and deeply nourishing. Used in the Volcanic Path and all prenatal treatments.

IV

Lavender & Chamomile

Grown in Cornish organic gardens

The calming pair. Together they reduce inflammation, ease the nervous system, and encourage deep rest. Present in every massage blend we use.
