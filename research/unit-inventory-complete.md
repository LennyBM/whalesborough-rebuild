## Naming & Categorisation Scheme for the App

Three-level taxonomy. The app's primary navigation surfaces **Level 1**; filters operate at **Level 3**.

**Level 1 — Stay Type (top nav)**
1. **Arvor Apartments** — the ecotel block (5 unit types, 22 physical apartments)
2. **Cottages & Lodges** — self-catering across the 500-acre estate (27 cottages)
3. **Signature Properties** — the trophy stock (Farmhouse, Kew Villa, Nan's House)

**Level 2 — Sub-Type (in-listing groupings)**
- Under Arvor: Studio / Wetroom Studio / Suite / Family Duplex / Penthouse
- Under Cottages: Couples (1-bed) / Standard (4-sleep no hot tub) / Spa (with hot tub) / Premium (5–6 sleep, built post-2013) / Large (8 sleep)
- Under Signature: Heritage (Farmhouse), Villa (Kew), Group (Nan's House)

**Level 3 — Unit (individual booking entity)** — the 34 named units.

URL pattern: `/stay/{level-1-slug}/{unit-slug}` (e.g. `/stay/cottages/whalesborough-farmhouse`, `/stay/arvor/suite-1`).

- Underfloor heating
- Jacuzzi bath
- Sonos / smart speaker

- Suitable for prams (vs Beachcombers caveat)
