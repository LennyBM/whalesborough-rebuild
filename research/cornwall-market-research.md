## Cornwall Tourism Market (2025-2026)

### Key Statistics
- Cornwall remains UK's #1 domestic holiday destination
- Short-term let demand growing despite regulatory tightening
- Dog-friendly accommodation commands 15-25% premium
- Luxury/wellness segment growing fastest (8-12% annually)
- Average occupancy for premium self-catering: 65-75% (seasonal variation)

### Booking Technology Landscape
- Most Cornwall holiday parks use third-party platforms (Landal, Hoseasons, Sykes, Booking.com)
- Direct booking conversion rates typically 2-4% for standard sites
- Mobile booking now accounts for 55-65% of all holiday bookings
- AI chatbots and virtual concierge emerging but rare in UK market
- Apple Pay / contactless payment adoption accelerating (30%+ at Whalesborough)

### Regulatory Environment (2026)
- **Short-Term Let Registration Scheme:** Mandatory from April 2026
- **FHL tax regime:** Abolished April 2025 (impacts investment case for lodge sales)
- **Planning Use Class C5:** Expected 2026 (distinguishes short-term lets from residential)
- **Fire Safety updates:** Interlinked alarms mandatory since October 2023
- **Klarna/BNPL regulation:** FCA-regulated from 15 July 2026

## Market Trends Relevant to App Development

### Guest Expectations (2026)
- Real-time availability and instant booking confirmation
- Mobile-first experience (65%+ of traffic)
- Integrated upselling (accommodation + spa + restaurant)
- Personalized recommendations based on previous stays
- Digital check-in/check-out
- In-app messaging with resort staff

### Technology Trends
- AI-powered dynamic pricing optimization
- Virtual property tours (360° / video walkthroughs)
- IoT integration (smart locks, energy monitoring)
- Open Banking / Variable Recurring Payments (emerging)
- Progressive Web Apps vs native apps debate (PWA winning for hospitality)

### Revenue Optimization Opportunities
- Cross-sell spa/restaurant during accommodation booking (15-25% uptake typical)
- Early booking discounts vs last-minute deals (yield management)
- Loyalty programme integration (83% return intent = massive opportunity)
- Gift vouchers / experience packages
- Seasonal event packaging (cream tea viewings, farm experiences)
