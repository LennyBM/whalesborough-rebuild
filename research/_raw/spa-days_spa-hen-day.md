# Spa Party Package

Our Spa Party Package is the ultimate way to celebrate any occasion with a larger group of friends and family. Available Monday-Saturday, you’ll arrive at 10 am and will be with us until 4 pm.

During that time, you’ll have exclusive use of the outdoor hot tub and spa lounge, with full use of the pool, sauna, steam room and Jacuzzi. Your day includes a 45-minute Gaia Jade Facial or Bespoke massage treatment in our spa, with the option to upgrade your treatments to 60 minutes at a cost of £25 per person.

You’ll be able to enjoy a buffet lunch and 3 bottles of Prosecco to help you celebrate.

This Spa Package is available for 8 to 12 guests and robes/slippers are provided for all.

**45 minute treatments:** £100pp \| **60 minute treatments:** £125pp
