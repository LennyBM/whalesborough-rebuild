# Opening Hours

We’ve put our usual opening hours below. If there’s any change, we’ll let you know through our Facebook page, so make sure you’re giving us a follow. If you’ve got any questions, please message us or email the team on [wclub@whalesborough.co.uk](mailto:wclub@whalesborough.co.u).

[Contact Us](https://www.wclubwhalesborough.co.uk/contact)

#### Opening hours

Access to all our facilities for everyone

**Monday – Friday:** 7:00am – 8:00pm

**Saturday – Sunday:** 8:00am – 7:00pm

#### Swimming pool hours

Access to our swimming pool for everyone

**Monday – Friday:** 7:00am – 8:00pm

_**Adults only:**_

_7:00am – 9:00am & 6:00pm – 8:00pm_

**Saturday – Sunday:** 8:00am – 7:00pm

_**Adults only:**_

_8:00am – 9:00am_ _& 6:00pm – 7:00pm_

#### Gym opening hours

Access to our gym for our gym members

**Monday – Sunday:** 7:00am – 9:00pm

#### Christmas opening hours

Our opening hours over the festive period

**New Years Eve:** 8:00am – 3:00pm (Gym and pool open, Spa closed)

**New Years Day:**

Closed (Gym can be accessed if needed)

**January 2nd:**

Regular opening hours resume
