# Celebrate at the W

Arrive for your celebration with your welcome glass of Prosecco or ‘Nosecco’, and enjoy full use of the thermal facilities which include; Hydrotherapy Pool, Sauna, Experience Showers, Steam Room and Swimming Pool.
