# Our Pool

Whalesborough has been designed to enable you to drift easily from the spectacular infinity pool to the soothing whirlpool; from the cleansing sauna to the clarifying aroma steam-room and refreshing drench showers, making it an ultimately relaxing and renewing environment. Retractable panels allow you to enjoy the view from the pool and mean we can let the outdoors in when the sun shines.

## Swimming Pool

Our beautiful infinity pool is kept at a constant 29-30℃, making it the perfect balance; warm enough to relax and yet still a comfortable temperature to swim for exercise. In keeping with our natural ethos, the pool is treated with UV light.

![Gaia spa treatment](https://www.wclubwhalesborough.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/elementor/thumbs/6a0c2b66-4947-4ed4-9e87-3fcf4abe490e-q87bf0kmr0pa66ciowjasn0gnxzcpacjoof11q4ifs.jpg)

## Spa Treatments

With a range of treatments to choose from, our experienced practitioners use exquisite products from the Gaia Natural Skincare Range, which blends the finest ingredients, plant actives and essential oils. Gaia products are full of intention and integrity, created to nourish and nurture your wellness.

![Sauna](https://www.wclubwhalesborough.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/elementor/thumbs/POOLSHOOT9.1-scaled-qfqny7pleql2km4d8zgjcna4cb4g5sqyrpbg49nxw8.jpeg)

## Swedish Sauna

Comfortable and ultimately cleansing, our sauna offers the classic swedish health enhancing experience. Tensions fade, muscles relax, the body detoxifies and even our cardiovascular performance improves. Time spent in the calming retreat of the sauna offers many benefits.

![Steam Room](https://www.wclubwhalesborough.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/elementor/thumbs/iStock-1284313768-q8wozfcdqygfrm3hh3lp1d31sn6z7v22r71hia5gew.jpg)

## Aroma Steam Room

The Aroma Steam Room, with its comfortable mosaic benches and scented and stimulating eucalyptus steam, brings relaxing and clarifying benefits. The atmosphere in the steam room helps us fight illness and viruses with an increased production of white blood cells, relieves nasal congestion and research has found that regular steam sessions help reduce the incidence of colds and influenza.

![Testing the swimming pool water](https://www.wclubwhalesborough.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/elementor/thumbs/DSC6935-1LR-q7r4c8sbszx1uim520z7m359xgtopbfte04yvzco60.jpeg)
