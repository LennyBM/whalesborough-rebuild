# Sunday Slow Down

Our Sunday Slow Down package is the perfect solo retreat for those short on time but in need of a little pampering before the weekend ends. This package includes a 30-minute treatment of your choice and an hour’s use of our luxurious spa facilities, ensuring you leave feeling refreshed and ready for the week ahead.

Choose from a selection of treatments, including a foot massage, Indian head massage, back, neck and shoulder massage, or facial. Please note, slippers and robes are not provided for this package.
