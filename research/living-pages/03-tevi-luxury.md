## Outdoor living

We’ll start with the views; you’re going to be in such an idyllic location that we want to make sure you can make the most of them.

That’s why there’ll be floor to ceiling glazing that leads onto extensive decking. You’ll not only be able to enjoy the scenery, you’ll feel connected to it. To extend the feeling of outdoor living, you’ll then be surrounded by beautifully landscaped, private gardens. And it doesn’t stop there. Trails across the farm will lead you to golden sandy beaches at Bude or Widemouth Bay. A walk down the hill from your villa will take you onto the stunning canal. On your way be sure to stop off for a light bite at our onsite Weir Restaurant where you can enjoy stunning views over the lake.
