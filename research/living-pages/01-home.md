# Luxury Holiday Homes for sale in Cornwall

Discover everything a holiday home owner could wish for.

Nestled on the North Coast of Cornwall, Whalesborough offers over 450 acres of beautiful countryside, an award-winning location, and exceptional onsite facilities — including a spa, swimming pool, gym, and restaurant.

Whether you’re looking for lifestyle, investment, or both — we’ve created the perfect destination for your holiday home dreams.
