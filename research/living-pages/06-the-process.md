## 𝐓𝐡𝐞 𝐒𝐭𝐞𝐩-𝐛𝐲-𝐒𝐭𝐞𝐩 𝐏𝐫𝐨𝐜𝐞𝐬𝐬 𝐭𝐨 𝐇𝐨𝐥𝐢𝐝𝐚𝐲 𝐇𝐨𝐦𝐞 𝐎𝐰𝐧𝐞𝐫𝐬𝐡𝐢𝐩

Buying a holiday home is a big moment — and we’re here to help you enjoy every step of the journey. With expert guidance and no pressure, we’ll walk you through the entire process so you always know what to expect.

Here’s how it all unfolds:

Step One

## Initial Chat & Discovery Call

We’ll start with an open, no-obligation chat to understand your goals, timescales, and preferences. Whether you’re just exploring or ready to view, we’ll guide you with genuine insight and helpful answers.

Step Two

## Book a Personal Tour

Next, you’ll be invited to tour Whalesborough in person.

You’ll get a feel for the park, walk through available lodges, and experience the spa, swimming pool, gym, The Weir Restaurant and surrounding walks. This helps you picture life here — not just as a visitor, but as an owner.

Step Three

## Explore Available Lodges

From cosy two-bedroom retreats to spacious four-bedroom homes, we’ll show you what’s currently available.

You can discuss layouts, plot locations, lodge features (such as hot tubs or pet-friendly extras), and what best fits your ideal lifestyle.

Step Four

## Understand the Costs & Earning Potential

We’ll provide a clear breakdown of all costs involved, including lodge price, site fees, and any optional extras.

We’ll also show you projected rental income if you plan to let your lodge when you’re not using it — helping you see the bigger picture.

Step Five

## Finance Options (If You Need Them)

While we don’t offer finance directly, there are a number of **specialist providers** who support holiday home purchases.

Once you’ve found the right lodge, we can point you in the right direction to explore what’s available — discreetly and independently.

Step Six

## Ownership Agreement & Support

We’ll guide you through the ownership terms, park licence, what’s included, and how the Whalesborough team continues to support you after you become an owner — whether you’re staying regularly or letting it out.

Step Seven

## Reserve & Get Ready

Found the one? You can secure your chosen lodge with a reservation deposit. From there, we’ll keep everything simple and transparent — helping you prepare for handover day with full confidence.

Step Eight

## Step into Your New Lifestyle

Keys in hand, you’ll be ready to begin your journey as a Whalesborough owner — whether that means relaxing with loved ones, exploring Bude’s coast and countryside, or earning from guest stays while you’re away.
