## 8% GUARANTEED RENTAL INCOME

**Earn up to 8% return with guaranteed rental income at Whalesborough Resort**

Own a luxury holiday lodge at Whalesborough and enjoy up to 8% of your purchase price back each year for three years with our guaranteed rental income scheme.

All new Whalesborough Living lodges are eligible to join our fully managed letting programme, giving you complete peace of mind while your property earns for you—even when you’re not there.

-Guaranteed rental income of up to 8% for 3 years

-No hidden fees – key ownership costs are included\*

-Hands-off letting experience – we market, manage, and maintain

-Enjoy up to 5 weeks of personal use per year

## HOW IT WORKS

Once you purchase your lodge, our expert team will manage everything—from marketing your holiday home and handling guest bookings, to maintenance, housekeeping, and post-stay care.

Your annual rental return is fixed and paid directly to you, helping you budget with certainty while enjoying the lifestyle perks of lodge ownership.

## WHATS INCLUDED WITH YOUR GUARANTEED RENTAL INCOME?

• Insurance

• Utility bills

• TV licence and broadband

• Hot tub care

• Linen and cleaning (excluding personal use)

• Rental commission

• Ongoing maintenance

## STAY WHEN YOU WANT

Enjoy your lodge for up to 5 weeks each year (some seasonal restrictions apply), giving you a perfect blend of investment and indulgence.

## FLEXIBILITY BUILT IN

Changed your mind about letting? No problem. You’ll have options to exit the guaranteed rental scheme before the full three years are up, or switch to our managed sublet programme for a variable return after the term ends.

The 8% rental income is based on the original purchase price and applies only to new sales. Not applicable on resale properties. Lodge usage allocation and exit terms will be clearly explained during your purchase process.
