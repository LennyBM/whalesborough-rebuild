## 𝐌𝐚𝐤𝐞 𝐢𝐭 𝐘𝐨𝐮𝐫𝐬!

𝐔𝐧𝐝𝐞𝐫𝐬𝐭𝐚𝐧𝐝𝐢𝐧𝐠 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐂𝐨𝐬𝐭𝐬 𝐨𝐟 𝐇𝐨𝐥𝐢𝐝𝐚𝐲 𝐇𝐨𝐦𝐞 𝐎𝐰𝐧𝐞𝐫𝐬𝐡𝐢𝐩

Purchasing a holiday home is more than just choosing a lodge — it’s about planning with confidence and clarity. At Whalesborough, we’re committed to providing all the information you need to make informed decisions, without any surprises along the way.

The initial purchase price of your lodge covers a high-spec, fully furnished, and beautifully finished property, set within a premium resort environment. From the moment you step inside, everything is ready — from modern kitchens and stylish bathrooms to outdoor terraces and landscaped surroundings.

Alongside the purchase, there are a few ongoing costs that help keep your holiday home and the resort running smoothly. These include site fees (which contribute to the maintenance of the grounds, communal areas, and facilities), as well as your own utility usage such as electricity and water. We’ll always provide a clear, itemised breakdown so you know exactly what to expect.

If you plan to let your lodge out, there are optional services available to make things easy — such as hot tub maintenance, linen hire, housekeeping, and a fully managed letting programme. These extras are designed to suit your lifestyle, whether you want to maximise income or simply turn the key and unwind.

We don’t offer finance directly and we’re not FCA-registered, but if you’re considering spreading the cost of your purchase, there are **independent specialists** in holiday home finance we can point you towards. Many of our owners have successfully used these services, and we’re happy to connect you with trusted providers when you’re ready.

Whether you’re buying purely for personal enjoyment or a blend of lifestyle and investment, our team will guide you through the cost structure openly and professionally — so you can move forward with total confidence.

**Luxury ownership starts with clarity. We’ll make sure you feel informed, supported, and excited — every step of the way.**
