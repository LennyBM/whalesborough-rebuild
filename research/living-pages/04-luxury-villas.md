### Outdoor living

We’ll start with the views; you’re going to be in such an idyllic location that we want to make sure you can make the most of them.

That’s why there’ll be floor to ceiling glazing that leads onto extensive decking. You’ll not only be able to enjoy the scenery, you’ll feel connected to it.
