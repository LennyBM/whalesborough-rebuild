Owning a holiday home is all about flexibility — enjoying it when you want to, and letting it work for you when you don’t. At Whalesborough, many of our owners choose to sublet their lodge to generate income during the weeks it’s not in personal use.

![8631961-interior25-800](https://whalesboroughliving.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/elementor/thumbs/8631961-interior25-800-r4zblxyc2qy8oqo40gzllib6b33bxp3j5aoo4gwcuc.jpg)

Our fully managed holiday letting service is designed to take all the hard work off your hands. From professional marketing and listing creation to handling guest communication, bookings, and cleaning — we manage the process from start to finish, with complete transparency and support.

Letting your lodge out can help offset annual running costs or even generate healthy returns, especially during peak holiday periods. You stay in control of your calendar, choosing exactly when your property is available and when it’s kept for your own use.

There’s no pressure to sublet — it’s simply an optional service we offer to help owners make the most of their investment.

With high demand for luxury staycations in Cornwall, and our dedicated team managing the details, it’s never been easier to enjoy the best of both worlds: regular time away _and_ reliable income.
