# Asset Creation Brief
## Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa — £500k App Launch
*Compiled 14 May 2026 · Target launch: Q4 2026 · 12-week production window*

---

## 0. Executive Summary

The Coastal Editorial design system demands cinematic, editorial-grade imagery — current Whalesborough assets are good for a Landal listing, but **insufficient for a £500k platform**. Of 163 catalogued URLs, roughly 60 are usable at hero scale; the rest are Landal-template thumbs, low-res sales graphics, or repurposed stock. The build requires a major commissioned shoot (photo + film + Matterport) totalling **£140–215k**, plus copy and brand documentation totalling **£35–55k**. Total asset budget: **£175–270k** (35–54% of the £500k envelope — appropriate for a brand-defining launch).

**MVP cut (£95–140k):** brand film, homepage + 10 hero unit shoots, spa + restaurant shoots, estate map illustration, 30 unit copy decks, legal pages, AI concierge knowledge base.
**V1 add-on (£50–90k):** remaining 22 unit shoots, Matterport library, sustainability film, journal launch content, seasonal sets.
**V2 (£30–40k):** owner testimonial films, lifestyle re-shoots, expanded local-area set.

## 3. Video Brief

### V1. Brand Film — 2:30 hero loop (MVP, Week 1–8)
Treatment: a wordless tone poem, drone + ground, dawn → dusk arc across the estate. Score: licensed instrumental (Cornish composer, ideally — Hannah Peel or similar mood). Opens on first light over the Tamar valley; closes on candle-lit Weir terrace. No dialogue, no on-screen text until final 4-sec brand card. Format: 16:9 master + 9:16 vertical + 1:1 social cut.

### V2. Estate Tour Film — 6:00 (MVP, Week 1–8)
Producer-narrated walking journey: arrival gate → reception → cottage check-in → spa ritual → farm experience → dinner. Mix of drone and Ronin gimbal. Used on Estate Navigator landing page, owner sales pitch, ads.

### V3. Property Walkthrough Films — 12 unit-type films × 60–90 sec (V1, Week 6–12)
One per Level-2 sub-type (not per unit). Format: silent gimbal walk-through with on-screen captions for key features. Used on each unit listing page as autoplay loop.

### V4. Spa Ritual Films — 4 films × 90 sec (V1, Week 7–10)
"The Cornish Hedgerow Ritual," "The Seafarer," "The Botanist," "The Restorer." Silent + ambient sound (water, fire, breath), no narration. Hands and product detail only — never the guest's face. ASMR-adjacent. Used on each treatment page + social Reels.

### V5. Chef Story Film — 4:00 (V1, Week 8)
Weir kitchen + farm walks with chef. Interview-style with B-roll. Used on Weir restaurant About + Journal.

### V6. Lodge Sales Film — 3:00 (V1, Week 6–10)
Trelowen build progression + interviews with two existing owners (anonymised faces optional). **MUST be compliance-reviewed** before release given financial promotions rules.

### V7. Owner Testimonial Films — 3–5 films × 60–90 sec (V2, post-launch)
Existing Trelowen owners. Anonymised options offered. Compliance-reviewed claims only.

### V8. Sustainability Film — 4:00 (V1, Week 9)
Wind turbine, beekeeping, microplastic filters, woodland regen, Calf House origin story.

### V9. Animal Moments — micro-content × 10 (V1, Week 7)
Alpacas, donkeys, lambs, sheep. 8–15 sec each for the Estate Navigator + social.

### V10. Local Area Moments — × 6 (V1, Week 8)
Surf, walking, cream tea, canal, sea pool, Tintagel.

### V11. Seasonal Reels — × 12 (V2, quarterly)
60-sec vertical, repurposed in app + social.

**Video total budget: £55–95k** (DP £1.2k/day × 18 days + drone op £750/day × 6 + edit £15–25k + composer/sync £5–8k + grade/mix £6–9k + post-prod overheads £5k + buyouts £4–8k).

Brief: a hand-illustrated estate map in the tradition of Soho Farmhouse / The Newt / Heckfield Place — top-down isometric perspective, watercolour-and-ink technique, featuring all 30+ named cottages, Arvor block, spa, restaurant, lake, woodland, farm, ANPR gate, EV charging, sea views. Must function as: (1) static hero on /estate, (2) interactive SVG with hover states on app, (3) PDF for arrival pack, (4) wall art for owners. Style: warm parchment ground (#fbf9f6), cognac line work (#703a1d), sage shading (#4a6457), muted watercolour fills, hand-lettered labels in Newsreader Italic feel.
Supplier: a specialist editorial illustrator (e.g. Adam Doughty, Jenny Bowers, or a Cornwall-based equivalent).
Cost: £6–12k, 6–8 week lead.

### Icon Library (custom set)
60–80 icons in a single hand-drawn style: amenity icons (hot tub, EV charger, dog wash, wifi, log fire), filter icons (sleeps, beds, accessibility), category icons (spa, restaurant, lodge, estate), navigation icons. Single-weight outline, 24×24 base grid, 1.5px stroke. Avoid Material Design / Heroicons defaults.
Cost: £3–5k.

### Editorial Spa Illustrations
6 hand-drawn motifs accompanying spa rituals — botanical line drawings of estate species (gorse, sea pink, lavender, rosemary, lichen, hedgerow). Used on treatment pages and the digital spa "magazine."
Cost: £1.5–2.5k.

### Seasonal Motifs
4 illustrated seasonal headers for the Vibe Switcher (spring blossom, summer wave, autumn leaf, winter storm).
Cost: £1.2–2k.

## 11. Critical Dependencies & Risks

1. **FCA legal sign-off on lodge sales** is the long pole — must start Week 1. Any video or copy with "rental return" claims is blocked until approved.
2. **Trelowen completion timing** affects show-home film and Matterport. If build slips, asset capture slips.
3. **Seasonal aerial returns** — autumn / winter aerial must be captured before Week 12 or deferred to V2.
4. **Model releases** — every identifiable person in lifestyle / staff sets must sign a release before publication. Build buffer into Week 6 for collection.
5. **Landal CDN dependency** — if API integration falls through and we redirect to Landal, some assets must match Landal's specs as well as ours (dual-format export).
6. **Rights / buyouts** — negotiate worldwide perpetual buyouts on all commissioned work; budget reflects this. Licensing-only deals risk re-fee on every campaign.
7. **DAM / asset management** — no current system. Recommend setting up Cloudinary or Bynder by Week 4 so assets land into a structured taxonomy from day one.
