# About us

Whether you’re a family with little ones, visiting with your dog, meeting friends for lunch, stopping by for breakfast, or simply looking for a cosy spot with free Wi-Fi while you’re in the area — **The Weir** is the perfect place to relax and unwind.

![347555422_2464275920402021_357268385412652312_n](https://weir-restaurant-bude.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/elementor/thumbs/347555422_2464275920402021_357268385412652312_n-q6vre0j5ja4gaiuv1d45bn56addw0z9sbjhyw6wcvc.jpeg)

Our location

Breakfast, lunch or dinner with a view; we’ve got a gorgeous location overlooking our picturesque fishing lake, which is also home to a wide range of wildlife. Depending on the weather, you can choose to sit inside or outside, and there’s also a large play area if you’re bringing the kids too.

![THEWEIRMENU8](https://weir-restaurant-bude.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/elementor/thumbs/THEWEIRMENU8-scaled-qrotoptaw3xlzt0aj99gb001hlaodn6r3lkb8bs10o.jpeg)

Our Food

All of our food is cooked in our kitchens using only fresh ingredients sourced from the best local suppliers. The freshness of the food is matched only by the stunning interior of the restaurant with its fully glazed facade offering sweeping views down to the fishing lake.

![CareersSlider](https://weir-restaurant-bude.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/elementor/thumbs/CareersSlider-q7k8w4jxshqaexaf43wv35etnpzl0khuct7m30exwo.jpeg)

Our Meals

Eat breakfast (daily from 9am) or lunch at affordable prices from our tantalising menu or just pop in for a freshly ground coffee and mouth-watering slice of cake. You can choose to eat, ‘Cornish’ weather permitting, in our sun-drenched garden and patio area or in front of our real log fire during those colder months. We also have heaters on the terrace for those cold but bright days.
