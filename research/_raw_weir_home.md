# Welcome to The Weir

We proudly serve breakfast and lunch daily, using quality local ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers across the South West. The Weir Restaurant is a dog friendly and relaxing spot to enjoy great food with beautiful views. With indoor and outdoor seating, a kids’ play area, and a scenic lake outlook, it’s the perfect place to relax, unwind, and tuck in.

About our Food:

Over many years, we have formed close partnerships with some of the highest quality producers & suppliers in Cornwall. Our goal is to bring you the freshest & most mouth watering flavors from paddock to plate. We even grow & rear plenty of produce on site right here at Whalesborough Farm.

A lovely relaxing location. Perfect spot for lunch or coffee & cakes to enjoy the nice weather which we’ve been fortunate with. Also comfortable indoors on the one wet day we visited.Reasonably priced food which is freshly cooked. The few meals we had were excellent, including lovely sandwiches all served promptly even on the busy days. Pleasant welcoming staff.

## Janice b. oct 2025

We found The Wier almost by mistake as we had nearly turned back on our walk along the canal from Bude. So glad we didn’t turn back and miss it. It was a blustery day so we were delighted to find we didn’t need to book and they are happy for dogs to be inside. We only intended to have a coffee and maybe a slice of cake, but couldn’t resist the breakfast, the small breakfast was the largest ‘small’ breakfast I’ve ever seen, delicious ingredients and cooked fantastically, and the coffee was hot and lovely. Definitely worth the walk, would highly recommend.
