# Morning circuits

**BLUF:** Wednesday morning circuits run at 07:00 in the meadow weather permitting (indoors at the gym if not). Functional fitness, mixed level, friendly. £5pp drop-in for in-stay guests; included for Pool + Spa Classes members. Bring trainers; mats and small kit provided.

The circuit is a 45-minute session of bodyweight movements, light kettlebell work, short cardio bursts and core stability. The instructor calibrates intensity to each participant; total newcomers and seasoned gym-goers train side by side comfortably.

What to wear: trainers (the ground is uneven), layers (it's an outdoor session and Cornwall's mornings vary), a water bottle. Mats are laid out by the team.

Weather cancellation: if conditions are dangerous (heavy rain making the ground slippery, high winds), the session moves indoors to the gym. The in-app activity dashboard updates by 06:30 with the location.

Suitable for adults of all fitness levels. Pregnancy and post-natal modifications are offered — let the instructor know at the start. Recent injury or surgery: discuss modifications first.

Booking: in the app, up to 14 days ahead. Walk-ins welcome if there's space (cap of 15).
