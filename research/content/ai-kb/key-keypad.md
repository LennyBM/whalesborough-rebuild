# Cottage keys and access

**BLUF:** From MVP launch most cottages use a key safe with a per-booking PIN code (rotated between guests). Trelowen and Gwari spa lodges plus all new-build units have a digital key in the app (BLE smart lock); other cottages will be retrofitted in phases.

Where digital key is enabled: open the Whalesborough app, tap the lodge tab, and bring your phone within 10 cm of the door. The lock confirms with a haptic and a low tone. The key activates at 16:00 on arrival day and deactivates at 10:00 on departure day. Up to five additional guests in your party can be granted their own keys via in-app invite — each is audited separately.

Where the key safe is in use: the welcome email and the in-app arrival screen give you the location of the key safe and a four-digit PIN. The PIN changes between guests; do not share it.

Backup access: every digital-key lodge has a physical backup PIN behind a wall-mounted lockbox by the door. The estate's on-site host has a master mechanical key for hardware failures. If your phone is lost or the lock will not engage, call reception on 01288 361940 — a one-time PIN will be issued within five minutes after verifying your identity.
