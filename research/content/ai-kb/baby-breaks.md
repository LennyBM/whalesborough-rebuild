# Baby breaks and fourth-trimester stays

**BLUF:** Whalesborough has dedicated packages for parents travelling with very small children: the Baby Moon (last-trimester stay, 2 nights), the Fourth Trimester (post-birth recovery, 3 nights) and the Family Restoration package (children under 3, 4 nights). All include cot, high chair, travel-friendly welcome pack and a treatment shaped around the parent's stage.

The Baby Moon includes a pregnancy-safe spa treatment, a take-home herbal tea, a quiet breakfast tray delivered to the cottage and a private slot in the indoor pool out of normal hours.

The Fourth Trimester (recommended weeks 4-12 post-birth) is built around rest: cottage check-in extended to 18:00 to ease travel; a postnatal massage at an extended duration; a Larder grocery box; cottage heat pre-set to a higher 21°C; quiet single-storey cottage prioritised; optional dawn pool slot.

The Family Restoration (under 3s) groups: a child's bed setup, a stair gate at request, a children's welcome pack, a kid-friendly path map and a parent's treatment on day two while the in-suite team can take a planned wander with the buggy. Childcare is not provided.

All baby-break units accept dogs (the standard dog policy applies). Bookings via the in-app concierge.
