# Damage deposit

**BLUF:** A security deposit (£250-£500 depending on cottage tier) is pre-authorised on your card 24 hours before arrival and released within seven working days of departure if no damages are reported. Standard wear and tear is not chargeable.

The pre-authorisation appears as a pending transaction on your statement and reduces your available balance but does not debit the funds. It releases automatically after housekeeping has inspected the property and confirmed no damage.

What we consider chargeable: smoking inside (deep clean £250 minimum), pet damage beyond expected (chewed furnishings, accidents on carpets if not declared), broken glass or crockery beyond a standard 10% buffer, equipment loss (TV remotes, hairdryers), missing welcome-pack items, late departure without consent (full late fee), excess waste left for housekeeping.

What is not charged: a broken glass or two, fingerprints, normal use of soft furnishings, hot tub water marks, sand in the entryway.

If a charge is raised, you will be notified within 48 hours of departure with photos and an itemised cost. You have 14 days to dispute. Aurelia cannot make refund or goodwill commitments — these are routed to the operations team.
