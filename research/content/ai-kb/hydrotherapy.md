# Hydrotherapy and water features

**BLUF:** The indoor pool incorporates a swim-in spa with hydrotherapy jets adjacent to the main pool — warm water with massage-pressure jet zones for shoulders, lower back and feet. Use is included in pool access. Session length is typically 10-15 minutes.

The swim-in spa runs slightly warmer than the main pool (around 33-35°C) and is intended for short relaxation sessions. The jets target specific muscle areas; cycle through them rather than parking on one spot for too long.

Safety: short sessions, hydrate well, do not use after heavy alcohol. Pregnancy contraindications follow the same guidance as the sauna and steam — discuss at consultation. Children must be supervised in the swim-in spa (the higher temperature is not suitable for very young children).

The rainbow lighting in the children's pool area is a feature for play, not a hydrotherapy setting.

For deeper hydrotherapy (Watsu water bodywork, full hydrotherapy circuit) we do not have a dedicated facility — Watsu is offered occasionally by visiting practitioners. Check the events calendar in the app.
