# Arvor Suites — the ecotel

**BLUF:** Arvor (Cornish for "coast") is 22 single-storey self-catering apartments built within the spa complex on the clifftop. Direct internal-corridor access to the indoor pool, sauna and steam room. Stays from £175 per person per night. Five suite types: Studio, Wetroom Studio (accessible), Suite, Family Duplex and Penthouse.

Arvor is designed for couples, walkers, spa-goers and anyone wanting a restorative break. Smaller party sizes (2-4 guests typical), modern interiors, smart TV with USB connection, high-speed Wi-Fi, four-poster or box-spring beds. Two suite types are dog-friendly (Studio 1.0, Wetroom Studio 1.0, Duplex 2.0); Suite 1.0 and Penthouse 2.0 do not accept dogs.

Sustainability features: purpose-built thermal envelope, air-source heating, MVHR ventilation, induction kitchens, all spa-block washing machines fitted with Cleaner Seas microplastic filters.

Each Arvor stay includes: unrestricted pool and wet-room access, self-catering kitchen, full linen change, smart TV, Wi-Fi, individually controlled thermostats, Cornish dairy welcome milk, single-origin coffee and a local biscuit. Robes and slippers in spa packages.

Seven curated Arvor packages layer treatments, hampers and guided walks onto the rate — Wellness, Baby Moon, Fourth Trimester, Anniversary, Long Weekend, Summer Stay and Midweek Escape.
