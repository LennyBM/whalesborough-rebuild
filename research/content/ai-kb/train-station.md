# Travelling to Whalesborough by train

**BLUF:** The nearest mainline rail stations are Bodmin Parkway (75 km / about 48 minutes by road) and Exeter St Davids (90 km / about 1h 15m by road). Bude itself has no station — the line was closed in 1966 — so the last leg is by taxi, pre-booked transfer, or hire car.

Public transport from Bodmin Parkway to Bude takes 2 hours or longer via Go Cornwall bus services with at least one change. This is not a practical onward link for guests carrying luggage or arriving on a tight schedule. The honest advice: book a pre-arranged private taxi or EV transfer from Bodmin Parkway. Indicative price £80-110 one way.

Exeter St Davids has more frequent direct services from London Paddington (around 2h 15m). From Exeter to Bude by taxi is approximately £120 one way; by hire car (Enterprise or Avis at the station) it is around 1h 15m drive.

Guests arriving by train and not bringing a car can use the in-app concierge to pre-arrange the transfer. Slow-travel arrivals are noted at booking and qualify for a 5% discount on a spa treatment as a small acknowledgement.
