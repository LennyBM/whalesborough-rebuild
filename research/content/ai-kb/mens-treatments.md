# Men's treatments

**BLUF:** The W Club treatments are mostly gender-neutral but a short men's-specific menu uses products and techniques calibrated for typically thicker, oilier skin and tighter muscle profiles. Standard treatments are equally suited to any gender; the named "men's" treatments are an explicit fit rather than a separation.

Specifically labelled men's treatments:

- Sportsman's Deep Tissue (60 / 90 min) — firmer pressure, focus on shoulders, lower back, glutes, IT band
- The Man Made Facial (60 min) — deep cleanse, exfoliation, decongesting mask, calibrated for shaving sensitivity
- The Estate Beard Trim & Scalp Massage (45 min) — by appointment with the visiting barber; weekends only

Across the gender-neutral menu, request "firmer pressure" at consultation for any massage if you want a more intense session. The standard treatment menu serves all guests well.

Stag-style group bookings: a group of 4-6 men can book the spa for a half-day with treatments staggered and pool access between. Routed to the human concierge for coordination.
