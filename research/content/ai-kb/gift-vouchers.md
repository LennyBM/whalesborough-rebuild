# Gift vouchers and experience gifts

**BLUF:** Gift vouchers are bookable in the app and on the website for a treatment, spa day, cottage stay or fixed value. E-vouchers delivered by email; printed cards posted by MOO with foil emboss available (5 working days). Vouchers are valid for 24 months from purchase.

Redemption: at booking the recipient enters the voucher code; balance is applied. Partial redemption is supported — buy £200 voucher, use £120 against a treatment, keep £80 for a return visit. At in-person redemption at the front desk, scan the QR code.

The voucher cards themselves use heavyweight uncoated stock with foil emboss; the design is restrained and editorial in line with the wider brand. Personal message in Newsreader italic on the inside cover.

Voucher types:

- Spa Treatment voucher — choose specific treatment or value
- Spa Day voucher — half or full day
- Stay voucher — cottage stay (value-based; recipient chooses dates and cottage)
- Larder hamper voucher
- Open value voucher — applied to anything

Unspent voucher balance does not extend the 24-month validity. Unused vouchers cannot be refunded for cash. Lost vouchers can be replaced if you have the order reference — call reception or use the in-app help.
