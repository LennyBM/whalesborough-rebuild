# Password reset

**BLUF:** Tap "Forgot password" on the sign-in screen. A reset email is sent to your registered email address within 60 seconds. The link expires after 60 minutes. The link is single-use. If you do not receive the email, check spam and confirm the address is correct.

Step-by-step:

1. From the sign-in screen tap "Forgot password"
2. Enter the email associated with the account
3. Check your email within 60 seconds for the message from "Whalesborough Account Security"
4. Tap the reset link in the email (single-use, 60-minute expiry)
5. Enter a new password (minimum 12 characters, including upper, lower, number)
6. Confirm; sign-in proceeds automatically

Common issues:

- Reset email doesn't arrive — check spam, junk, social/promotions folders. If absent, the email on the account may not be what you remember; contact reception.
- Link expired — request a fresh one. Old links are invalid after 60 minutes.
- "Password rejected" — minimum 12 characters, mix of types. Avoid common passwords.

If you have lost access to the email account itself — that is a security recovery situation, not a simple reset. Reception can verify identity through other means and walk you through account recovery. This protects you against someone trying to take over your account.

Aurelia does not handle password operations — these are kept out of the chat for security.
