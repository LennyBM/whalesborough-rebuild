# Mid-stay clean and refresh

**BLUF:** Stays of seven nights or more include a complimentary mid-stay refresh on day four: linen change, fresh towels, kitchen wipe-down, hoover, bathroom clean and bins emptied. Shorter stays are not serviced mid-stay but you can request a refresh for £55.

The mid-stay refresh is a one-hour visit by the housekeeping team. You can either be out during the visit (recommended; the team works fastest with a clear space) or stay in. A "do not disturb" toggle in the app skips the refresh entirely if you would rather not.

What is included: full linen change for all made-up beds, fresh bath and hand towels, fresh tea towels, kitchen wipe-down, hoover all floors, bathroom clean (basin, shower, loo), bins emptied. What is not included: dishwashing, fridge tidy, wardrobe-folding, deep clean.

Extra services available at cost: additional linen change (£25), full clean (£75), oven clean (£30), pet groom drop-off referral. Hot tub water-change is not standard — these are managed on a different schedule by the maintenance team.

Schedule the mid-stay clean for a different day than the standard via the app. Default is day four after arrival.
