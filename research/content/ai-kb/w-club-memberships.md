# W Club membership tiers

**BLUF:** Three monthly membership tiers: Pool & Gym at £85/month, Pool + Spa Classes at £125/month, Full Spa Member at £225/month (includes one treatment per month, classes and member rates). Stripe subscription; pause for one month, max twice a year; pro-rata billing on tier change.

Pool & Gym (£85/month):

- Unlimited pool and gym access during opening hours
- Discounted spa-day and treatment rates
- 7-day booking window

Pool + Spa Classes (£125/month):

- All Pool & Gym benefits
- Unlimited group fitness classes (yoga, pilates, aqua, circuits)
- 10-day booking window

Full Spa Member (£225/month):

- All above benefits
- One 60-minute treatment per month included (carries over up to 3 months)
- 14-day priority booking window for treatments
- Member-only event invites
- Guest pass (1 per month)

DMCC Act 2024 compliance: clear renewal reminders, easy 1-click cancellation, no dark patterns. Cancel any time before the next renewal date. Downgrade pro-rated. Pause feature available.

Aurelia will not advise on whether membership is "worth it" — that is a personal decision routed to the membership team for an individual conversation.
