# Pooch welcome pack for dogs

**BLUF:** Every dog-friendly cottage receives a Pooch Welcome Pack on arrival. It contains a Whalesborough biscuit treat, a dog throw for the sofa, a dog towel, a poo bag dispenser and a printed map of the on-estate and local dog walks. Larger groups of dogs receive a pack scaled to the dog count noted at booking.

If you have a dog profile saved in your account (name, breed, age, treat preference, allergies, anxieties), the pack is personalised on return visits. A spaniel with a wheat-free preference gets a wheat-free biscuit; a senior dog with hip issues gets a memory-foam top mat for the crate. The detail is small — the recognition matters.

Dog bowls are provided in dog-friendly cottages. Dog beds and crates are also in every dog-friendly unit. Soft furnishings — sofa throws, bed throws — are washable and replaced between guests.

Treats are loose biscuits in a paper bag (no nuts, no xylitol). If your dog has an allergy not in the standard pack ranges, bring familiar treats from home — the pack is a welcome touch, not a meal plan.
