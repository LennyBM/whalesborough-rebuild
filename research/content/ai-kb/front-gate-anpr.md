# Front gate and ANPR access

**BLUF:** The front gate uses Automatic Number Plate Recognition. Your vehicle's registration plate is linked to your booking on arrival day during your declared arrival window, and the gate opens automatically as you approach. No code, no fob.

If the gate does not open on arrival, the most common reasons are: arrival outside the stated 16:00-20:00 window, a plate mismatch (different car than booked, lent vehicle), or a booking made under a different name. Call reception on 01288 361940 and the team will open the gate manually within minutes.

On departure, the gate releases automatically as you drive towards it. Cyclists and walkers use the small pedestrian side gate, which is unlocked during daylight hours and reopens via intercom outside those times.

For pre-arrival plate registration, the system reads the plate provided at booking — if you change car after booking, update reception before arrival.
