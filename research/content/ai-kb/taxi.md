# Taxi services from and to Whalesborough

**BLUF:** Reception keeps a list of trusted local taxi firms in Bude that serve the estate. Cars are not on the rank at the gate — pre-book at least 30 minutes in advance, longer for evening or weekend pickups.

Typical fares: Bude town centre to Whalesborough £10-14; Bude to Widemouth Bay £8-12; Bude to Tintagel £35-45; Bodmin Parkway station £80-110; Exeter St Davids station £120-150; Newquay airport £75-95. Fares vary by operator and time of day.

For arrivals: the firms accept advance bookings with a flight or train reference and will track delays. For evenings out in Bude or at one of the dog-friendly pubs (Bay View Inn, Brendon Arms, Coachman's), book the return journey before you sit down to dinner — local cab availability after 22:00 is thin.

Uber is not reliably available in the Bude area. Bolt and similar app-based ride services do not operate in north Cornwall.

Reception can book a taxi on your behalf. The in-app concierge can pre-arrange transfers from Bodmin Parkway or Exeter St Davids as part of your booking.
