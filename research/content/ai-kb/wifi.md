# Wi-Fi at Whalesborough

**BLUF:** Wi-Fi is included free in every cottage, Arvor Suite and across the spa and restaurant. Each unit has its own dedicated SSID and password printed in the welcome folder, with full-fibre speeds in most properties.

Network names follow the pattern "Whalesborough-[Cottage]" and the password is on the welcome folder by the kitchen or in the suite information sheet. The estate uses a managed Wi-Fi controller, so signal handoff between buildings within range is automatic.

Speed varies by location. The Arvor block, the W Club spa and properties closer to the central plant typically see 100+ Mbps. Cottages on the estate's outer edge can dip to 30-50 Mbps, sufficient for streaming and video calls. The lower estate near the canal towpath has weaker signal — phones may fall back to 4G/5G.

Guest Wi-Fi is segmented from the property's payment, EPOS and back-office networks for security. Treat any Wi-Fi connection as you would a public hotspot: avoid entering banking credentials on shared devices.
