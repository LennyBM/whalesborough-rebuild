# Dog-friendly beaches near Whalesborough

**BLUF:** Year-round dog-friendly beaches within 15 minutes: Black Rock Beach (less than 10 minutes), Northcott Mouth (5 miles), Millook Haven (10+ minutes, quieter). Summer-restricted beaches: Summerleaze and Crooklets allow dogs from October to mid-May only; Widemouth Bay has split-zone rules — north end year-round, south end seasonal.

Black Rock Beach: dogs year-round, rocky outcrops, surf waves, rock pools. Best at low tide. Parking limited.

Northcott Mouth: dogs year-round, sandy and rocky terrain. Quieter than Summerleaze. Limited parking — get there early on busy days.

Millook Haven: dogs year-round, large pebbles, dramatic cliffs, quiet atmosphere, limited parking, no facilities. Locals' favourite for a wild walk.

Summerleaze (Bude town beach): dogs October to mid-May only. Big sandy beach, easiest access.

Crooklets: same rules as Summerleaze.

Widemouth Bay: north end (south of the main car park) dogs year-round; south end (around the surf school zone) dogs October to mid-May only. Signs are clear at the access points.

Always check the latest signage at the beach — local council rules can change. Carry a poo-bag and use the beach bins.
