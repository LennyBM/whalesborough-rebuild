# Modifying an existing booking

**BLUF:** Booking modifications — date change, party-size adjustment, cottage swap, dog addition, dietary update — are handled in the in-app concierge or by reception. Changes more than four weeks ahead are generally free if availability allows; closer in, fees may apply if the original cannot be rebooked.

In-stay guests can also modify spa, restaurant and activity bookings the same way. Aurelia can pre-fill a modification request and present it for your confirmation but never makes a change without your explicit "Confirm" tap.

What can be modified: dates (subject to availability and seasonal rate difference), party size (subject to cottage capacity), addition or removal of dogs, dietary or accessibility flags, special-occasion notes for housekeeping.

What cannot be modified post-booking without a fresh booking: changing from one cottage type to a wholly different type after the cancellation window. Cancelling and rebooking applies in those cases.

If you simply need a small admin change (corrected email, updated phone, party-name swap), the in-app concierge handles it instantly.
