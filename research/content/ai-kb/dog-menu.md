# Dog menu at The Weir

**BLUF:** A short dog menu is available at the bar and outdoor terrace for resident dog guests. Items: sausage roll (free), sweet-potato chew, marrow-bone broth and a "Pooch Plate" of small bites. All free for in-stay guests; £4 charge to off-property visitors as a contribution to the dog welfare fund.

Menu items:

- A complimentary Cornish sausage (kitchen scraps, cooled, no salt)
- Sweet-potato chew (£0 / £4 to non-resident dogs)
- Marrow-bone broth (£0 / £4)
- The Pooch Plate — three small bites (£0 / £6)

Ingredients are vetted by a small-animal vet — nothing with onion, garlic, chocolate, raisins, xylitol or grapes. Free water bowls always available.

For dogs with allergies (declared at cottage booking or at the table), the kitchen will substitute. For very small or very old dogs, the chef happily adjusts portion size.

The dog menu is a small kindness, not a marketing exercise — please feed at the right size for your dog and observe normal "no human food on the floor" hygiene.
