# Large groups and exclusive use

**BLUF:** Parties of 12 or more book multiple cottages. The estate accommodates groups of 30+ comfortably across adjacent units. Exclusive use of a cluster (Trelowen lodges, Gwari barns) is possible for events at lead time. Aurelia routes party-of-12+ enquiries to the human concierge.

Recommended large-group combinations:

- Twelve guests: Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa (8) + a 4-bed spa cottage (Warrens, Long Down) or Whalesborough Cottage Spa
- Sixteen guests: Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa (8) + Whalesborough Cottage Spa (8) — immediate neighbours
- Twenty guests: Add an 8-bed (Calf House Spa or Kew Villa) to the Farmhouse + Cottage combo, or a Trelowen 4-bed and a Trelowen 6-bed for couples-and-families splits
- Thirty plus: Five 6-bed premiums (Trevelyan, Sheep's House, Moleyns, Medlands, Arundell)

For weddings, special-occasion celebrations and corporate retreats, the human concierge coordinates: cottage cluster selection, restaurant pre-bookings (long-table sittings), private spa or activity blocks, photographer scheduling, supplier liaison and any tent or external supplier permits.

Minimum lead time for groups of 20+: three weeks. For weddings, six months recommended.
