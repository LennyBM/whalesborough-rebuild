# Kitchen equipment in cottages

**BLUF:** Every cottage and Arvor Suite has a full self-catering kitchen with oven, hob, fridge-freezer, dishwasher, microwave, toaster, kettle, and a coffee machine (Nespresso or filter depending on the property). Premium cottages add a wine cooler. Spa lodges (Trelowen, Gwari) also have soft-bake equipment and quality cookware.

Basic kit included in every kitchen: pots and pans, baking trays, mixing bowls, sharp knives, chopping boards, cutlery for the number booked plus four, wine glasses, tumblers, mugs, dinner plates, side plates, bowls, serving dishes. Tea, coffee starter pack, washing-up liquid, dishwasher tabs, sponge, kitchen roll, tea towels, oven gloves.

What is not provided: salt, pepper, oil, herbs and spices beyond a basic starter. Most cottages have a starter pantry with sugar, salt, pepper, oil and a few essentials, but plan to bring or buy ingredients. The Weir Restaurant sells fresh bread, milk and a small range of pantry items in the morning. The estate's market garden boxes can be pre-ordered for delivery to the cottage — see welcome-hamper chunk.

For special equipment (high-power blender, pasta machine, ice-cream maker) call reception 48 hours ahead and we will see what is available.
