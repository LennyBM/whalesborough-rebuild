# Weather at Whalesborough

**BLUF:** Bude was officially the sunniest place in the UK in 2013 and consistently enjoys above-average sunshine for the country. Expect mild summers (16-22°C typical highs), cool winters (4-9°C typical highs), Atlantic winds, and rapid changes — the coast and the inland estate can have different conditions on the same day.

The Met Office Marhamchurch grid is the closest forecast point and is what the app uses for the on-screen weather tile. Live current conditions are pulled every five minutes; forecasts are 48 hours rolling.

Practical packing notes: a windproof outer layer matters more than a heavy coat from October to April. Waterproof shoes are useful year-round. In high summer take swim kit, sun cream and a hat — there is little shade on the South West Coast Path. Spring (April-May) and early autumn (September-October) are reliably good for walking; the woodland is at its best in late April and again in October.

The outdoor heated pool (26°C, 17m × 8m) operates in summer months only — typically late May to mid-September depending on weather. The indoor pool (29-30°C) is open year-round.

Live tide times for Widemouth Bay are also pulled into the app and refreshed daily from the UKHO data feed.
