# Klarna and Buy-Now-Pay-Later

**BLUF:** Klarna BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later) is available at checkout for bookings over £100. Choice of "Pay in 3" interest-free instalments or "Pay in 30 days" deferred payment. From 15 July 2026 Klarna is FCA-regulated; all BNPL promotions comply with the FCA Consumer Duty.

How it works at checkout:

- Pay in 3 — three equal instalments, first today, then 30 and 60 days later. No interest, no fee if paid on time
- Pay in 30 days — full balance deferred 30 days from booking; no interest, no fee if paid on time

Klarna handles credit decisioning at checkout in real-time. If Klarna declines, alternative payment methods (Apple Pay, Google Pay, card) appear.

Important: BNPL is a credit product. Missed payments may affect your credit file (post 15 July 2026 under FCA rules). Klarna's terms and conditions cover late fees and recovery — review them before confirming.

Aurelia notes: she does not advise on whether BNPL is appropriate for your circumstances. The choice is between you and your financial position. The FCA Consumer Duty requires us to communicate the cost and risks clearly, which we do at the checkout page — please read it before confirming.

Suitable for: spreading the cost of a full-stay booking or a high-value Larder hamper. Not suitable for: anyone who cannot comfortably afford the repayments on the deferred schedule.
