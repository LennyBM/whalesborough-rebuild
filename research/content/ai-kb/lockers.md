# Spa lockers and storage

**BLUF:** Lockers are provided in the changing rooms for in-stay guests and day visitors. They are sized for a swim bag and a folded jacket; they fit medium-sized rucksacks. Key wristband is worn during your visit. Valuables are stored at your own risk — the spa accepts no liability for high-value items.

How they work: at the pool reception you are given a locker number and a waterproof wristband with a key. The locker is exclusively yours for the duration of your visit. Bring small change is not required — lockers are free of charge.

What fits: swimwear, towel, change of clothes, toiletries, a phone, a watch, a wallet. Larger items (luggage, suitcases) are left at the spa reception desk or, for in-stay guests, in your cottage.

Valuables: we recommend leaving expensive jewellery, watches and large amounts of cash in your cottage safe or at home. There is no in-spa safe for high-value items. The locker is secure but not safe-deposit-grade.

End of visit: return the wristband at reception; the locker is reset for the next user.

For day-visitors arriving without a bag, a complimentary cotton bag is provided for swim kit.
