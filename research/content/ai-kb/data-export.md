# Exporting your data (Subject Access Request)

**BLUF:** Under UK GDPR you have the right to a copy of the personal data we hold about you. Request it in the app under Settings → Privacy → Export My Data. We provide it in machine-readable format within 30 days (DUAA allows a "stop the clock" for clarification). Free of charge for the standard request.

What's included in the export:

- Account profile (name, email, phone, address, marketing preferences, account creation date)
- Booking history (all stays, treatments, restaurant tables, Larder orders, voucher purchases and redemptions)
- Stored preferences (dietary, accessibility, dog profile, special-occasion notes)
- Loyalty programme balance and history
- Chat transcripts with Aurelia (where retained — see retention chunk)
- Communications log (which marketing emails / SMS we have sent you)

What's not included: anonymised aggregate analytics, technical logs that don't contain your personal data, security event logs (these are kept for legitimate-interests grounds).

How to request: in the app or by emailing privacy@whalesborough.com. Verify identity (we ask security questions); we have one month to respond (30 days).

Format: a JSON file plus a human-readable PDF. Photos and video from your stay are linked separately (signed URLs).

If we cannot fulfil within 30 days, we extend by up to two months with an explanation. Right to complain to the ICO if you are unhappy with our response.
