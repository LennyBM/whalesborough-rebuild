# Lakeside Locals membership

**BLUF:** Lakeside Locals is a free membership scheme for residents within a 20-mile radius (postcodes EX23, EX22, PL34, PL35, PL33 and PL32). Members get a 10% Monday discount at The Weir, member-only event invites, and early access to Grill and Chill tickets. Sign up at the restaurant or in the Whalesborough app.

How it works: prove you are a resident (a recent utility bill, council tax, or Cornwall library card with the address); sign the consent form for marketing emails (PECR-compliant; opt out any time); receive a digital membership card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet. Present the wallet card at the till — 10% Monday discount applies automatically.

Member benefits:

- 10% off Monday lunch
- Locals-only ticket release windows (24 hours ahead of public) for Grill and Chill, long-table dinners, special-occasion menus
- Monthly Locals' Coffee — free coffee and cake first Tuesday morning
- 5% off at the Whalesborough Larder shop
- Access to a small "members' table" at busy times

The scheme renews yearly (PECR compliance keeps marketing consent fresh). Renewal is a one-tap "still here?" check rather than a fresh signup.

Not a residents-of-Cornwall scheme; restricted to the specified postcodes around Whalesborough. For wider Cornwall reach, see the Whalesborough Circle loyalty programme.
