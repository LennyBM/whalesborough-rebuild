# Tractor rides around the estate

**BLUF:** Tractor rides are a summer-season feature — the farmer drives a tractor pulling a covered trailer around the estate, down toward the beach footpath and back. Twenty seats; runs after the Little Farmers feeding sessions on Tuesday and Saturday at 11:15. Weather-dependent. Free for in-stay guests; £5pp for day visitors.

The route: from the farmyard hub, down the central path past the chickens and ducks, through the woodland edge, to the lower fields with the long view toward Widemouth, and back via the apple orchard. About 45 minutes.

Best for ages roughly 3-12. Babies welcome with adults; under-3s should be held during the journey. The trailer has bench seating, sides and a roof — comfortable for older guests too. Wheelchair access via a portable ramp; book ahead so the team can prepare it.

Weather cancellation: rain at the level that would soak the seats means we postpone — the farmer's call from 09:00 on the day. The in-app activity dashboard updates by 09:30. Refunds for paid day-visitor seats are processed within 48 hours.

Out of summer season (October-April), tractor rides are not scheduled but can sometimes be arranged for groups by request via the concierge.
