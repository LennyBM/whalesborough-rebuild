# Welcome hampers and pre-arrival provisioning

**BLUF:** The Larder is the in-app pre-stay shop. You can pre-order welcome hampers, breakfast hampers, gin tasting boxes, vegan / gluten-free / dairy-free grocery boxes, fresh flowers, champagne and a kids' welcome pack. Items are delivered to your cottage before arrival. The standard welcome hamper is around £85; the breakfast hamper around £45.

Order at booking or up to seven days before arrival — the cut-off varies by item; perishables typically need 72 hours, custom hampers 96 hours. The system pushes a "last chance" reminder seven days before arrival if nothing is ordered.

What is in a standard welcome hamper: estate-grown produce from Neetfield Market Garden where in season, Cornish butter, sourdough from a Bude bakery, a bottle of Cornish wine, local cheese, biscuits and a small jar of estate honey. Tea and a starter coffee pack. Quantities scale to party size.

For dietary requirements: every Larder item carries a clear allergen tag. Vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, halal options are available. Children's welcome packs include a small toy or activity (no nuts).

A photo of the hamper on the kitchen counter is sent to you when it is placed.
