# Off-estate experience partners

**BLUF:** A short list of vetted local partners delivers off-estate experiences with concierge co-ordination: surf school, sea-kayaking and SUP, foraging walks, sea swimming guides, horseback riding, e-bike hire, falconry experiences. Booking and transport are co-ordinated via the in-app concierge.

Current partners (subject to seasonal change and verified through the concierge):

- Surf schools at Widemouth and Summerleaze (group lessons, family lessons, private)
- Bude Canoe Experience (sea kayak, river kayak, family-friendly)
- Stand-up paddleboard hire and lessons at Bude
- Foraging walks with a Cornwall-based forager (spring and autumn seasons)
- Sea swimming guide for Crooklets/Summerleaze (year-round, wetsuit hire arranged)
- Horseback riding from a Marhamchurch livery (under-16s welcome with instructor)
- E-bike hire (delivered to the estate)
- Falconry experience at a nearby raptor centre

Partners are not booked through the resort's standard channels — the concierge holds a hot line to each. Aurelia can pre-fill a request and present it to you; the human concierge confirms within hours during operating hours.

Co-payment: most partners charge directly. Some can be billed to your stay account.
