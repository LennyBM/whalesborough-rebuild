# Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa — address and location

**BLUF:** The full address is Whalesborough Farm, Marhamchurch, Bude, Cornwall, EX23 0JD. The estate sits on 450-500 acres of working Cornish countryside on the north coast, minutes inland from Widemouth Bay and a short drive from Bude town.

Marhamchurch is a quiet hamlet two miles south of Bude on the A39 corridor. The estate is bordered on its west side by the Bude Canal towpath and on its south-west by the South West Coast Path approach to Widemouth Bay. The nearest town for everyday needs (supermarket, pharmacy, GP) is Bude, three miles north. The nearest mainline rail station is Exeter St Davids, then road; Bodmin Parkway is a closer alternative.

Reception is staffed daily and reached on **01288 361940**.
