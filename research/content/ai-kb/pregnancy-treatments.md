# Pregnancy-safe treatments

**BLUF:** Pregnancy-specific treatments are available from the second trimester onwards (after 12 weeks). They include a side-lying pregnancy massage, a gentle facial using pregnancy-safe Gaia products, and a pedicure adapted for swollen feet. Treatments in the first trimester are not offered — industry safety standard. Pregnancy treatments must be cleared at consultation.

What's available in pregnancy:

- Side-lying pregnancy massage (60 min): adapted positioning, light pressure, pregnancy-safe oils
- Pregnancy facial (45 min): pregnancy-safe Gaia products, no actives that are contraindicated
- Maternity manicure / pedicure (45 min): gentle, breathable polish, foot massage adapted for the third trimester

What's not available in pregnancy: deep-tissue massage, hot stone, sauna and steam room sessions beyond 5-10 minutes per session, hydrotherapy at high temperatures, body wraps, peels with active acids.

The Baby Moon package layers a pregnancy massage with a quiet breakfast tray, an out-of-hours pool slot and a take-home herbal tea. Recommended for last-trimester stays.

If you are pregnant and uncertain about a specific treatment, route the question to the spa team via the in-app concierge — Aurelia will not give medical advice or treatment-clearance commitments. The therapist will confirm at consultation.
