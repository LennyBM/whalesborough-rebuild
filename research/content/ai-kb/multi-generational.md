# Multi-generational stays

**BLUF:** For multi-generational stays (grandparents + parents + children), the best single-cottage options are Nan's House Spa (sleeps 10), Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa (sleeps 8 with snooker and cinema snug), Whalesborough Cottage Spa (sleeps 8, ground-floor master) and Kew Villa (sleeps 8). For larger or split-generation parties, pair two adjacent cottages.

Practical considerations: Whalesborough Cottage Spa has a ground-floor master with ensuite for grandparents who would rather avoid stairs. Nan's House Spa is the only true single-stay 10-bed option — generations under one roof, with parking for five cars.

For mobility considerations across generations, Nettlecoombe Spa is the only single-storey hot-tub cottage with mobility-friendly design. Pair it with an adjacent standard cottage for the rest of the party.

Dining: The Weir Restaurant has a long-table option that fits 12-16 comfortably. Reserve at least 48 hours ahead. The kitchen will adapt for picky eaters, dietary requirements, allergies and very small children.

Children of all ages are welcome across the estate. The play areas, the pool, the Little Farmers feeding and the tractor ride suit roughly 3-12. Older children (12-18) tend to gravitate to the SWCP walks, surfing at Widemouth and the spa pool. Quiet rooms (cinema snug, library nooks) suit grandparents.
