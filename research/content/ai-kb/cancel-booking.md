# How to cancel a booking

**BLUF:** Cancel in the in-app bookings tab or by contacting reception (01288 361940). The refund follows the sliding scale: more than 8 weeks ahead, 30% retained; 4-8 weeks, 50%; 2-4 weeks, 75%; under 2 weeks, 100% of the stay fee is non-refundable. See cancellation-policy chunk for the full structure.

Step-by-step: open the app, navigate to "My Bookings," select the booking, tap "Cancel," confirm. The system calculates the refund automatically based on the sliding scale and the booking's current status. Confirmation by email within a minute; refund to original payment method within 5-10 working days.

For spa, restaurant and Larder items: each has its own cancellation rule. Spa treatments — free up to 24 hours ahead, then 50% within 24 hours. Restaurant — free up to 24 hours ahead, then a £10 per-cover fee. Larder — free up to 48 hours ahead for perishables.

For partial refunds (not the full sliding scale, but you're moving dates or reducing party): use the modify-booking chunk's process — sometimes more flexible than a full cancel and rebook.

Travel insurance: strongly recommended for any stay. Standard travel insurance with cancellation cover protects against illness, family emergencies and travel disruption — we will provide documentation to support an insurance claim.

Aurelia hard limit: she will not commit to refunds outside the published policy. Goodwill credits and special arrangements are routed to the human team during operating hours (08:00-22:00 daily).
