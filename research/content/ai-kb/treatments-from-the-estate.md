# Estate-to-Treatment signature rituals

**BLUF:** A short menu of signature rituals uses botanicals grown on the 450-acre Whalesborough estate — wildflower oils, honey from the on-site hives, herbs from Neetfield Market Garden. These sit alongside the standard Gaia treatment menu. Examples: Estate Honey & Oat body wrap, Wildflower Petal facial, Cornish Black Bee Pollen body treatment.

The estate-to-treatment range is small (4-6 treatments) because we only use what we grow in volumes we can sustain. The Wildflower Petal facial uses pressed petals from the meadow that surrounds the kitchen garden. The Estate Honey body wrap blends raw honey from our 15,000 Cornish Black Bees with oat and chamomile.

Seasonality: certain treatments are only available when the ingredient is in season. The Pumpkin & Spice ritual runs September-November; the Lavender Rest treatment runs June-September; the Sea Buckthorn Glow treatment runs October-February when sea buckthorn is at its best.

These treatments are around 75-90 minutes long, priced £95-£135. Often booked as a slow-pace 2-hour spa visit including pool and steam either side.

Provenance is visible — your therapist can show you the meadow where the petals were picked, or the hives where the honey came from, on the in-resort map.
