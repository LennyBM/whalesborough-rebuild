# Awards and recognition for sustainability

**BLUF:** Whalesborough holds Cornwall Tourism Awards Gold 2024/25 for Ethical, Responsible & Sustainable Tourism. Other recognised credentials: Wildlife Trust accreditation, DEFRA environmental recognition, Cleaner Seas Initiative recognition (first UK holiday resort with microplastic filters on all washing machines). Five-star Gold VisitEngland ratings across the cottage estate.

Cornwall Tourism Awards Gold 2024/25: the category recognises commitment to sustainability and eco-conscious experience delivery. The judges checked verifiable evidence of land management, energy use, supplier relationships and community engagement.

Cleaner Seas: not strictly an award but recognised as first-in-UK adoption. Cleaner Seas Group's own communications reference Whalesborough as the lead case study for hospitality.

Wildlife Trust: the local accrediting body is Cornwall Wildlife Trust. Recognition is for biodiversity work — the 50 acres of new woodland, the pollinator strips, the hedgerow extension.

DEFRA: recognition under a specific named scheme; the specific scheme name and dated letter are held in the estate office.

VisitEngland 5-Star Gold: held across most individual cottages. Visit Britain 5-Star Gold on Woody Platt and Sand Parks specifically.

Future certifications on the roadmap: Green Tourism Gold (Q1 2027), 1% for the Planet membership (Q3 2026), Soil Association on the market garden (2027-28), B Corp (target 2028).
