# Parking at Whalesborough

**BLUF:** Parking is free and onsite, with at least one dedicated bay per cottage and additional shared bays near the spa, restaurant and Arvor block. Larger units (8 and 10 sleepers, including Nan's House) accommodate up to five vehicles on their immediate parking apron.

Parking is private property; vehicles are left at owner's risk. Spaces are not numbered for daily guests but each cottage has either a private driveway or a labelled spot. Arvor Suite guests park in the shared apron at the spa-block end of the estate, a short level walk to their door. Day-visitors to the spa and the restaurant park in the visitor area near the bistro lake.

Coach parking is not standard but can be arranged for events and group bookings — call reception in advance on 01288 361940. Caravans, motorhomes and trailers are not accommodated on the estate.

EV chargers are located in a separate marked bay — see the EV-charging chunk for details and current spec caveats.
