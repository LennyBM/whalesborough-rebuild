# Lunch at The Weir Restaurant

**BLUF:** Lunch is served 12:00-15:30 daily. Modern Cornish, paddock-to-plate. 90% of ingredients come from the estate or a 5-10 mile radius. Menu changes weekly with the season. Reservations recommended for groups of six or more; walk-in for smaller parties most days.

Menu structure (rotates weekly):

- Snacks and starters — estate sourdough with whipped butter, soup of the day, smoked mackerel pate, market garden salad with goat's curd, croquettes, Cornish charcuterie
- Mains — sustainable line-caught fish (hake, gurnard, mackerel depending on the catch), Cornish dry-aged beef burger, slow-cooked estate lamb shoulder, mushroom and barley risotto, market garden ploughman's, weekly daily-vegetable plate
- Sides — fennel and apple slaw, kale Caesar, market garden hot vegetables, hand-cut chips
- Pudding — apple tart with estate honey ice cream, sticky toffee pudding, dark chocolate ganache, Cornish cheese plate

A short kids' menu sits alongside (see kids menu chunk). A dog menu is on the bar (see dog menu chunk).

For dietary needs declare at booking and again at the table — allergies are flagged on the kitchen printer.

The Lakeside Locals scheme gives Monday-discount on lunch for local residents (see Lakeside Locals chunk).
