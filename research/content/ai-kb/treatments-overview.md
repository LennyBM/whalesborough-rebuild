# Treatment menu overview

**BLUF:** The W Club treatment menu is organised as rituals and journeys rather than a services list. Categories: massages (30/60/90/120 min), facials, body treatments, hand and foot rituals, signature estate-to-treatment rituals using on-site botanicals, and couples treatments in the double room. All treatments use Gaia Natural Skincare.

Massage durations and prices (indicative; check the live menu in the app):

- 30 min focus massage (back, neck and shoulders) £55
- 60 min full body £85
- 90 min full body £120
- 120 min restorative £155
- Hot stone (60/90 min) £95-£135
- Deep tissue / sportsman's (60/90 min) £95-£135

Facials: standard 45-min £55, results-focused 60-min £85, the bespoke 90-min Restoration Facial £125, the Estate Wildflower Petal Facial (seasonal) £105.

Body treatments: 60-min body scrub £75, the Estate Honey & Oat body wrap (seasonal) £125, the Pumpkin & Spice ritual (autumn only) £135.

Hand and foot: 45-min manicure / pedicure £55 each, gel polish add-on £15, the Cornish Sea-Salt Foot Ritual (60 min) £75.

Signature estate-to-treatment rituals are detailed in the treatments-from-the-estate chunk. Couples treatments in the couples-treatments chunk.

Booking 7+ days ahead is recommended for spa-day-tier packages and the popular weekend slots. W Club members have 14-day priority window.

Aurelia: hard limit on medical advice, contraindications and treatment "is this safe for me" — routed to the therapist at consultation.
