# The Weir Restaurant opening hours

**BLUF:** The Weir Restaurant is open daily for breakfast and lunch. Standard hours: breakfast 08:00-10:30, lunch 12:00-15:30, coffee and cake service in between. Dinner is not served as standard but is available on event nights and Grill and Chill events. Confirm today's hours via the in-app status dashboard.

The Weir is a lakeside bistro and tea room. Indoor seating with views over the resort's lake; outdoor terrace with a large kids' play area immediately adjacent. The fire is lit on cold days; the outdoor seating is heated in shoulder seasons.

Breakfast covers full English, vegetarian and vegan equivalents, smaller plates (granola, yoghurt, fruit), pastries, sourdough toast. Coffee from a Cornish roaster. Lunch is paddock-to-plate: 90% of the food and drink comes from the estate or within a 5-10 mile radius.

Coffee and cake service runs between breakfast and lunch — no booking required, walk in, find a seat. Children's play area open during all service hours.

For dinner, watch the in-app events calendar. Grill and Chill summer events run Saturday evenings May-September. Long-table seasonal dinners run monthly. Special-event dinners (Christmas, Valentine's, Mother's Day) are pre-bookable.
