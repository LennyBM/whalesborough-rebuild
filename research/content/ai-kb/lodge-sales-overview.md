# Buying a lodge at Whalesborough — overview

**BLUF:** Whalesborough sells managed holiday lodges in three ranges: Trelowen (£425k-£525k), Tevi (around £750k) and a Bespoke commission service (from £399k). These are leisure properties (not residential), avoiding council tax and stamp duty issues that affect traditional Cornish second homes. Capital is at risk; income figures are not guaranteed and should not be treated as investment commitments.

Trelowen: contemporary lodges with floor-to-ceiling glazing, private hot tub, premium fixtures. Two, three and four-bedroom configurations.

Tevi: top tier, larger plot, premium specification.

Bespoke: commissioned plan and build, from £399k for the smaller specification, scaling with personal choices.

All lodges sit within the working resort. Ownership is structured as a leisure property on a long-lease with management services included. Rental income through Whalesborough's rental programme is optional but available.

Important — capital at risk and not investment advice: every page that discusses income, returns or yield carries the warning that capital is at risk and that past performance is not indicative of future results. The Aurelia AI does not provide financial advice and will not commit to yield numbers — see lodge-income chunk.

For serious enquiries: book a viewing day, request the brochure, or speak to the lodge sales team. Routed to the human team for any serious purchase conversation. Anti-money-laundering checks apply at offer stage.
