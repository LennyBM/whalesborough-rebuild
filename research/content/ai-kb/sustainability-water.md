# Water stewardship

**BLUF:** Water work at Whalesborough centres on three things: low-flow fittings reducing per-stay consumption, microplastic filtration (see Cleaner Seas chunk) on cottage washing machines, and Ocean Saver plant-based plastic-free cleaning products in every welcome pack. Per-stay water use is modelled and shared on the in-app sustainability dashboard.

Low-flow fittings: showers fitted with aerating heads (5-6 L/min vs 10-15 L/min standard), tap restrictors, dual-flush toilets, dishwasher and washing machine A-class energy and water efficiency ratings. Estimated saving: 30% vs comparable Cornwall self-catering benchmark.

Ocean Saver: plant-based, plastic-free cleaning concentrates supplied for all in-cottage cleaning (washing-up, surface, multi-purpose, floor). Refills available; the bottle stays, the concentrate refills it. Reduces plastic waste in the supply chain.

Microplastic filters: see microplastic-filters chunk — every cottage washing machine has a Cleaner Seas INDIKON filter, first UK holiday resort to install.

What we do not yet claim: greywater recycling, full closed-loop water systems, on-site rainwater harvesting at scale. These are roadmap items; the current claims are limited to what is verifiably true.
