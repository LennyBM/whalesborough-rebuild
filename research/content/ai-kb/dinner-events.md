# Dinner availability and special events

**BLUF:** Dinner is not part of the standard daily service at The Weir. Evening dining is available at scheduled events: Grill and Chill summer Saturdays (May-September), monthly long-table seasonal dinners, and special-occasion menus around Christmas, Valentine's, Mother's Day and Bonfire Night. Pre-booking essential.

Grill and Chill summer Saturdays: outdoor grill cooking over wood and charcoal, paddock-to-plate sharing platters, Cornish wine and beer, live music. From 18:30. £55pp adult / £25 child. Sells out — book in the app on the events calendar tab.

Monthly long-table seasonal dinner: a single long table seating 24, chef-led tasting menu of 5-7 courses paired with Cornish wines, mead and ciders. From £85pp. Themed by season — Spring Pasture, Summer Harvest, Autumn Game, Winter Hearth. Couples and singletons share the table; conversation is part of the format.

Special-occasion dinners (Valentine's, Christmas Eve, Boxing Day, Burns Night, Mother's Day, Father's Day): two seatings, set menu. From £65pp. Booked 8-12 weeks ahead.

For a private dining experience for groups of 10-24, the long-table format can be arranged as a private hire. Routed to the concierge team for coordination — minimum 21 days lead time.
