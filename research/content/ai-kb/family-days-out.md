# Family days out from Whalesborough

**BLUF:** The local area has a strong line-up of family-friendly days out: Tintagel Castle (Arthurian legend), Camel Creek Adventure Park (rides and meerkats near Wadebridge), Newquay Zoo (1,000+ animals), Bude Canoe Experience and the local beaches. Most are 30 minutes to 1 hour by car.

Top family choices, ranked by age suitability:

Under 6s: Whalesborough's own farm and playroom, Bude Sea Pool (with lifeguard cover in summer), Widemouth Bay rock pools at low tide, the gentle Bude Canal walk.

6-12: Tintagel Castle (Arthurian story, the bridge), Camel Creek Adventure Park near Wadebridge (rides, meerkats, 5D theatre), Newquay Zoo (1 hour drive), surf school lessons, Bude Canoe Experience.

12-17 and adults: Tintagel Castle, the Museum of Witchcraft at Boscastle (older children, mature material), the SWCP day walks, surf lessons, sea swimming at Bude Sea Pool.

For wet days specifically: the Witchcraft Museum, Tintagel Castle exhibition rooms, Camel Creek (some attractions indoor), Newquay Zoo (mixed indoor/outdoor), Bude Library, the Castle (small Bude museum), the in-resort spa pool and playroom.
