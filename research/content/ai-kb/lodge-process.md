# Lodge purchase process — step by step

**BLUF:** The purchase process from first enquiry to completion typically runs 4-12 weeks for stock lodges (Trelowen) and 9-15 months for a Bespoke commission. Steps: enquiry → viewing → reservation deposit → contract → AML and source-of-funds checks → exchange → completion → handover.

1. Enquiry — via the in-app lodge tab, website, brochure request or sales-team contact. We respond within 48 hours.
2. Viewing — open day or private appointment.
3. Reservation deposit — £5,000 to take a lodge off the market for 28 days during contract negotiation. Refundable if the sale does not proceed for our reasons; non-refundable if the buyer pulls out without contractual cause.
4. Contract — long-lease purchase contract issued by Whalesborough's solicitors; buyer's solicitors review.
5. AML and source-of-funds — anti-money-laundering checks on the buyer's identity and the source of the purchase funds (HMRC requirement on all property transactions). See AML chunk.
6. Exchange — formal commitment; full balance scheduled.
7. Completion — keys handed over, smart-home setup walk-through, rental programme enrolment if chosen.
8. Handover — first week at the lodge, on-site team available for questions.

For Bespoke: replace steps 3-7 with commission, plan approval, build, snagging and handover — 9-15 months.

Aurelia will not negotiate price, commit to completion dates, or interpret legal language. Routed to the human sales team and to your conveyancer.
