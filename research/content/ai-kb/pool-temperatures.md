# Pool temperatures at the W Club

**BLUF:** The indoor infinity pool is heated to 29-30°C with UV light treatment and runs year-round. The outdoor 17m × 8m pool is heated to 26°C, depth 1m-1.5m, and operates in summer months only.

The indoor pool is the spa's quiet centrepiece — warm enough to relax in and cool enough to swim laps. UV treatment is part of the natural-ethos approach (low chlorine smell, gentler on skin and eyes). The children's pool area has rainbow lighting, a swim-in spa feature and a shallower depth suitable for under-6s with adult supervision.

The outdoor pool sits south-facing — a real sun trap when the sun is out. Temperature is 26°C, which is comfortable for summer swimming. Depth 1m-1.5m. Available throughout summer (typically late May to mid-September depending on weather and demand).

Live pool temperature: in the V2 release of the app, real-time sensor data will show the current pool temperature on the dashboard. At MVP the displayed temperature is the scheduled target — call reception if you want to confirm conditions before heading down.

Pool capacity is managed at peak times. On busy days a soft cap applies; the in-app dashboard shows current occupancy.
