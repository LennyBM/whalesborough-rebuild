# Breakfast at The Weir Restaurant

**BLUF:** Breakfast is served 08:00-10:30 daily. Full English, vegetarian and vegan versions, plus smaller plates. Bread is sourdough from a Bude bakery. Eggs are from the estate's chickens. Coffee from a Cornish roaster (single-origin and decaf). Booking recommended on weekends.

Menu highlights (subject to seasonal change):

- Full English — Cornish back bacon, Cumberland sausage, estate egg cooked your way, mushrooms, grilled tomato, beans, sourdough toast
- Vegetarian English — same plate, halloumi and avocado in lieu of meat
- Vegan English — smoked tempeh bacon, mushroom and lentil sausage, avocado, beans, herb tomatoes, vegan butter on sourdough
- Smoked salmon and scrambled eggs on sourdough
- Boiled estate eggs with soldiers
- Granola with Cornish yoghurt and seasonal fruit
- Pastries — almond croissant, butter croissant, pain au chocolat, Bakewell slice
- Children's breakfast — half-portion English, eggy bread, fruit pots

Dietary: gluten-free bread on request; full allergen menu at the desk; nut-free and dairy-free options as standard. Allergies must be declared to the server — kitchen runs a strict separation protocol.

Booking 24-48 hours ahead is recommended for weekends and school-holiday breakfasts. Walk-in welcome midweek and on quieter days. The in-app dashboard shows current availability.
