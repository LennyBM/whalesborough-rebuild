# Restaurant table booking

**BLUF:** Table reservations at The Weir are bookable in the app. Most days, walk-ins are welcome for 2-4 guests for breakfast and lunch. Bookings recommended for weekends, school holidays, groups of 6+ and any dietary requirements. Bookings open 12 weeks ahead.

In-app: tap the restaurant tab, choose date and time, party size, indicate dogs/dietary/highchair needs, confirm. Confirmation by email and in-app notification. Modification via the same screen.

Bookings hold the table for 15 minutes after the booked time. If you are running late, message via the in-app concierge — the team will hold the table if possible.

For groups of 6+: long-table or two adjacent tables are arranged. Lead time 48 hours for the kitchen to plan. For groups of 10+, route to the concierge team for a dedicated long-table format.

Cancellation: free up to 24 hours before. Within 24 hours a £10 per-cover charge applies (and within 4 hours, the full deposit if one was taken). This protects the kitchen and team — they have already shopped and prepped.

Walk-in policy: 2-4 guests, weekday lunch and breakfast — usually possible. The in-app status dashboard shows whether there is current availability. Saturday and Sunday lunch get busy; book if you can.
