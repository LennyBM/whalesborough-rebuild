# Cancellation policy

**BLUF:** Cancellations follow a sliding scale. Cancel more than 8 weeks before arrival: 30% deposit retained. Between 4-8 weeks: 50%. Between 2-4 weeks: 75%. Less than 2 weeks: 100% of the stay fee is non-refundable. Holiday accommodation for specific dates is exempt from the 14-day cooling-off period.

This policy is presented before payment so the guest acknowledges it at booking. UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 require us to inform you of the no-cooling-off rule before purchase; we do so on the payment screen.

Travel insurance with cancellation cover is strongly recommended for any stay — illness, family emergencies and travel disruption are covered by standard policies but not by us. We are happy to assist your insurer with documentation if needed.

For modifications (date change, party-size change, cottage swap) made more than four weeks ahead, we will typically rebook free of charge subject to availability. Within four weeks, modification depends on whether we can rebook the original. Bring requests to the in-app concierge as early as possible.

Aurelia and the in-app concierge will not commit to refunds, goodwill credits or partial refunds outside this published policy — these are routed to the human concierge team during operating hours (08:00-22:00 daily).
