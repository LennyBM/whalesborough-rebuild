# Lodge brochure and information pack

**BLUF:** A printed brochure and a PDF download cover the three lodge ranges (Trelowen, Tevi, Bespoke), the resort facilities, the commissioning process, the rental programme and the FAQ. Request via the in-app lodge tab or via the website. We follow up with a phone call within 48 hours from a named member of the sales team.

What the brochure includes: high-quality interior and exterior photography of the show homes, floorplans, technical specification (heating, smart-home, glazing, materials, fixtures), plot map, price ranges (current at print date), purchase process step-by-step, rental income picture (with the FCA-compliant capital-at-risk warning), AML overview, frequently asked questions (the 12 standard counter-arguments — FHL tax, council tax, compliance, CGT, growth, tourism, affordability, stamp duty, occupancy, business rates), and contact for next-step conversation.

What the PDF download misses vs the printed brochure: the printed version has a thicker paper feel and bespoke envelope; the digital version is identical content and updates more frequently with the latest price list.

The phone follow-up is a soft conversation. The sales team will not pressure for a viewing or a decision — many brochure requests turn into a viewing weeks or months later. PECR-compliant consent is taken when you submit the request.
