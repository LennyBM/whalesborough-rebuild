# Full list of dog-friendly units

**BLUF:** 31 of the 34 bookable units welcome dogs. The exceptions (no dogs accepted) are Arvor Suite 1.0, Penthouse 2.0 and Eagle's Nest. Standard dog count is two; Beachcombers caps at two, Kew Villa and Nan's House Spa cap at two.

Dog-friendly Arvor units: Arvor Studio 1.0, Arvor Wetroom Studio 1.0, Arvor Duplex 2.0. Arvor Suite 1.0 and Penthouse 2.0 do not accept dogs.

Dog-friendly cottages: Barley Park, Beachcombers (2 dogs max), Westcotts, Venners, Middle Hill, Little Main, Chapel Park, Woody Platt, Sand Parks, Long Down Spa, Warrens Spa, Nettlecoombe Spa, Jack's House, Trevelyan, Sheep's House, Moleyns, Medlands, Arundell, Windy Hills Spa, Gwari 2.0, Gwari 3.0, Trelowen 2.0, Trelowen 3.0, Calf House Spa, Whalesborough Cottage Spa, Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa, Kew Villa (2 max), Nan's House Spa (2 max).

Not dog-friendly: Arvor Suite 1.0, Penthouse 2.0, Eagle's Nest.

All dog-friendly cottages have enclosed private gardens (with one exception per estate copy), dog beds, crates, throws and a pooch welcome pack. The dog shower is shared on the estate near the central facilities for post-walk rinses.
