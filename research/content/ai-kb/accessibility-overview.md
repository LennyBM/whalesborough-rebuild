# Accessibility at Whalesborough — overview

**BLUF:** Whalesborough has one purpose-built fully accessible unit (Arvor Wetroom Studio 1.0) and a mobility-friendly single-storey spa cottage (Nettlecoombe Spa). Other single-storey cottages offer step-free access but were not designed to a specific accessibility standard.

The Arvor Wetroom Studio 1.0 is the recommended choice for guests using a wheelchair full-time. It features a walk-in level-access wetroom with seat and grab rails, wide doorways and a flush threshold. Nettlecoombe Spa Cottage is a single-storey four-bed with a hot tub, walk-in wardrobe and easy circulation — well-suited to mixed-ability families.

Other accessibility considerations across the estate: most pathways are tarmac or compacted gravel and step-free between the spa, the restaurant and the central facilities. The pool has a fixed ladder; pool hoist availability should be confirmed at booking. The fishing lake, woodland trails and farm paddocks have uneven ground.

For any specific need — pool hoist booking, accessible parking close to a particular unit, additional grab rails, profile bed loan, BSL interpretation for a spa consultation — call reception 24 hours ahead on 01288 361940 and the team will pre-arrange.
