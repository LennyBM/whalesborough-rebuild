# Full list of hot-tub cottages

**BLUF:** Fourteen cottages have a private exterior hot tub: Long Down Spa, Warrens Spa, Nettlecoombe Spa, Windy Hills Spa, Gwari 2.0, Gwari 3.0, Trelowen 2.0, Trelowen 3.0, Calf House Spa, Whalesborough Cottage Spa, Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa, Kew Villa and Nan's House Spa. Barley Park has an indoor Jacuzzi bath, not an exterior tub.

By party size:

- 2-sleeper: Barley Park (Jacuzzi bath only — couples romantic)
- 4-sleeper hot tub: Long Down Spa, Warrens Spa, Nettlecoombe Spa (accessible), Gwari 2.0, Trelowen 2.0
- 6-sleeper hot tub: Windy Hills Spa, Gwari 3.0, Trelowen 3.0
- 8-sleeper hot tub: Calf House Spa, Whalesborough Cottage Spa, Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa (Grade II 14th-century), Kew Villa
- 10-sleeper hot tub: Nan's House Spa

All hot tubs are heated to 38°C standard with a 40°C ceiling. Standard chemical maintenance regime; water changed between guests. Towels for hot-tub use are provided in addition to bath towels.

Pre-arrival heating: opt in via the app. Pre-heat supplement of £8 covers the energy cost. See the hot-tub-access chunk for safety notes.
