# Short breaks and minimum stay

**BLUF:** Short breaks (3-4 night midweek or weekend) and 7-night stays are the two dominant length-of-stay patterns. Three-night minimum applies to most cottages year-round, with some flexibility for last-minute gaps. Single-night stays are only available in selected Arvor Suites and only at certain times.

Arrival days: Monday, Friday and Saturday are the standard changeover days. Friday-Monday or Saturday-Tuesday are typical weekend breaks. A Monday-Friday is the classic four-night midweek. Seven-night stays usually start Saturday-Saturday or Friday-Friday.

For shorter stays at peak times (school holidays, August, Christmas, New Year), seven-night minimums apply across most units. Off-peak (mid-January through March, mid-October through mid-December excluding half-term) allows more flexibility — 2-night and even single-night Arvor stays open up.

Single-night last-minute deals: keep an eye on the in-app deals tab or sign up for the dispatch. These are availability-driven and not bookable in advance.

For specific cottage minimum-stay rules use the in-app calendar — sold-out dates and 7-night-only blocks show clearly.
