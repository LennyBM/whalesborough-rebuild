# Facilities for children

**BLUF:** Whalesborough has a children's pool inside the indoor pool building, an Under-6s playroom, an outdoor play area beside the restaurant, twice-weekly Little Farmers animal feeding sessions, summer tractor rides, and 450 acres of safe estate to explore. The outdoor pool is heated to 26°C in summer with a depth from 1-1.5m.

The Under-6s Playroom is indoors, weather-proof and stocked with soft play, ride-on tractors, picture books and quiet activities. It is accessible to all in-stay guests during opening hours. The outdoor play area beside The Weir restaurant has frames suitable for ages roughly 3-10.

Little Farmers feeding sessions run twice weekly during the season and include alpacas, goats, pigs, chickens, ducks and the donkeys. In summer the feeding session is followed by a tractor ride around the estate, the lower fields and (weather permitting) towards the beach. Tractor rides are weather-dependent.

Cots and high chairs are provided in cottages where requested at booking. Stair gates and travel cot bedding are also available — request 48 hours ahead.

Most cottages are family-friendly. The exceptions are couples-oriented or single-bedroom units (Barley Park is a 1-bed romantic cottage; some Arvor suites). The pool, the play areas and the playroom are not staffed — children are the responsibility of their accompanying adult at all times.
