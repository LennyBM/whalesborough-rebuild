# Dietary requirements and allergens

**BLUF:** The Weir kitchen accommodates gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, vegetarian, nut-free, low-FODMAP and most other dietary requirements. Allergens are managed under UK Natasha's Law standards with declared ingredients on every dish. Declare dietary needs at booking and again at the table. Special diets in cottages — the Larder offers themed boxes (vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, kids-friendly).

What we routinely accommodate at The Weir:

- Vegetarian (full menu has 4-6 options)
- Vegan (3-4 options or adaptations)
- Gluten-free (bread, pasta, batter swaps as needed)
- Dairy-free (oat milk, plant butter, dairy alternatives)
- Nut-free (kitchen separated; declared)
- Halal (vegetarian or fish options always; meat is not Halal-certified)
- Pescatarian, flexitarian, low-FODMAP

What needs more notice (24-48 hours ahead): Kosher, ultra-low-carb medical diets, individual rare-allergen accommodation (e.g. severe sesame, soy, sulphite). Call ahead.

For severe allergies: declare at booking. The kitchen runs a separated allergen prep station. The server confirms again at the table. The chef will come out to discuss your meal if you would like.

In cottages: The Larder hampers carry full allergen tagging. A vegan/dairy-free/gluten-free grocery box is bookable. For severe allergies, bring familiar food from home; check the welcome hamper ingredients before consuming.

Aurelia hard limit: she will not give medical advice or guarantee allergen-free meals. Routed to the kitchen and the head chef for confirmation.
