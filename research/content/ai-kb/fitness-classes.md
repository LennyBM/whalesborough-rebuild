# Fitness classes at the W Club

**BLUF:** The W Club runs a daily class schedule covering yoga, pilates, aqua-fitness and circuits. In-stay cottage guests can drop-in for £8 per class; Arvor spa-package guests get four classes free. W Club Pool & Spa Classes members (£125/month) have unlimited access.

Typical weekly schedule (subject to seasonal change):

- Monday: morning yoga 07:00, aqua-fit 11:00, restorative yoga 18:00
- Tuesday: pilates 08:00, circuits 18:30
- Wednesday: morning circuits 07:00 (outdoor, £5pp drop-in), aqua-fit 11:00
- Thursday: pilates 08:00, yoga 18:00
- Friday: morning yoga 07:00, deep-stretch 18:30
- Saturday: family yoga 10:00 (over 8s with adult), aqua-fit 11:30
- Sunday: silent yoga 09:00 — no music, no instruction beyond cues

Class capacity: 12-15 depending on the studio. Book in the app up to 14 days ahead. Members get a 14-day window; non-members 7 days.

The 07:00 Wednesday morning circuits are outdoors in the meadow when weather permits — £5pp drop-in. Functional fitness, mixed level, friendly.
