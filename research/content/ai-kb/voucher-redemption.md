# Redeeming a gift voucher

**BLUF:** Enter the voucher code at the payment step of any booking — accommodation, spa, restaurant or Larder. The balance is applied automatically. Partial redemption is supported: use £120 of a £200 voucher and keep £80 for later. Vouchers are valid 24 months from purchase.

Where to enter the code: at checkout, the "Add gift voucher" option appears above the payment buttons. Paste the code or scan the QR. The system checks the balance and applies it. The remaining balance (if any) is paid by card or BNPL.

If you have a printed voucher card: bring it to reception for in-person redemption. The QR code on the card scans at the front desk. The desk team applies the value to your booking.

Partial redemption: you can use a voucher across multiple bookings until the balance reaches zero. Each redemption emails you a balance update.

Voucher will not redeem? Reasons: code typed incorrectly (re-enter carefully — distinguish 0 from O, 1 from l), voucher expired (24 months from purchase), already fully redeemed, voucher hasn't been activated by the gift-giver (rare; activation is automatic on purchase).

Lost vouchers: if you have the order reference number, reception can re-issue. Without the reference, we cannot replace.
