# Getting to Whalesborough by car

**BLUF:** From the M5 take junction 27 (Tiverton), follow the A361/A39 north-west towards Bude. The estate entrance is signposted from the A39 a mile south of Marhamchurch; satnav postcode EX23 0JD takes you to the front gate.

Driving times from common starting points: Exeter 1h 20m, Bristol 2h 45m, London 4h 30m, Birmingham 4h, Manchester 5h 30m. The final approach is single-track lane for the last 400 metres — reduce speed, expect tractors, and watch for the discreet stone sign on the right. The front gate uses ANPR (number-plate recognition); your plate is linked to your booking on arrival day during your stated arrival window and the gate opens automatically.

If satnav routes you through Marhamchurch village, follow road signs for "Whalesborough" rather than the postcode for the final mile — the postcode resolves to the centre of the estate, not the gate.
