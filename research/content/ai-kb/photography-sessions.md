# Photography and Photo Memories

**BLUF:** Photo Memories is an opt-in lifestyle-photography programme. A photographer roams the estate at agreed times (commonly Saturday mornings 09:00-11:00 during farm activities). Photos of opted-in guests are delivered as a curated gallery within 48 hours of capture. No facial recognition; no biometric storage.

How to opt in: a pre-arrival email asks; the in-app consent toggle is per adult (parental consent for children). Withdrawal at any time — one tap deletes all photos of you from your gallery and from the photographer's roll.

What is captured: candid moments from the farm activities, the spa terrace, the lakeside, the woodland walks. Never sensitive areas (treatment rooms, changing rooms, anyone obviously not opted in).

What happens to the photos: the photographer uploads a raw set tagged with timestamp and geofence zone. The curation worker cross-references your booking, your zone history and your opt-in. An editor reviews candidate matches before publication. Your gallery appears in the app within 48 hours and via post-stay email with a 72-hour signed download URL.

Storage: processed JPEGs retained 12 months. Raw originals deleted 14 days post-stay. Sharing: native share sheet, no auto-watermark unless you select one. Right to erasure honoured within 48 hours.
