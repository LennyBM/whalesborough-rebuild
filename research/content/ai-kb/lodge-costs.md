# Lodge ownership costs

**BLUF:** Annual ownership costs for a Whalesborough lodge include the resort management charge (covering grounds, communal facilities, security, smart-home maintenance), buildings insurance, contents insurance (optional), utilities consumed during owner-use, and any optional rental programme management fee if the lodge is rented.

Indicative annual costs (current at last verification; confirm with sales team):

- Resort management service charge: £6,500-£9,500 depending on lodge size and specification
- Buildings insurance (included or required separately depending on contract): £400-£800
- Contents insurance (optional, recommended): £200-£400
- Utilities (gas, electricity, water): metered to the lodge; varies by usage
- Council tax: none (leisure properties)
- Stamp Duty: none on the leasehold purchase
- Optional rental management fee: a percentage of rental gross revenue, typically 25-30%

Capital-at-risk warning: any rental income picture is indicative and not guaranteed. Past performance is not indicative of future results. Capital is at risk; the leasehold value may go up or down with the market.

What is included in the service charge: grounds maintenance, road maintenance, drainage and water, on-site security and gate access, smart-home platform maintenance, communal facilities access (pool, gym, restaurant on owner-rate terms), 24-hour on-site support.

Aurelia will not estimate net income or yields. Routed to the sales team and your accountant for individual cost modelling.
