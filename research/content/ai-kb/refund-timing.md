# Refund timing by payment method

**BLUF:** Refunds appear in your account within 5-10 working days for card payments, 3-7 working days for Apple Pay and Google Pay, and reduce your Klarna instalments in real-time. The refund returns to the original payment method automatically — we cannot redirect to a different card.

Card payments (Visa, Mastercard, American Express): 5-10 working days for the refund to appear. Your bank's processing time varies; we cannot speed this up.

Apple Pay and Google Pay: 3-7 working days. The refund goes back to the underlying card you have linked to the wallet.

Klarna: the refund reduces your outstanding instalments. If you have already paid in full and a partial refund is due, Klarna issues the credit back to the original payment method (typically a card).

Gift voucher redemption refund: balance is returned to the voucher. The voucher's 24-month validity extends by the time taken to process — no penalty for the refund.

Damage deposit pre-authorisation: not a debit. Pre-auth release within 5-10 working days of departure once housekeeping has confirmed no damage. The pending balance simply disappears from your statement.

If a refund hasn't arrived after 14 working days, contact reception or the in-app concierge. We can check our payment provider logs and chase on your behalf. Aurelia will not commit to a refund timeline outside this published policy.
