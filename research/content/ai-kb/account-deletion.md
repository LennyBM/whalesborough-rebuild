# Deleting your account

**BLUF:** Under UK GDPR's right to erasure you can delete your Whalesborough account at any time. Request in the app under Settings → Privacy → Delete My Account, or by emailing privacy@whalesborough.com. We confirm the deletion within 30 days. Some data must legally be retained for a longer period — see below.

What we delete on request:

- Account profile (name, email, phone, address)
- Saved preferences (dietary, accessibility, dog profile)
- Marketing consent records (your earlier choices are erased; you simply receive no more marketing)
- Aurelia chat transcripts that have not been flagged for legitimate interest retention

What we cannot delete (and why):

- Booking and contract records: retained 6 years (Limitation Act 1980)
- Financial transaction records: retained 6 years (HMRC tax)
- Hotel-guest immigration records: retained 12 months minimum (Hotel Records Order 1972)
- CCTV / ANPR records: retained 30 days then automatically purged
- Health intake records held by the spa team: retained 12 months for legitimate interest in case of post-treatment concerns

These retained items are anonymised where possible (your name and contact details are scrubbed, the transaction record is anonymised by booking reference). After the retention period they are deleted.

Effects of deletion: you cannot sign in, your booking history is anonymised, gift voucher balance reverts to the cardholder (if you hold balance, redeem first), loyalty points reset to zero on a fresh account if you return.

Right to complain to the ICO if you are unhappy with how we handled the request.
