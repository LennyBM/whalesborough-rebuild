# Accessibility-adapted unit — Arvor Wetroom Studio 1.0

**BLUF:** The Arvor Wetroom Studio 1.0 is the estate's only purpose-built fully accessible unit. It is a single-storey couples studio with a level-access walk-in wetroom with seat and grab rails, wide doorways and a flush threshold throughout. Dog-friendly. Direct internal corridor to the spa, pool, sauna and steam room.

Specifications: one four-poster bed, accessible bathroom with shower seat and grab rails, lever-operated taps, accessible kitchen with lowered countertop, induction hob with safety cutoff, smart TV with audio description, USB-C bedside charging, MVHR ventilation, individually controlled thermostats. Wide door frames throughout (820mm+ clear opening).

Direct level access to the indoor pool (29-30°C) via an internal corridor — wheelchair-accessible the whole way. The pool has a fixed ladder; a pool hoist can be arranged with 48 hours' notice. The W Club spa treatment rooms have lift access; treatments can be adapted for guests using a wheelchair — discuss with the therapist at consultation.

For guests bringing a personal assistant or carer: the studio sleeps two; the assistant can be booked into an adjacent Arvor at the carer discount (call reception). Carer's day pass to the spa is complimentary.

Bookings: directly via the app or call reception 01288 361940 to ensure the unit is set up correctly for your specific needs.
