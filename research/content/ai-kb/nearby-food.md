# Nearby food and eating out

**BLUF:** Beyond The Weir on the estate, the local area has a strong line-up of eateries: dog-friendly pubs in Bude (Bay View Inn, Brendon Arms, Coachman's), beach cafes at Widemouth and Summerleaze, harbour seafood in Boscastle and Padstow. The in-app concierge keeps the live local list and books on your behalf.

In Bude town:

- The Bank Bistro and Bar — modern small plates
- Olive Garden — Mediterranean
- Mantra Bistro — relaxed brasserie
- Coastline Café — surf-side cafe
- Various fish and chips at Summerleaze (queue at lunch)

Around Bude:

- The Bay View Inn at Widemouth — coastal pub
- The Brendon Arms by Bude Canal — pub food
- The Coachman's Inn in town — local pub

Further afield (taxi or car needed):

- Rick Stein's Seafood Restaurant, Padstow (hour drive, books months ahead)
- Paul Ainsworth's No. 6, Padstow (Michelin-starred)
- The Cobweb Inn, Boscastle (pub food, dog-friendly)
- The Pig at Combe (Devon side, fine-dining country pub) — 30 minutes
