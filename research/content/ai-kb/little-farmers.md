# Whalesborough Little Farmers

**BLUF:** Little Farmers is the twice-weekly farm-animal feeding session for children. Tuesdays and Saturdays at 10:30. Free for in-stay guests. About 40 minutes. Covers alpacas, goats, pigs, chickens, ducks and the donkeys. In summer, the session is followed by a tractor ride at 11:15 (weather permitting).

The session is led by a farm hand who carries the feed and tells short stories about each animal — how the alpacas got their names, what the chickens eat, why donkeys hate being left out. Children get to feed each species in turn (carrot for the donkeys, special pellet for the alpacas, scraps for the chickens, leftover veg for the pigs). Hand-washing station at the start and end.

Recommended age: walking confidently up to 12. Babies in carriers welcome. The terrain is uneven in places (small slopes, gravel) — a sturdy pushchair manages it; a smaller stroller may struggle.

Booking: in the app on the activities tab. Capacity 15 children per session (siblings count as one for capacity if under 5). Walk-ups welcome if there's space.

What to wear: wellies or sturdy shoes (it can be muddy), warm layer in spring/autumn, no dangling phones or earrings (small fingers and curious animals).

Photographs are fine; please ask before photographing other children.
