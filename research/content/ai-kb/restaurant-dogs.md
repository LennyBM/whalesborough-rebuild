# Dogs at The Weir Restaurant

**BLUF:** Dogs are welcome on the outdoor terrace and at the bar inside The Weir. The main dining room is dog-free for hygiene and comfort. Water bowls, a sausage from the kitchen, and the dog menu are provided. Dogs must be on a short lead and under control.

Where dogs can be: outdoor terrace (year-round, heated in shoulder seasons), bar area inside (small dog-only zone with low stools and bowls).

Where dogs cannot be: main indoor dining room. This rule exists because of food-allergen guests, deep cleaning logistics and the unavoidable mix of dogs and small children at floor level.

Service tip: dog water bowls are at the bar and at the terrace. Ask for the dog menu (short list — sausage roll, sweet-potato treat, marrow-bone broth) which is free for in-stay guests.

If your dog is anxious in cafes or barks, the outdoor terrace and the lawn are more comfortable than the bar. The lakeside lawn (with seating) is dog-friendly and ideal in summer.

The kids' play area on the terrace is dog-allowed; please keep dogs and children supervised together — neither is the responsibility of the other.
