# Hot tub access and safety

**BLUF:** Fourteen cottages on the estate have a private hot tub: Long Down Spa, Warrens Spa, Nettlecoombe Spa, Windy Hills Spa, Gwari 2.0, Gwari 3.0, Trelowen 2.0, Trelowen 3.0, Calf House Spa, Whalesborough Cottage Spa, Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa, Kew Villa, Nan's House Spa, plus Barley Park (Jacuzzi bath only, not exterior tub). Standard temperature is 38°C; the ceiling is 40°C and cannot be raised.

Hot tubs are pre-heated for your arrival. In the app you can set the desired temperature, the on-time and a "ready by" arrival pre-heat. Pre-heating a tub takes four to six hours from cold. An optional pre-heat supplement of £8 covers the energy cost of getting the tub to temperature before your stated arrival; we charge this transparently rather than absorb it.

Safety notes (please read): supervise children at all times; do not use after heavy alcohol; consult a clinician if pregnant, on heart medication, or under recent surgery before using a hot tub; limit sessions to 15-20 minutes; rinse off before entering; do not bring glass into the tub area; the cover stays closed when not in use to keep the water clean.

If the tub appears not to be heating or has a fault, call reception. Do not attempt to adjust the equipment.
