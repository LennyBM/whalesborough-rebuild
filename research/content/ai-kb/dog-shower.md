# Dog shower

**BLUF:** Whalesborough has a dedicated dog shower facility shared between cottage guests. It is a tiled wet-room with low platform, hand-held shower head with adjustable temperature and pressure, and dog-friendly shampoo. Located near the central facilities, signposted from the main car park.

The shower is free for in-stay guests. Open during daylight hours; closed at night to prevent unsupervised use. Towels for drying are provided.

How to use: lift your dog onto the low platform (built for ease), warm water on, rinse, lather with the supplied shampoo (gentle, no fragrance, pH-balanced for dog skin), rinse again, towel off. Allow your dog to shake naturally; the floor is tiled and grated.

When to use: after beach trips (salt water dries to flaky residue on coat — rinse it off), after muddy SWCP walks (clay can stain), before an indoor visit to The Weir's bar terrace, before a fresh blanket on the lodge sofa.

Hygiene: please wipe down the platform after use; the cleaning cloth is hung beside the shower. Used towels in the basket provided.

For very small or very nervous dogs, a freestanding step stool is provided. For very large dogs (Newfoundlands, Great Danes), the platform may be too small — bath your dog at the cottage with the provided dog bath kit.
