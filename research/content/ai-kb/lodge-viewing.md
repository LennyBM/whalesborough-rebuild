# Booking a lodge viewing

**BLUF:** Lodge viewings happen on scheduled open days roughly monthly, or by private appointment. Open days run 10:00-15:00 with tours of the Trelowen show home, the Tevi plot and the Bespoke commission process. Private appointments are 90 minutes one-to-one with the sales team. Both bookable in the app.

Open Day format: arrive to coffee and brochure, 45-minute group walk-through of the show home, 30 minutes on the bespoke options and the rental income picture, 30 minutes Q&A with the sales lead, optional lunch at The Weir (£25pp).

Private appointment: scheduled to suit, includes a deeper conversation about your goals, preferred plot, intended use (personal use, rental, mix), financing options if relevant, and a walking tour of one or more lodges.

What to bring: a sense of your budget, your timeline, the kind of usage you have in mind, any specific questions on the commissioning process or the rental programme. We do not ask for proof of funds at viewing.

Bring children: yes; the sales team is family-experienced. The show home is a working home — children are welcome but please supervise.

Sales meetings are no-pressure: the lodge sales team is happy to talk you through the process without committing to a next step. Aurelia and the in-app concierge can pre-fill a viewing request; the sales team confirms within working hours.
