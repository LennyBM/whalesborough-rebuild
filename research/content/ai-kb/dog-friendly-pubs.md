# Dog-friendly pubs near Whalesborough

**BLUF:** Three reliable dog-friendly pubs within 10 minutes' drive of the estate: The Bay View Inn (Widemouth Bay), The Brendon Arms (Bude, edge of canal towpath) and The Coachman's Inn (Bude town). All welcome dogs in the bar and outdoor seating; book ahead at weekends.

The Bay View Inn at Widemouth Bay: hill-top pub overlooking the surf. Dog-friendly bar, large outdoor terrace, classic pub menu plus surf-themed seasonal specials. 5 minutes drive from the estate. Booking sensible for Sunday roast.

The Brendon Arms in Bude: at the edge of the town near the canal basin. Dog-friendly throughout. Hearty pub food, real ales, large beer garden by the water. Walk-in or short-notice booking generally fine midweek.

The Coachman's Inn in Bude: town centre pub, dog-friendly in the bar and outside. Standard pub menu, generous portions. Friendly local atmosphere.

For an evening out by taxi (no car) or a stop-off after a walk, these three are the go-to. Reception keeps a list of others — and the in-app concierge can pre-book a table at any of them.

For dog dinners: each pub keeps water bowls and most have a small treat dish. The Bay View also has a "dog's beer" (broth-based, alcohol-free).

Last orders: typical 22:30; kitchen closes 21:00-21:30. Confirm with the pub for current hours.
