# Swedish sauna

**BLUF:** The W Club has a traditional Swedish dry-heat sauna at the indoor pool. Temperatures around 80-90°C, low humidity. Recommended session length 8-12 minutes followed by a cool shower or pool dip, then rest. Open during spa opening hours.

The sauna is a small wood-lined room with bench seating. Towel on the bench is mandatory for hygiene. Phones and electronic devices are not permitted — the heat damages screens. Conversation soft, please; many guests are recovering or meditating.

Safety: limit each session to 15 minutes maximum. If you feel dizzy, lightheaded or short of breath, leave immediately and rest in the cool zone with water. Do not use the sauna after heavy alcohol or a heavy meal. Pregnancy use is contraindicated beyond 5-10 minutes per session — discuss at consultation.

Hydration matters. The water station is at the pool entrance; bring a bottle, refill freely.

For first-time users, the recommended approach is: 5-8 minute first session, cool shower, 5-10 minute rest, optional second session if comfortable. Always finish with cool water.
