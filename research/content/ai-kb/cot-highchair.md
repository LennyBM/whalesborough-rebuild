# Cots, high chairs and baby equipment

**BLUF:** Travel cots and high chairs are available free of charge in cottages where requested at booking. Cot bedding (sheet, blanket) is provided. Many family-friendly cottages can sleep an additional baby in a cot on top of the published "sleeps" number — see the unit chunk for the specific +cot allowance.

Request cot and high chair at booking or up to 48 hours ahead. Cottages flagged "+cot" in the inventory accept a cot in addition to the standard occupancy (e.g. Venners sleeps 4+cot; Calf House sleeps 8 with cot capacity in the bunk-room area).

What is supplied: a travel cot frame with mattress, fitted cot sheet and lightweight blanket. We do not provide bottles, sterilisers, baby monitors, baby baths or bath supports — please bring your own. Wipes, nappies and baby food are available at Bude supermarkets a short drive away.

For accessibility-needs babies (specialist feeding equipment, mobility aids) call reception ahead so we can ensure the right pre-arrival setup.
