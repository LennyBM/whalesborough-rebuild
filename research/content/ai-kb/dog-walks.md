# Dog walks at Whalesborough and nearby

**BLUF:** The 450-500 acre estate has several waymarked dog-walking routes from gentle 20-minute loops to 90-minute woodland and field walks. Direct access from the estate onto the Bude Canal towpath, the South West Coast Path approach to Widemouth Bay, and the lower fields toward the beach. All on-estate walks are dog-friendly off-lead in fields without livestock; on-lead near paddocks.

On-estate walks:

- Short loop (20 min) — out the back of the estate, around the wildflower meadow, back via the apple orchard
- Mid loop (45 min) — out to the lower fields, woodland edge, return via the canal-side
- Long woodland (90 min) — through the rewilded 50 acres of new woodland, with views across the estate

Off-estate walks from the estate gate:

- Bude Canal towpath to Bude town and back — 2 hours total
- Lower fields to Widemouth Bay — 25 minutes each way, dogs year-round at the north end
- SWCP segment south toward Millook — 5 miles round trip; the dramatic cliff walk

A printed estate map of dog-walking routes is in each dog-friendly cottage. Digital version in the app with GPS tracking, distance and elevation.

Year-round dog-friendly off-estate beaches: Black Rock, Northcott Mouth, Millook Haven. Seasonal: Summerleaze, Crooklets, Widemouth (split rules — see dog-beaches chunk).
