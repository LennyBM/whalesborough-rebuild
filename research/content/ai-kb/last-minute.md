# Last-minute availability

**BLUF:** Last-minute deals appear in the in-app deals tab when a cottage is unsold within seven days of the arrival date. Discounts are usually 15-25% off the standard rate. Subscribing to the Saturday Dispatch newsletter is the fastest way to hear about the best ones.

Last-minute availability is most likely in cottages without a hot tub (the spa cottages tend to sell first) and in shoulder seasons (March, April, late September, October). High summer and school holidays rarely have last-minute stock.

Deals are not pre-bookable in advance — they exist because a stay has not sold, so they are timed and limited. If you have flexibility on dates and cottage choice, set up an alert in the app for the price band and party size you want.

For genuine same-day arrivals: call reception on 01288 361940. The team can check what is available and confirm a same-day cottage if housekeeping turnaround allows. Same-day rates are full retail; the discount is on stays booked 1-7 days ahead.
