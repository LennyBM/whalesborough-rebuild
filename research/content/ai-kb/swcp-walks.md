# South West Coast Path walks from Whalesborough

**BLUF:** The South West Coast Path (SWCP) is the 630-mile UK national trail. Whalesborough sits a few minutes from the path's north Cornwall section, with direct access from the estate's lower fields. Easy walks (2-3 miles), half-day walks (5-8 miles) and full-day sections are all bookable as guided routes in the app.

Easy walks (under 3 miles):

- Whalesborough lower fields to Widemouth Bay — a 25-minute walk, mostly downhill on the way out, easy gradient back
- Bude Canal towpath from the estate to Bude town — 3 miles, flat, suitable for buggies and mobility scooters
- Crooklets and Northcott Mouth loop — 2 miles, beach return

Half-day walks (5-8 miles):

- Widemouth to Millook and back — 5 miles, dramatic cliff scenery
- Northcott Mouth to Sandymouth — 5 miles, sandstone cliffs
- Black Rock to Crackington Haven — 7 miles, harder gradient

Full-day sections (10+ miles): Bude to Boscastle (16 miles, full day), Boscastle to Tintagel (8 miles), Hartland Quay to Bude (14 miles, very strenuous, recommended only for experienced walkers).

Maps are downloaded as part of the in-app offline pack. Tide times matter for some beach-cut routes — check the in-app tide tile before leaving. Dogs welcome on most stretches; check seasonal restrictions on individual beaches.
