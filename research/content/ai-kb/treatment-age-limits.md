# Treatment age limits

**BLUF:** Spa treatments at the W Club are for guests aged 16 and over. Treatments for 16-17 year olds require a parent or guardian to sign the consent form at the consultation and may be on-site during the treatment.

The age limit reflects industry safety standards: the active ingredients in skincare, the pressure used in massage and the contraindications screening are all calibrated for adult bodies. Specific treatments (deep-tissue massage, certain peels, hot stone) are restricted to over-18s.

For children's hands-on experiences, look at family-oriented activities: Little Farmers feeding sessions, tractor rides, the playroom and the supervised pool area. There is no children's spa programme.

For teenage skincare introductions (15-17), a consultation-only appointment with the spa therapist is possible — no treatment, just product advice for at-home routines. Free of charge for in-stay guests.

Pregnancy-specific treatments and post-natal recovery treatments have their own age and stage rules — see the pregnancy treatments chunk.
