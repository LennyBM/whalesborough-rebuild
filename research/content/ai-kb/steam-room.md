# Aroma steam room

**BLUF:** The W Club's aroma steam room runs at around 45-50°C with 100% humidity infused with a light botanical aroma. Sessions of 10-15 minutes are typical. Open during spa opening hours; capacity is limited to maintain the experience.

The steam room is a tiled cabin with tiered benches. The aroma blend rotates seasonally (eucalyptus and pine in winter, lavender and rosemary in summer). The temperature is gentler than the sauna; the humidity makes it feel warmer.

Safety: like the sauna, limit sessions to 15 minutes maximum. Take cool water between sessions. Avoid after a heavy meal or alcohol. Pregnancy use is contraindicated beyond very short sessions — discuss at consultation. Contact-lens wearers may find the moisture uncomfortable.

Hygiene: bring a towel to sit on. Rinse off before and after. Do not bring food, drinks, glass or electronic devices into the cabin.

The combination people often choose: 8 minutes sauna, cool shower, 10 minutes steam, cool shower, 15 minutes resting in the relaxation room with herbal tea. The therapists call this the "three-stage reset."
