# Day-visitor access to the spa

**BLUF:** Non-resident day visitors can book a Spa Day package or a class drop-in. Standard rates start at £85 for a half-day spa package including pool, sauna, steam, robe and slippers; full-day with treatment from £165. Bookings via the in-app spa tab or via the W Club website at thewclub.try.be.

Spa-day packages are bookable up to 12 weeks ahead and require pre-payment. Day visitors arrive at the spa reception, sign the standard health intake and receive a locker key. Lockers, robes, slippers and towels are provided.

Children under 16 are not permitted on day-visitor spa packages — the spa is adult-focused for day passes. In-stay families have access to the children's pool area and the indoor pool during specified family hours.

W Club members (see membership tiers chunk) receive discounted day rates and have priority booking access (14 days ahead vs 7 for non-members).

Gift vouchers are valid against any spa-day package — bring the printed card or QR code to reception. Vouchers also cover treatments.

Capacity is limited (the pool is a relax-and-restore space, not a public bath). On peak days (Saturdays in summer, school holidays), book early.
