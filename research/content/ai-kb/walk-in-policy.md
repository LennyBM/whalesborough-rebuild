# Walk-in and waitlist at The Weir

**BLUF:** Walk-ins are welcome for breakfast and lunch most days. If the restaurant is full, join the in-app waitlist — you'll get a push when a table becomes available. Average waitlist time on a busy summer Sunday is 25-40 minutes; midweek under 10 minutes.

The waitlist runs through the app: arrive, tap "join waitlist", give your party size and dietary flags. The team holds your place; you can walk the estate, sit at the bar with a drink, or wait by the lake. A push notification when your table is ready.

For larger walk-in groups (6+), expect to wait longer or split the group across two tables. Calling ahead even 30 minutes makes a big difference.

The bar (separate from the main dining room) has a no-booking, no-waitlist policy — pull up a stool, order from the bar menu (a short list of small plates, drinks, coffee, cake). Open during full restaurant hours.

The outdoor terrace seats roughly 30 in good weather — first-come, first-served unless pre-booked.

For dog-owning walk-ins, the dog-friendly seating is the bar and the outdoor terrace.
