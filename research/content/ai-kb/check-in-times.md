# Check-in and check-out times

**BLUF:** Standard check-in is from 16:00 and standard check-out is 10:00. The arrival window during which the gate will open for you is 16:00-20:00 unless an alternative has been agreed with reception. Late checkout to 12:00 is available for £25 subject to availability.

If you expect to arrive after 20:00, contact reception in advance on 01288 361940. Outside this window the gate stays closed and the property may not be fully prepared. Early check-in before 16:00 is usually not possible — the housekeeping team needs the full turnaround window to deliver the standard our guests expect — but you are welcome to arrive at the spa or restaurant ahead of cottage check-in and leave bags with reception.

Check-out at 10:00 lets housekeeping prepare the cottage for the next guest. Late checkout can be requested at booking or in-app and is confirmed in writing. Departure with luggage left in the car while you use the spa or have lunch is encouraged — return your key by 10:00 and your day is your own.

On arrival the ANPR gate reads your plate, your cottage door is unlocked (digital key in app-enabled lodges, key safe code in others), and the welcome folder on the kitchen table walks you through the rest.
