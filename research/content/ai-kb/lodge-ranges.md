# Lodge ranges and pricing

**BLUF:** Three ranges. Trelowen at £425k-£525k for the 2-4 bed contemporary lodge. Tevi at around £750k for the top-tier larger plot. Bespoke from £399k upward depending on commissioned specification. All prices subject to confirmation; current showroom price list available on request.

Trelowen range:

- Trelowen 2-bed — £425k-£475k depending on plot
- Trelowen 3-bed — £475k-£525k depending on plot
- Specification: floor-to-ceiling glazing, full smart-home integration (heating, hot tub, lighting scenes, Sonos), private hot tub, fully equipped kitchen, wine cooler, contemporary finishes

Tevi range:

- Tevi — around £750k, premium plot, premium specification
- Larger square footage, enhanced privacy, four-bed standard

Bespoke commission:

- From £399k for the smaller bespoke specification
- Choice of plot, plan, finish level, smart-home options
- Lead time 9-12 months from commission

What is included: leasehold purchase, internal furnishings and equipment to specification, smart-home setup, integration with the resort management service, optional inclusion in the rental programme.

What is not included: legal fees, stamp duty (none on leisure properties), survey fees, finance arrangement fees, contents insurance.

The published price list is updated quarterly. Aurelia will not negotiate price.
