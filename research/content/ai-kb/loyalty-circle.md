# Whalesborough Circle loyalty programme

**BLUF:** Whalesborough Circle is the resort loyalty programme. Auto-enrolment on first booking. One point per pound spent across accommodation, spa, restaurant, retail and hampers. Four tiers: Visitor, Friend, Member and Patron. Tier benefits include member rates, priority booking, complimentary treatments and (at Patron) a personal Guest Experience Maker.

Tiers and qualification:

- Visitor — first booking, small welcome and account setup
- Friend — 2+ stays or £2k lifetime value, member rates, priority booking window
- Member — 5+ stays or £5k LTV, complimentary treatment per stay, late checkout
- Patron — 10+ stays or £15k LTV, personal Guest Experience Maker assigned (named real person, WhatsApp line), custom welcome, off-season private estate access

Tier upgrade: no confetti, no fanfare. The app displays an editorial line: "You're now a Friend of Whalesborough. We've sent you something." A physical hand-written card arrives by post the next morning.

Points value: 1 point per £1 spent. 100 points = £5 against any service. Points never expire. Bonus events occasionally — autumn shoulder season 3x points, off-peak nights 2x.

Loyalty rules are clear and customer-friendly. We grandfather any future changes. Closing the programme would be telegraphed years ahead.

Aurelia: she can show your current points balance, tier and qualifying spend, but she will not give financial advice on whether a stay is "worth" buying for points.
