# How to modify a booking — payment side

**BLUF:** Booking modifications (date change, party size, cottage swap, hamper add, treatment swap) happen in the bookings tab in the app. Modifications that change the total are handled with a top-up payment or a partial refund based on the original payment method. Apple Pay, Google Pay and card refunds appear within 5-10 working days.

Step-by-step: open the booking in the app, tap "Modify," choose the change you want (dates, party, cottage, add-on, dietary, accessibility), the system shows the new total and either prompts for a top-up payment or queues a partial refund.

For substantive changes (different cottage, materially different dates) the cancellation-and-rebook route may be more economical depending on the timing — the app shows you both options at the modify screen.

Topping up: the same payment methods accepted at original booking. Apple Pay, Google Pay, card, Klarna for amounts over £100, gift voucher balance.

Refunds for downsize: returned to the original payment method. Card refunds 5-10 working days. Apple Pay / Google Pay 3-7 working days. Klarna refunds reduce your outstanding instalments.

For free changes (>4 weeks ahead with no fee impact): the modification is instant in the app.

For paid changes (within 4 weeks): the change is held for 24 hours while you decide; the booking is unaffected until you confirm.
