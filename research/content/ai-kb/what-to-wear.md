# What to wear at the spa

**BLUF:** For a treatment, you typically lie down in disposable underwear or your own swimwear under a heated towel — the therapist guides you. For the pool, sauna and steam room, normal swimwear. Robes and slippers are provided to spa-day guests; cottage guests can borrow them at the pool desk.

What to bring: swimwear, a hair tie, a water bottle (refill station at the pool entrance), reading material if you intend to relax in the relaxation room between treatment and pool.

What we provide: bath towel, hand towel, a robe and slippers for spa-day guests (free of charge), a pool towel for borrowing if you forgot yours. Locker key. Disposable underwear for treatments where appropriate.

In the sauna and steam room you wear swimwear or wrap in a robe; we ask that you sit on a towel for hygiene. Footwear off in the wet zones.

Dress code in the spa cafe and relaxation room: robe and slippers is normal and expected. The relaxation room is quiet — phones on silent, conversations soft, please.

For treatments using oils (massage, body wraps), the oil washes out of swimwear but bring older swimwear if you have pale or fragile fabrics.
