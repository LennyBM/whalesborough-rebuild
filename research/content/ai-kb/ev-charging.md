# EV charging onsite

**BLUF:** The estate has four onsite EV charging points described historically as "super-fast" by Whalesborough's marketing; the actual confirmed spec is rapid AC chargers (most likely 22 kW), with the specific kW per point pending verification. Charging is paid via the operator's app, not the resort.

Sustainability content uses the term "super-fast" but EV industry terminology reserves "super-fast" or "ultra-rapid" for ≥50 kW DC chargers. Based on the equipment fitted, the most accurate description is "rapid AC chargers." The Aurelia content lead must avoid the "super-fast" claim until the installer's spec sheet confirms output.

Sessions are paid by tariff via the charger network's app (commonly Pod Point, Rolec or Project EV — confirm at booking). Allow one to four hours for a typical replenishment depending on your car. The chargers are shared between cottage and Arvor guests on a first-come basis. There are no charger-equipped private driveways at present.

If you arrive with low charge and need certainty for departure, call reception 24 hours ahead.
