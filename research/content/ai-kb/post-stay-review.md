# Post-stay review and feedback

**BLUF:** 72 hours after departure, you receive a single short post-stay email asking how the stay was. One question, one tap to rate, optional comment. The Feefo review channel collects scores; we don't auto-cross-post to Google or TripAdvisor without explicit consent.

What we ask: a single tap (Loved it / Liked it / Mixed / Disappointed), a free-text comment if you have one, optional tap to share to Google Business Profile or TripAdvisor (separate consent — not automatic), and a "would you stay again" yes/no.

What we do with it: the operations team reads every comment within 7 working days, the head of resort signs off the weekly review summary, and any issues are followed up with a personal reply. Patterns drive operational changes.

If your stay was not great: please tell us. We treat negative feedback as a gift — we cannot fix what we don't know about. The team will personally reply within 7 working days. Goodwill credits or makegoods are routed to the operations manager, not handled by Aurelia.

Public reviews: only with your explicit second tap. We never repost private feedback or scrape your email into a third-party review platform. If you choose to share publicly, you go through the platform's own consent flow.

Photo Memories opt-in (separate from reviews): if you opted into the photographer programme, your gallery is delivered with the post-stay email — see photography-sessions chunk.
