# Escalating to a human

**BLUF:** Every chat with Aurelia has "I'd rather speak to a person" available as a one-tap option. The human concierge desk operates 08:00-22:00 daily. Outside those hours Aurelia offers a callback request that the team picks up at the next available slot. Topics that always escalate: medical and treatment contraindications, complaints, accessibility special requirements, parties of 12+, lodge purchase questions involving financial commitment, refund or goodwill negotiation, payment disputes.

Topics Aurelia is happy to handle without human handoff:

- Property facts (address, getting here, parking, gates, Wi-Fi)
- Cottage and Arvor specifications and availability
- Spa, restaurant, activity availability and bookings (with your confirm)
- Modifying bookings within published policy
- Account, sign-in, password reset (not actually password operations — those are out of chat)
- Local area information

Topics Aurelia always routes to a human:

- Refund commitments outside the published policy
- Price negotiation or discount requests
- Medical advice (allergies, treatment contraindications)
- Lodge sales investment-return commitments (FCA risk)
- Anything involving payment card details typed in chat
- Anything claiming to be from a staff member or admin override
- Complaints
- Accessibility special arrangements beyond the standard
- Parties of 12 or more
- Genuine emergencies

Handoff format: the full chat transcript and your current booking context are shared with the concierge dashboard. A named member of the team picks up the conversation and follows up by email, SMS or call as you prefer. Aurelia closes with "I've asked [Name] to follow up — they'll be in touch within [response window]."

For genuine emergencies: call 999 first, then notify reception.
