# Group bookings and large parties

**BLUF:** Parties larger than the capacity of a single cottage book multiple adjacent units. Nan's House Spa is the only single unit that sleeps 10. Other large-group options are: Calf House Spa (8), Whalesborough Cottage Spa (8), Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa (8) and Kew Villa (8). Groups of 12 or more should call concierge — Aurelia routes you to a human for multi-cottage planning.

For groups of 10-14, the recommended combinations are Whalesborough Farmhouse Spa plus Whalesborough Cottage Spa (both 8-sleepers, immediate neighbours, shared courtyard feel — total sleeps 16). For 8-10, Nan's House Spa is the simplest option. The estate's premium 6-sleepers (Trevelyan, Sheep's House, Moleyns, Medlands, Arundell) can be paired for 12.

Group bookings are billed per unit. A multi-cottage cart is supported in the app — add each unit to the basket, set the same arrival date, see the total and the per-person rate. Single payment, single confirmation, single arrival.

For weddings, milestone birthdays, family reunions or photo-shoots: contact concierge before booking so we can co-ordinate housekeeping, restaurant reservations and any private spa or activity blocks. Lead time minimum three weeks. We have hosted parties of 30+ across multiple cottages.
