# Sea-view and valley-view cottages

**BLUF:** Whalesborough is an inland estate, so direct sea views from cottage windows are limited. The premium 6-bed cottages (Trevelyan, Sheep's House, Moleyns, Medlands, Arundell) have valley vistas and balconies; Arvor Suites have countryside-towards-coast views from the clifftop block; the lower-estate woodland cottages have garden and field outlooks.

For genuinely sea-edge views you go to the South West Coast Path five minutes from the estate. The Arvor block has the best long-range coastal views — particularly the Penthouse 2.0 — from the top floor over the cliff-edge fields.

Specific cottages with notable outdoor outlooks:

- Trevelyan, Sheep's House, Moleyns, Medlands, Arundell — balconies with valley views across the estate
- Sand Parks — canal-view garden access
- Whalesborough Cottage Spa — stunning views across the former Whalesborough Estate (its own marketing copy)
- Trelowen lodges — floor-to-ceiling glazing over fields toward the coast

If sea view is the primary requirement, the Arvor Suites or a hotel on the immediate Bude coast may be a better choice. We will be honest about this — the value of Whalesborough is the working farm, the woodland and the spa, with the coast a short walk away rather than the front-row view.
