# Robes and slippers at the spa

**BLUF:** Robes and slippers are included free for spa-day visitors and Arvor spa-package guests; cottage guests can borrow them at the pool desk for a session. The robes are heavyweight white waffle; slippers are open-back foam — both replaced and laundered between guests.

How to access: at the pool reception, you are issued a robe and slippers when you check in for a spa day. In-stay cottage guests can ask at the desk to borrow a set for a specific visit and return it before leaving the spa. Returns are at the same desk.

If you would like a robe and slippers throughout your stay, two sizes are available: medium and large. Some Arvor packages include a takeaway "stay robe" — yours to use through the stay; left in the suite on departure for laundering.

Children's robes: limited sizes available at the spa reception for older children using the family pool sessions; ask at the desk.

Robes are not for off-property use — please do not wear them to the restaurant unless you are coming directly from the spa, and not to dinner. The restaurant team is happy to see robes at breakfast.
