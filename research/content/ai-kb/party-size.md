# Party size and occupancy

**BLUF:** Each cottage has a published "sleeps" number that is the maximum overnight party size. Exceeding it is not permitted: it breaks the fire-safety capacity assessment, the council registration and the insurance. Some cottages allow an additional baby in a cot (+cot units) above the published number.

How occupancy is counted: every adult and every child over the age of 24 months counts as one. Infants under 24 months in a travel cot do not count in +cot units. Day visitors are welcome at your cottage during the day but must not stay overnight without prior consent.

If you have extra family wanting to visit for a meal or an afternoon, that is fine — they should park at the visitor area and you walk them in. Day-visitors cannot use the spa or pool without a paid day pass.

Splitting a large party across two adjacent cottages is the recommended pattern for groups beyond a single unit's capacity. See the group-bookings chunk for combinations.

If your party size changes between booking and arrival (someone drops out, a friend joins), update via the in-app concierge so we can adjust the welcome hamper, dog count and housekeeping setup.
