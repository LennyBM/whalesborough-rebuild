# Gaia Natural Skincare in the spa

**BLUF:** The W Club uses Gaia Natural Skincare across the treatment menu. Gaia is a Cornish-made brand that blends fine ingredients, plant actives and essential oils. The full product range is available for retail in the spa shop — bring home what worked in your treatment.

What we use in treatments: facial cleansers, exfoliants, serums, moisturisers, body oils, body scrubs, hand and foot creams. The therapist matches products to your skin profile during consultation and notes which Gaia line was used so you can buy the same at retail or order online afterwards.

Retail prices typically: cleansers £24-32, serums £45-65, moisturisers £38-52, body oils £40-55, gift sets from £75. Members and stay guests have a 10% retail discount when buying directly after a treatment.

Sustainability: Gaia is a small Cornish manufacturer; product miles are low. Glass and recyclable packaging. No animal testing. Vegetarian; some products vegan — check the label.

For a personal product consultation (no treatment, just product advice), book a free 20-minute consultation with the spa therapist in the app.
