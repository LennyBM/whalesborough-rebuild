# The W Club spa opening hours

**BLUF:** The W Club spa is typically open daily 08:00-21:00 for in-stay guests; treatment hours run 09:00-19:00. Day-visitor access is bookable for 09:30-18:00 windows. The exact hours by day are displayed in the in-app Estate Status Dashboard and refreshed daily.

In-stay guests in cottages and Arvor Suites have unrestricted access to the indoor pool, sauna, steam room and gym during opening hours. Robes, slippers and towels are provided at the pool desk for spa-package guests; cottage guests bring their own pool towels or borrow one at the desk.

Treatment rooms close 30 minutes before the overall spa closes; book treatments to end at least half an hour before closing.

Day-visitor spa-day packages (see day-visitor-access chunk) have specific arrival windows — typically 09:30 or 12:00.

For exclusive pool slots (out-of-hours, private hire), call reception ahead. These are subject to availability and a fee.

Aurelia surfaces today's spa hours and current availability from the in-app dashboard — ask "what's the pool like right now" and the answer is pulled live from the booking system.
