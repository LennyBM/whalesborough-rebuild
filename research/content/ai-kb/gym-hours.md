# The W Club gym hours and equipment

**BLUF:** The air-conditioned gym is open daily during spa hours (typically 08:00-21:00) and is included for in-stay guests, spa-day visitors and W Club members. Equipment is biomechanically precise Technogym machines plus a free-weights and strength-training area.

What is in the gym: full Technogym cardio line (treadmills, bikes, rowers, cross-trainers), Technogym strength stations covering chest, back, legs, arms and core, free-weight rack with dumbbells (1-30 kg) and barbells, bench, kettlebells, resistance bands, foam rollers, yoga mats.

The gym is unstaffed during standard hours — a member of the spa team is available to demonstrate equipment on request. For a personal training session, the resident PT works limited hours; book via the in-app spa tab. Sessions are £55 for 45 minutes.

Etiquette: wipe equipment after use (wipes at the station), return weights, towel on the bench, drink water from a refillable bottle (no glass).

Children under 16 are not permitted in the gym for safety reasons. Children 14-16 may use specific cardio equipment with a parent present for supervised periods — speak to spa reception ahead.
