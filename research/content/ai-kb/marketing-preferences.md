# Marketing preferences and PECR consent

**BLUF:** Email and SMS marketing are separately controllable in your account under Settings → Communications. PECR compliance means we ask for consent separately for each channel and you can withdraw at any time. Holiday-stay marketing follows soft opt-in rules; lodge sales marketing requires explicit consent.

What we may send if you consent:

- Saturday Dispatch — weekly editorial newsletter, 600-900 words, slow content
- "Today at Whalesborough" — in-stay morning brief (07:30 push notification)
- Booking confirmation, pre-arrival, post-stay emails (transactional, not marketing — always sent)
- Loyalty programme tier upgrades and tier-specific offers
- Seasonal offers (Christmas, summer holidays, last-minute)
- Lodge sales marketing — explicit opt-in only, separately controlled
- Lakeside Locals event invites — only if you are a member
- W Club Membership Tier offers — only if you are a member

Separate controls: email channel, SMS channel, lodge-sales channel, push notification, post (for printed cards). Granular opt-outs honoured immediately.

Withdraw consent: in the app, in any email's unsubscribe link, or by reply STOP to any SMS. Within 24 hours your account stops receiving marketing in that channel.

PECR rule: marketing emails for holiday products to existing customers can be sent under soft opt-in if obtained during a transaction with the option to refuse. Lodge sales is a "different product" and requires explicit consent.

DUAA 2025 update: from 19 June 2026 a formal data protection complaints procedure is in place; details on the privacy page.
