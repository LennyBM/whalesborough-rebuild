# Spa health intake and consultation

**BLUF:** Every spa visit starts with a short health intake — pregnancy, allergies, recent surgery, current medication, skin sensitivities and any conditions that affect pressure or product choice. Intake is on a digital form before arrival or on iPad in the spa reception. The form is private to the spa team.

What we ask: any pregnancy or breastfeeding, recent surgery (within 6 months), heart conditions, high or low blood pressure, blood-thinner medication, skin conditions (eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, recent sunburn), known allergies (nuts, fragrances, latex), recent injuries, areas to avoid.

The form is reviewed by the therapist before treatment; anything flagged is discussed in a one-minute consultation at the start of your appointment. Treatments may be modified to be safe and effective for you — pressure adjusted, product swapped, areas avoided.

If you decline to provide intake information, the therapist may decline the treatment for your safety. This is industry standard — we are not insured to treat without intake.

Information from intake is held only by the spa team, retained for 12 months (in line with hospitality records guidance under DUAA), and not shared with marketing, the booking system or anyone else. It is treated as Special Category personal data.
