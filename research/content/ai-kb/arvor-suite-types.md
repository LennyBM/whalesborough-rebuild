# Arvor Suite types

**BLUF:** Arvor has five suite types across 22 physical apartments: Studio 1.0, Wetroom Studio 1.0 (accessible), Suite 1.0, Family Duplex 2.0 and Penthouse 2.0. All are single-storey accessible from the spa complex via internal corridor. Two-person to four-person capacities. Stays from £175pppn including pool and wet-room access.

Studio 1.0 (2 sleeps, dog-friendly):

- Four-poster bed, smart TV, induction kitchen, single-storey
- Couples retreat or solo wellness break
- £175pppn typical

Wetroom Studio 1.0 (2 sleeps, dog-friendly, accessible):

- Walk-in wetroom with seat and grab rails, accessible
- Same four-poster as Studio 1.0
- Same price tier

Suite 1.0 (2 sleeps, no dogs):

- Modern suite with box-spring bed
- Couples-focused boutique
- Top-volume seller in the Arvor block

Family Duplex 2.0 (4 sleeps, dog-friendly):

- Two bedrooms (box-spring + twin), two ensuites
- Family layout, pet-friendly

Penthouse 2.0 (4 sleeps, no dogs):

- Top-floor with the best long-range view from the clifftop block
- Elevator access, single-storey
- King + twin, two ensuites
- Premium Arvor

Bookable separately or as part of the seven Arvor packages — Wellness, Baby Moon, Fourth Trimester, Anniversary, Long Weekend, Summer Stay, Midweek Escape.
