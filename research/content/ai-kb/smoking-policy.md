# Smoking and vaping policy

**BLUF:** All Whalesborough cottages, Arvor Suites, the W Club spa, and the indoor area of The Weir Restaurant are strictly non-smoking and non-vaping. Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor areas away from doors and windows.

Smoking inside any accommodation will incur a deep-clean charge of £250 from the damage deposit or by separate invoice, and may result in the booking being terminated without refund. The policy applies equally to cannabis, herbal cigarettes and electronic vapes — including the smoke-free vape models — because of the lingering scent on soft furnishings.

Designated outdoor smoking areas are at the bin store near each cottage row and at the side of the restaurant's outdoor terrace. Please use the sand-filled stub urns; cigarette butts on the ground are a hazard to working livestock and to dogs.

The estate is a working farm with woodland and hay barns — fire risk during dry summer months is real. We ask all guests, smoker or not, to be mindful with disposable barbecues and bonfires (which are not permitted on the estate). Open fires inside cottages with woodburners are fine using the wood provided.
