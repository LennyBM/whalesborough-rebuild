# Late departure and late checkout

**BLUF:** Standard departure is 10:00. Late checkout to 12:00 is available for £25 subject to availability. Departures after 12:00 require the cottage to be available — usually only possible if the next booking is not arriving the same day.

Request late checkout in the app any time after arrival, or at booking. The system confirms availability instantly. Booked late checkout shows in your itinerary and the digital key remains active to the new time.

If you need to delay further than 12:00 — for example a flight is later in the evening — luggage can be left at reception while you use the spa, lunch in the restaurant or take a final walk. Reception is staffed 08:00-22:00 daily. Outside those hours, leave luggage in your car.

For genuinely flexible departures, book an Arvor day-room (£75) — same-day access to a suite for showering, working and resting between cottage departure and your final journey. Subject to availability, usually possible.
