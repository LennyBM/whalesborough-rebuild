# Kids' menu at The Weir

**BLUF:** A short, real-food kids' menu sits alongside the main menu. Half-portion versions of the adult menu are also available. Highchairs in the dining room, lakeside terrace and bar. Children's play area immediately beside the outdoor terrace.

Kids' menu structure (changes seasonally):

- Breakfast — half English, eggy bread with seasonal fruit, mini granola
- Lunch — mac and cheese with hidden veg, fish goujons with hand-cut chips, pasta with pesto or simple tomato, beef burger with potato wedges, market garden plate
- Pudding — apple crumble, vanilla ice cream with estate honey, fruit pot

Portion sizing: standard kids' portion suits 4-9; half-adult portion for 10-14; over 14 typically order from the main menu.

Allergens and dietary requirements: declare at booking and at the table. Most items can be adapted (gluten-free pasta and bread, dairy-free swap, nut-free always). The kitchen has a separate prep station for allergen work.

For picky eaters: the chef will gladly do "plain" — beans on sourdough, pasta with butter and parmesan, scrambled eggs and toast, fruit. Just ask the server.

Free children's drink (orange juice, apple juice, milk or water) with every kids' meal.
