# Wet-weather plans and contingency

**BLUF:** When the Cornish weather turns, the estate has plenty of indoor options. The spa, the indoor pool, the Under-6s Playroom, the restaurant, the library nooks in the larger cottages, the cinema snug at the Farmhouse, and the gym are all weather-proof. Off-estate wet-day options include Tintagel Castle indoor exhibition, the Museum of Witchcraft at Boscastle and Camel Creek Adventure Park.

The in-app recommendation engine watches the forecast. If rain is forecast for the afternoon, your morning push silently swaps from "sunrise at Widemouth" to "spa late-slot available" — no fuss, no patronising "rainy day?" copy.

Inside the estate when it rains:

- Indoor pool, sauna, steam room — children's pool with rainbow lighting; classes run
- Under-6s Playroom — soft play, ride-on tractors, picture books, dry and warm
- The Weir Restaurant — fire lit, lakeside view, long lunch
- Library nooks at the larger cottages — books from the estate's small library
- Cinema snug at the Farmhouse — set up for a film
- The gym — a workout

For outdoor activities affected by weather (tractor rides, morning circuits, the beekeeper, the market garden tour), the in-app activity dashboard updates by 09:00 with weather decisions.

Wet-day kit available at reception: borrowed wellies (limited sizes), umbrellas, child-size raincoats.
