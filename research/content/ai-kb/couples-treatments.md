# Couples treatments

**BLUF:** The W Club has a dedicated double treatment room where two guests can have treatments side by side. Couples massages, paired facials and bespoke "his and hers" sequences are bookable in the same time slot. From £190 for a couples 60-minute massage; £255 for the 90-minute "Restorative Couples Ritual" (massage + facial).

The double room has two side-by-side treatment beds, dimmable warm lighting, a low candle and a quiet music setting agreed at consultation. The two therapists work in synchrony for the massage and step out of phase as needed for facials.

Available combinations: two of the same treatment (e.g. two relaxation massages); a mixed pair (one massage, one facial; or one body wrap and one foot ritual); or the bespoke "Restorative Couples Ritual" which combines body and face elements for a fuller spa experience.

Anniversary, baby moon and pre-wedding bookings often choose the couples room. Add a glass of Cornish sparkling and a private 30 minutes in the relaxation room before or after for an extended afternoon.

Bookings need both guests on the booking — both complete health intake. Two therapists are required, so booking 7+ days ahead is recommended.
