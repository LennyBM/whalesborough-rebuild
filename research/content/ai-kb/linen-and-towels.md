# Linen and towels in cottages

**BLUF:** All cottages and Arvor Suites come with bed linen, pillows, duvets, bath towels and hand towels for the number booked. Beds are made up before arrival. Pool towels are not provided in standard cottages — bring your own or borrow from the spa.

Each adult sleeping space is set with a duvet, two pillows and a base sheet. Bath towel and hand towel per guest. Tea towels and oven cloths in the kitchen. In spa cottages with hot tubs, dedicated hot tub towels are provided in addition to bath towels.

Pool towels can be borrowed from the W Club spa reception during stays — return them when finished. Day visitors using the spa receive towels and robes as part of their booking.

On longer stays (seven nights or more) a mid-stay linen change is included — see the mid-stay clean chunk for the schedule. For shorter stays linen is changed only between guests.

If you need extra towels, additional bedding for a cot or sofa bed, or hypoallergenic pillows, request 24 hours ahead via reception or the in-app concierge.
