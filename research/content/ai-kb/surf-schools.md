# Surf schools nearby

**BLUF:** Widemouth Bay and Summerleaze are the two main beaches for surf lessons within 15 minutes of the estate. Several well-regarded surf schools operate from each. Lessons from £45 for a group session of two hours; family lessons from £140. Beginner-friendly; equipment and wetsuit included. Book in the app via the concierge.

Widemouth Bay surf schools include Outdoor Adventure (the largest), Big Blue Surf School and Ocean Adventure. All operate from the south end of Widemouth with classroom briefings on the beach. Suitable for beginners through to intermediate.

Summerleaze Beach (north of Bude town centre) has Raven Surf School. Conditions are slightly more sheltered — often better for nervous first-timers and small children. Lessons typically 09:30, 11:30 and 14:00 during the season.

Wave conditions: the north coast of Cornwall has consistent Atlantic swell. Best months May-September for comfortable lessons. Summer water is around 16-18°C; spring and autumn 12-14°C — wetsuits are essential and always provided.

For experienced surfers, board hire from any school. Tide info in the in-app dashboard. Pre-book lessons 48-72 hours ahead in summer.

Children under 8 generally need a parent in the water with them — discuss with the school at booking.
