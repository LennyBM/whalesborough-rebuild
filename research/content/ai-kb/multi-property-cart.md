# Multi-property cart and cross-booking

**BLUF:** The Whalesborough app supports a multi-property cart so you can add accommodation, a spa treatment, a restaurant table and a Larder hamper to the same basket and pay once. Cross-sell prompts at booking are gentle and never demanded — accommodation can stand alone or include any combination.

How it works: tap "Add to stay" on any spa treatment, restaurant booking or Larder item once you've selected accommodation dates. Each item is listed in the basket with its own date and time. The total is summed at checkout. Single payment, single confirmation, single calendar.

What's bookable in the cart:

- Accommodation (cottage or Arvor)
- Spa treatments and spa-day packages
- Restaurant tables (any service)
- Larder hampers, breakfast packs, dietary boxes
- Special-event tickets (Grill and Chill, long-table dinners)
- Activities (Beekeeper for a Day, market garden tours, photography sessions)

Pricing: each item is priced individually. There is no automatic discount for combining items. Member rates (W Club, Lakeside Locals) apply where eligible. Some packages (Arvor Wellness Package, Family Restoration) bundle treatments and hampers at a slight discount.

Removing items: tap to remove at any time before checkout. After payment, modifications follow the cancellation policy for the specific item type.

Cross-group bookings: if you are booking two cottages for the same arrival date, both go into the same basket and confirmation.
