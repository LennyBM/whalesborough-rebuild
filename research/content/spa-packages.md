## 1. Sunrise

**Slug:** `sunrise-spa-day`
**Display name:** Sunrise at the W
**Duration:** 3 hours (09:00 – 12:00)
**Price:** £80 weekday / £90 weekend
**BLUF:** A three-hour morning at the W — welcome juice, a 45-minute treatment of your choice and the run of the thermal facilities before the building gets busy. Built for early risers who want the pool quiet and the steam room to themselves.

The thing nobody tells you about a spa day is that the first ninety minutes are the best. The hydro pool hasn't been used. The sauna stones are dry. The light through the retractable windows of the infinity pool is still doing the soft thing it does between nine and ten. Sunrise gets you in for that window. Arrive at nine, change into the robe and slippers we've laid out, and start with the welcome juice (a cold-pressed seasonal blend — we'll tell you what's in it when you arrive). You've got three hours: forty-five minutes of that goes to your chosen treatment, the rest is yours to spend in the water, the steam, the sauna, the relaxation suite, or the loungers on the terrace if the wind off the Bristol Channel isn't doing its worst.

It's a solo or twos package. Friends use it as a quiet midweek catch-up. Couples like it as a Saturday morning before lunch on the coast.

**Hourly itinerary:**
- 09:00 — arrival, robe, slippers, welcome juice
- 09:15 — into the thermal suite: hydro pool, sauna, aroma steam, experience showers, infinity pool
- 10:15 — 45-minute treatment of choice (Gaia Bespoke Massage, Indian Head, Salt & Oil Scrub, or Jade Facial)
- 11:00 — back to the pool and relaxation suite until departure
- 12:00 — change, depart (or stay for lunch at The Weir — see upsells)

**What's included:**
- Welcome cold-pressed juice
- Full use of thermal suite (18m infinity pool, hydrotherapy pool, Swedish sauna, aroma steam, experience showers, relaxation suite, terrace loungers)
- 45-minute treatment of choice from the Sunrise menu
- Robe, slippers and towels

**What's not included:**
- Food (lunch at The Weir is bookable separately; mention at booking and we'll hold a table)
- Alcohol (Sunrise is a juice-and-water package — Prosecco available on the Celebrate package below)
- Treatment upgrades to 60 or 90 minutes
- Use of the gym (W Club members only)

**Lead time / booking notes:** Bookable up to the day before subject to therapist availability. Twelve-week booking window opens for Patron and Founder Circle tiers. Two guests per slot maximum unless booked as a group via the spa team.

**Suitable for:** Solo, couples, friend pairs, regulars who want minimum fuss, first-timers who don't want a six-hour commitment.

**Upsells offered:** Upgrade your treatment to 60 minutes (+£15). Add a Gaia Lifting Yoga Facial as a second treatment (+£110). Pre-book lunch at The Weir on arrival. Add Prosecco at arrival (+£12 per glass).

**Cancellation:** Cancel free up to 48 hours before. Within 48 hours or no-show, charged at full cost.

## Cross-package mechanics

**Voucher purchases (all packages):** All 14 packages are available as gift vouchers at the package-specific price. Vouchers are valid 12 months from purchase, non-transferable, non-extendable, non-refundable. Cancellation within 48 hours of a voucher-paid booking reinstates the voucher to its original expiry. No-show forfeits the voucher.

**Circle Acorns:** All packages earn Acorns at the tier-appropriate rate (5/£1 Visitor, up to 10/£1 Founder). Founder's Day earns 1.5× across the day. Direct app bookings earn the 1.2× channel multiplier; phone bookings earn the standard rate.

**Member discounts:** W Club Wellness members get 5% off treatments and 10% off retail on any package add-ons. W Club Signature members get 15% off both. Lodge owners with Signature included receive the same.

**Pre-arrival consultation form:** All packages require the pre-treatment health consultation form completed within seven days of the visit. Sent automatically on booking; reminder at 48 hours. Treatments cannot proceed without it.

**Adults-only swim windows (matters for short packages):** The 18m pool is shared with the holiday park during the day. Mon–Fri 7–9am and 6–8pm are adults-only; Sat–Sun 8–9am and 6–7pm are adults-only. If you want the pool quietest, book Sunrise (lands you in the 9–10am gap), Sunset (6pm onwards is adult-only), or Founder's Day (cabana access removes the variable).

**No-alcohol rule outside designated areas:** All Prosecco and wine offered as part of a package is consumed in the designated areas (relaxation suite, cabana, The Weir, terrace lounger zone). Bringing your own is not permitted.

**Group bookings (4+ people):** Subject to 72-hour cancellation window and 50% non-refundable deposit at booking. Final numbers confirmed 14 days ahead.
