## 10. Seasonal Vibe Switcher

**Eyebrow**
THE YEAR HERE

**Headline**
*Four seasons. Four reasons.*

---

### Spring (March — May)

The lambs come first, then the daffodils through the lane, then the kitchen garden starts feeding the kitchen again. Wood fires are still lit. The outdoor pool opens in May. Cold-water swimming weather, sometimes.

---

### Summer (June — August)

The outdoor pool runs at 26°C. The tractor goes out after Little Farmers feeding. Widemouth Bay is twelve minutes by bike. Long evenings on the Weir terrace. The estate is busiest, but the meadows absorb it.

---

### Autumn (September — November)

The light goes amber. Mushroom walks, late-season sea swims, the hedgerows in full burn. The kitchen turns toward root vegetables and game. The first wood fires return. Half-term is the quietest of the family weeks.

---

### Winter (December — February)

The indoor pool stays at 29–30°C. The spa is at its quietest. The restaurant does long lunches in front of the bar fire. The coast path is empty, the cliffs are dramatic, the dogs love it most.
