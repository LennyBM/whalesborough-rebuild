## 1. General — About the Resort

### Where exactly is Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa?
**BLUF:** Whalesborough is at Marhamchurch, Bude EX23 0JD — a 450-acre working farm estate on the North Cornwall coast, five minutes from Bude town and Widemouth Bay.

**Detail:** The estate sits between the A39 (Atlantic Highway) and the South West Coast Path, with the Bude Canal towpath walkable directly off the property. From the M5 at Exeter the drive is around 80 minutes; from London Paddington the route is train to Exeter St Davids then taxi or hire car for the final hour. We are 30 minutes from Tintagel, 15 from Crackington Haven, and 4 hours from central London by car.

### How big is the estate?
**BLUF:** 450 acres of working Cornish farm — woodland, meadows, market garden, livestock pasture, fishing lake, and the resort village.

**Detail:** Of the 450 acres, around 50 acres are newly planted woodland, the organic market garden supplies The Weir Restaurant and welcome hampers, and roughly 15 acres form the inhabited resort village (cottages, Arvor Suites, spa, restaurant, parking). The rest is grazing land, wildflower meadows, and walking trails. The estate is privately owned, fenced and gated — guests have full freedom to roam the marked trails.

## 3. Accommodation — Arvor Suites

### How are Arvor Suites different from the cottages?
**BLUF:** Arvor Suites are 22 single-bedroom adult-focused apartments designed for couples, walkers and spa-goers. Cottages are family-sized self-catering houses with full kitchens, gardens and dog access.

**Detail:** Arvor (the Cornish word for "coast") is a curated retreat product — packages bundle accommodation with spa, dining and activity credits. Each suite has a 2-person box-spring bed, smart TV, calming neutral interiors and countryside views. There is no full kitchen — a kitchenette with fridge, kettle, and microwave is provided. Most stays are 1-3 nights with optional wellness packages from £175 per person per night. The Suites sit in a single landscaped courtyard adjacent to the spa.
