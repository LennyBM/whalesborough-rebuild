## 4. INVESTMENT CLAIMS — FCA COMPLIANCE FLAGS

### 4.1 8% Guaranteed Rental Income — EXACT WORDING (for FCA review)

Page: /guarenteed-rental-income (note typo in URL: "guarenteed")

**Primary headline:** "8% GUARANTEED RENTAL INCOME"

**Subheading:** "Earn up to 8% return with guaranteed rental income at Whalesborough Resort"

**Hero copy (verbatim):** "Own a luxury holiday lodge at Whalesborough and enjoy up to 8% of your purchase price back each year for three years with our guaranteed rental income scheme."

**Detailed claim (verbatim):**
- "Guaranteed rental income of up to 8% for 3 years"
- "No hidden fees – key ownership costs are included*"
- "Hands-off letting experience – we market, manage, and maintain"
- "Enjoy up to 5 weeks of personal use per year"

**Mechanics (verbatim):** "Once you purchase your lodge, our expert team will manage everything—from marketing your holiday home and handling guest bookings, to maintenance, housekeeping, and post-stay care. Your annual rental return is fixed and paid directly to you, helping you budget with certainty while enjoying the lifestyle perks of lodge ownership."

**What's included (in the 8% scheme):**
- Insurance
- Utility bills
- TV licence and broadband
- Hot tub care
- Linen and cleaning (excluding personal use)
- Rental commission
- Ongoing maintenance

**Stay-when-you-want:** "Enjoy your lodge for up to 5 weeks each year (some seasonal restrictions apply)."

**Flexibility statement:** "You'll have options to exit the guaranteed rental scheme before the full three years are up, or switch to our managed sublet programme for a variable return after the term ends."

**Terms (verbatim):** "The 8% rental income is based on the original purchase price and applies only to new sales. Not applicable on resale properties. Lodge usage allocation and exit terms will be clearly explained during your purchase process."

> **CRITICAL FCA COMPLIANCE RISK FLAGS for Leonard/Peake Management to review:**
>
> 1. **"GUARANTEED"** in the headline is high-risk language under FCA financial promotion rules (FSMA 2000 s.21 / FCA Handbook COBS 4) if the product could be construed as a Collective Investment Scheme (CIS). Even if structurally not a CIS, "guarantee" requires the guarantor to be named and the guarantee to be genuinely contractual.
> 2. **"Earn up to 8% return"** — "return" language paired with "investment" elsewhere on the site reads as a financial promotion. Risk warnings (capital at risk, past performance not a guide, illustrative figures) are absent.
> 3. The site self-declares "we're not FCA-registered" on /the-costs — directly contradicts the regulatory expectations of marketing an income-bearing scheme. If buyers are paying for the lodge AND signing up to a managed letting scheme with a fixed yield, this could fall within FCA perimeter (CIS or alternative investment).
> 4. **"Investment"** wording is used repeatedly on homepage ("£13 million of investment", "investment options", "investment property"). For consumer-facing marketing of an income product to retail buyers, this triggers consumer duty considerations.
> 5. Single asterisk on "key ownership costs are included*" with no visible footnote definition — should be expanded.
> 6. The "up to 8%" wording is softer than "8%" but the headline contradicts itself ("8% GUARANTEED" vs "up to 8%").
> 7. The £70k/year claim on Nan's House (/cottage-sales) is an unsubstantiated income projection presented as a headline — requires "based on past performance, not a guarantee" qualifier.

## 5. BUYER FAQS (/faqs) — VERBATIM Q&A

**Phone numbers given as primary contact:** 01288 361940 / 07496 537633

### Q1: Where is Whalesborough?
Whalesborough offers holiday homeowners with the very best of both worlds. Nestled amongst 450 acres of Cornish countryside, you are only a short drive to the North Cornwall coast. The closest town is Bude (around a five minute drive) which has plenty of places to shop, eat and also offers you a couple of great beaches too. If you're in search of sand, there's also Widemouth Bay a couple of minutes in the opposite direction, which has not one but two beaches.

### Q2: What facilities are there onsite?
Whalesborough offers a wide range of facilities to our owners and their guests. These include:
- **The W Club Spa** — complete with indoor swimming pool and state-of-the-art gym. You can also book yourself in for a spa treatment or spa day.
- **The Weir Restaurant** — serving the very best local produce, some of which is grown on our own farm.
- **Walking and cycling trails** — extensive trails surrounded by countryside, the canal or the coast.
- **Children's play areas** — including an indoor area for toddlers.
- **Farm animals** — donkeys, alpacas and lambs. Animal feeding each week.
- **Outdoor swimming pool** (open in summer) and yoga areas.

### Q3: Is the site dog friendly?
All of our accommodation options on site are dog friendly and it doesn't just end with the accommodation. Whalesborough is the perfect place to explore with your dog — you can enjoy a walk down the canal to Bude or Widemouth Bay.

### Q4: Is the site sustainable?
Sustainability is at the heart of everything we do at Whalesborough and we're currently investing more in this area. We have a large solar array which helps to power some of the site, whilst we also take a keen eye on the detail; we use special filters in our washing machines to ensure particles don't end up in the sea and are currently installing lots of different initiatives such as bug hotels.

### Q5: What options are there for ownership?
We have three potential ownership options:
- Holiday cottages
- Luxury villas
- Bespoke lodges

### Q6: What entertainment is on offer for holiday home owners?
A dedicated support Team organises activities and events. Regular events exclusive for owners including Grill and Chill events at the Weir Bistro. Fishing on site at Whalesborough Lake, indoor and outdoor heated pools, fully equipped modern gymnasium, 5-star GAIA spa. Whalesborough Farm — meet and feed the animals. Off-site activities such as surfing, paddleboarding and beach trips.

### Q7: Does holiday home ownership come with any perks?
- Discount in all bars and restaurants
- 20% discount off treatments and products in the spa
- 10% water sports hire discount through 3rd party partners
- Discount to local restaurants and attractions
- Complimentary entrance to meet the animals days
- Designated owners events
- Designated sublet service if you wish to rent your home to holidaymakers

### Q8: Will my holiday home have wifi?
All of our holiday homes come with free super-fast Wi-Fi which is accessible across the park including the gym, spa and the Weir restaurant.

### Q9: How long is the season?
Whalesborough Resort and Spa is open to holidaymakers and owners for 12 months of the year. You can enjoy your holiday home at any time. The holiday home cannot be used as your main residence.

### Q10: Can I sublet my holiday home?
Yes, fully managed letting packages are available through our designated sublet service. This unique service analyses booking trends and market conditions to maximise your holiday home occupancy, with all bookings, payments, guest correspondence, key handling, and check in/check out services included.

### Q11: Can I live in my holiday home?
No, Whalesborough Resort and Spa is designed to be enjoyed as your perfect retreat, not as a permanent residence.

### Q12: Are finance options available?
Yes, we can offer finance packages available through our 3rd party specialist lenders. Our holiday homes (Villas and Cottages) are fully mortgageable (subject to status). We have a selection of pre-packed mortgage products available to help finance your holiday home.
