# RECOMMENDED RESTAURANT MODULE FOR WHALESBOROUGH APP

The Weir's current digital footprint is essentially a **brochure + phone-booking** site. The booking app is a chance to leapfrog 95% of UK village restaurants. Beyond the existing content, the module should ship:

### TIER 1 — Booking & Ordering (the missing core)
1. **Table booking** — date/time/party-size flow with live availability, indoor vs outdoor vs terrace vs dog-friendly section preference, special-occasion flag (birthday/anniversary), high-chair/buggy/accessibility requests, dietary flags at booking time (allergens routed to kitchen pre-arrival), deposit-on-card for parties 6+
2. **Walk-in waitlist** — virtual queue when full, SMS when table ready (Weir's USP is "no need to book")
3. **Pre-order at booking** — Sunday Roast pre-order, set menus for events, kids menu pre-ticked
4. **Click-and-collect / takeaway** — coffee, cream teas, hot cross buns, sandwich rolls
5. **Order-to-table** — QR code at table, view menu, order more drinks/sides without flagging staff

### TIER 2 — Events & Memberships
6. **Event ticketing** — Grill and Chill fortnightly, Valentine's, Music nights, Easter Sundays, Meet the Animals (replace Calendly), Galentine's (consolidate the Try.be flow into one app)
7. **Lakeside Locals Club, app-native** — digital card in app, push-notification each Monday with this week's offer, scan-to-redeem at till, locals-only tier in booking flow
8. **Gift cards / gift experiences** — £25/£50/£100 monetary; Sunday Lunch for Two experience; Afternoon Cream Tea gift; Spa-Stay-Dine bundle (cross-sells with resort)
9. **Private dining & function enquiries** — structured form (date, party size, occasion, dietary), suggested packages, deposit & T&Cs in-app
10. **Sunday Roast pre-pay & reserve** — set price per head, pay deposit, choose meat & sides

### TIER 3 — Loyalty, Discovery & Cross-Sell
11. **Stamp-card loyalty** — 10 breakfasts = 1 free, integrated with Locals Club tiers
12. **Allergen & dietary filtering** — toggle vegan/veg/GF/dairy-free on menu view, only matching dishes show
13. **Menu seasonality** — surface "On the specials board this week" with chef notes
14. **Supplier story interactives** — tap a dish → "where does the lamb come from?" → Land Meets Sea map pin + supplier card
15. **Cross-sell into resort** — "Stay over after dinner" inline lodge availability check; "Spa + dinner" combo offer; resort guest = automatic locals-club tier
16. **Push notifications** — booking confirmation, day-before reminder, "your table is ready" (walk-in), weekly Locals Club deal, event drop alerts, kitchen-closed/seasonal-closure heads-up
17. **Reviews capture** — post-visit prompt with one-tap to Tripadvisor/Google
18. **Wine list (proper, digital)** — currently kept on paper only; full Olivers North Coast Wines list in-app with pairings
19. **Kids' play area + animal feed schedule** — show feeding/play times so families plan visits
20. **Dog policy clarity** — "Dogs welcome inside and out" + water bowls, dog menu (sausage scrap, biscuit) for upsell

### Compliance & Ops
21. **Allergen log per booking** routed to kitchen tablet (Natasha's Law compliance)
22. **Real-time covers cap** by section to respect fire code
23. **No-show fee** policy enforcement (card hold on parties 6+)
24. **POS integration** — Toast / Square / Lightspeed sync so app bookings show on kitchen floor plan
25. **Security cleanup** of compromised sitemap as part of decommissioning old WordPress site
