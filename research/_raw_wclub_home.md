# The W Club Spa

Situated in the gorgeous countryside of North Cornwall, just a stone’s throw from the coast and a few minutes from Bude and Widemouth Bay, The W Club Spa is a place for you to relax, to recover and restore. Choose from one of our tailored Spa Days or simply enjoy a treatment of your choice.

# Facilities

The W Club comes full of everything you’ll need to relax, restore or to reach your health and wellbeing goals; from our state of the art gym area, to our extensive spa facilities; your wellbeing is our focus.

- Hydrotherapy pool
