Bringing the Kids

Cornwall's finest family resort

Looking for a family holiday in Cornwall? Whalesborough sits minutes from the gorgeous North Coast beaches at Bude and Widemouth Bay, surrounded by 428 acres of Cornish countryside. Your family can be as busy or as lazy as they like.

Choose from more than 20 cottages sized for families — sleeping 4 to 10 — all graded Five Star Gold or Five Star by VisitEngland. Every cottage has an enclosed private garden; most welcome dogs.

On Site

Everything the family needs, right here

01

Indoor Pool & Spa

The W Club features a heated indoor pool, spa treatments, sauna, steam room, and a Technogym fitness suite. Kids and adults both love it.

02

Indoor Playroom

Soft play, ride-on tractors and games keep smaller guests entertained whatever the weather. No need to watch the forecast.

03

Meet the Animals

Goats, chickens, pigs, lambs, and alpacas — all part of our regular Meet the Animals sessions. Hands-on feeding gives children an unforgettable introduction to real farm life.

04

The Weir Restaurant

Our on-site restaurant has an outdoor play area so parents can actually eat their meal. Lakeside views make it a lovely setting for everyone.

05

428 Acres to Explore

Canal walks to Bude, the Whalesborough Trail to Widemouth Bay (approx. 5km), tennis courts, and open countryside — the estate is your playground.

06

Private Hot Tubs

Many family-sized cottages come with private hot tubs. Nothing beats an evening in the tub with the stars overhead after a big day at the beach.

Nearby

Beaches within 10 minutes

Widemouth Bay

6 minutes · Dog friendly · Surf school

A wide, sandy beach ideal for families. Surf schools operate here — great for older kids wanting to catch their first wave.

Crooklets Beach

3 miles · Blue Flag · Lifeguards

Blue Flag awarded, with lifeguards during season. A firm family favourite in Bude with rock pools and gentle surf.

Summerleaze Beach

Bude town centre · Tidal seawater lido

Home to Bude's legendary tidal seawater swimming pool. A unique Cornish experience that children remember for years.

Black Rock Beach

5 minutes · Dogs year-round · Rock pools

Dogs welcome all year. Rocky outcrops and surf waves make it an adventure playground for curious children and active dogs alike.
