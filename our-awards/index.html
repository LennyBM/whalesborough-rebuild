Cornwall Tourism Awards

Gold — Ethical, Responsible & Sustainable Tourism

The highest recognition in Cornish tourism for sustainability. Whalesborough was awarded Gold in the Ethical, Responsible & Sustainable Tourism category at the 2024/25 Cornwall Tourism Awards — the most credible third-party endorsement of our environmental and social commitments in the region.

UK First

Cleaner Seas Initiative

Microplastic Filters — Every Cottage

Whalesborough became the first holiday resort in the United Kingdom to install PlanetCare microplastic filtration on every cottage washing machine. Each filter prevents up to 90% of microplastic fibres entering the water system — keeping our local river, the coastal ecology, and ultimately the ocean cleaner. An initiative as much about principle as practicality.

Ongoing · Multiple Properties

VisitEngland

5-Star Gold — Self-Catering Accommodation

VisitEngland's 5-Star Gold rating is the highest standard awarded to self-catering accommodation in England. Achieving Gold — rather than simply 5-Star — requires properties to meet exceptional standards across all assessed areas: furnishings, facilities, welcome, and overall guest experience. Multiple Whalesborough properties hold this rating.

Ongoing · Selected Properties

VisitBritain

5-Star Gold — Self-Catering

In addition to VisitEngland accreditation, selected cottages at Whalesborough carry VisitBritain 5-Star Gold status — the national tourism body's highest quality grade for self-catering accommodation across England, Scotland, and Wales. This recognition reflects the consistency and quality of the guest experience across the resort.

Partnership

Cornwall Wildlife Trust

Conservation & Biodiversity Partnership

Whalesborough works in partnership with Cornwall Wildlife Trust on the management of its 428-acre estate. Hedgerow restoration, wildflower meadow creation, and pond management programmes run alongside the working farm — helping to protect and extend the local habitat corridor for native species including dormice, bats, barn owls, and otter.
