Pre-Arrival Extras

Holiday Treats

Everything waiting when you arrive. Order in advance and we'll take care of the rest.

Available to Pre-Order

Make it Memorable

All extras are arranged at least 48 hours before arrival. Call us on 01288 361940 or email to order.

Hampers

Cornish Welcome Hamper

A curated selection of Cornish produce — artisan cheese, charcuterie, local honey, hand-baked biscuits, a jar of Tregothnan tea, and a bottle of Cornish sparkling wine. Ready on your kitchen counter when you arrive.

From £65

Serves 2. Larger hampers available for groups.

Arrival

Champagne & Flowers

A chilled bottle of Champagne or Cornish sparkling wine alongside a fresh seasonal flower arrangement from our estate garden. Perfect for anniversaries, honeymoons, or simply because you deserve it.

From £45

Choice of Champagne, Prosecco, or non-alcoholic sparkling.

Occasions

Birthday Package

A personalised birthday cake from a local Bude bakery, decorated balloons, birthday card from the team, a bottle of bubbly, and a handwritten note in the cottage. We'll sort the surprise — you just get them there.

From £80

Please book at least 5 days before arrival.

Spa

Couples Spa Package

Two 60-minute Gaia treatments at The W Club Spa — choose from deep tissue massage, hot stone, or our signature Cornish seaweed wrap. Includes spa robe use, access to the sauna and steam room, and a post-treatment herbal tea.

From £160 per couple

Subject to therapist availability. Book well in advance in summer.

Dining

Private Dining at The Weir

Reserve the private dining room at The Weir Restaurant for an exclusive farm-to-fork dinner for two or a group. Our head chef will prepare a bespoke menu using produce from the estate and local Cornish suppliers.

From £95 per person

Minimum 2 guests. Available Friday and Saturday evenings.

Dogs

Premium Pooch Hamper

An upgrade on the standard welcome pack — artisan dog treats, a Whalesborough collar tag, a plush dog bed for the cottage, a grooming kit, and a signed certificate of stay from our resident estate dog, Barley.

£35

In addition to the standard Pooch Welcome Pack included with every booking.

Wellness

In-Cottage Yoga Session

A private one-hour yoga or meditation session in your cottage or garden, delivered by one of our visiting instructors. All levels welcome. Equipment provided.

£55 per session

Available for individuals or groups up to 6.

Groceries

Essential Supplies Shop

Arrive to a stocked fridge and cupboard. Send us your list and we'll do the shopping from our partner local suppliers — fresh bread, milk, eggs, fruit, coffee, and whatever else you'd like. Charged at cost plus a £15 shopping fee.

Cost price + £15

Submit your list at least 48 hours before arrival.

Occasions

Romantic Evening Setup

Your cottage prepared for a romantic night in — candles throughout, rose petals, chocolates, a chilled bottle of wine, and the hot tub heated and ready. Everything sorted so the evening is entirely yours.

£75

Available for cottages with hot tubs. Book at least 3 days before arrival.

How it Works

Simple to Arrange

1

Book your cottage

Complete your cottage booking through Landal as normal. You'll receive a confirmation with your booking reference.

2

Contact us at least 48 hours before arrival

Call 01288 361940 or email enquiries@whalesborough.co.uk with your booking reference and what you'd like.

3

We'll confirm and arrange payment

Our team will confirm availability and take payment securely over the phone. Everything is then prepared and waiting for your arrival.

4

Arrive and enjoy

Walk in, find your extras in place, and your holiday starts exactly how it should.
