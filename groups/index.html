Stay as a Group

One resort. Multiple cottages. One unforgettable trip.

With 26 luxury cottages sleeping 2–10, Whalesborough can accommodate groups of almost any size. Book adjacent properties and you effectively have a private compound — individual privacy, shared access to the pool, spa, restaurant, and 450 acres of estate.

Call our team to discuss adjacent availability, group spa packages, and restaurant reservations: 01288 361940

For Your Group

Shared facilities, private cottages

01

The Weir Bistro

Our lakeside restaurant accommodates group bookings. A long shared table at The Weir for dinner is one of the highlights of any group Whalesborough stay. Contact us to reserve.

02

Spa Group Packages

The W Club can accommodate group spa bookings. Indulgent treatment packages for hen parties, birthdays, or milestone celebrations — call us for bespoke pricing.

03

Surf Lessons

Partnered with Free Wave Surf Academy at Widemouth Bay. Group surf lessons are great for mixed abilities — everyone gets in the water, everyone comes out grinning.

04

Heated Pools

Both indoor and outdoor heated pools are available to all guests. On a warm Cornish day, the outdoor pool is the social centre of the resort for group stays.

05

Fishing Lake

Our well-stocked onsite coarse fishing lake is great for total beginners and experienced anglers alike. A peaceful counterpoint to the more energetic activities on offer.

06

450 Acres of Estate

The estate trails, Bude Canal walk, and Whalesborough Trail to Widemouth Bay give groups who want to be active more walking and cycling than a week can fit in.
