Short Breaks

The right kind of escape

A week isn't always possible. A weekend isn't always enough. Whalesborough short breaks typically run Friday to Monday (3 nights) or Monday to Friday (4 nights), giving you the sweet spot — long enough to properly switch off, short enough to fit around real life.

All 26 Five Star Gold cottages are available for short breaks subject to availability. Book early for the best choice of dates and cottages.
