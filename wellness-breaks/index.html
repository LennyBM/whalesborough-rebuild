The W Club Spa

Your private wellness sanctuary

The W Club Spa is the heart of the Whalesborough wellness experience. A beautiful indoor pool maintained at 29–30°C and UV treated. A Swedish sauna and an aroma steam room. Treatment rooms where Cornish-made Gaia skincare is used exclusively.

Spa access is included with all cottage stays. Treatments are bookable separately and can be arranged before or during your stay. Spa days are available for visitors too.

Outside, a 17m heated outdoor pool opens through the summer months — perfect for an early morning swim overlooking the Cornish countryside.

Beyond the spa

500 acres is its own kind of therapy

Walking through ancient woodland, breathing sea air on the coastal path, watching the sun go down over the Atlantic — the Whalesborough estate offers a form of restoration that no treatment room can replicate. It simply works alongside it.

Estate trails lead through 50 acres of newly planted woodland, along the Bude Canal towpath and all the way to Widemouth Bay on the coast. Early morning circuits run every Wednesday at 7am. Or simply sit, read, and watch North Cornwall do what it does best.

What's included

Wellness facilities at Whalesborough

All facilities except treatments are included in your cottage booking.

Indoor Pool

Maintained at 29–30°C, UV treated. Open year-round. A calming environment for slow lengths or simply floating.

Swedish Sauna

Traditional dry heat sauna. Promotes circulation, eases muscle tension and provides a genuine sense of deep relaxation.

Aroma Steam Room

Warm aromatic steam with therapeutic botanical blends. Ideal for respiratory wellness and skin hydration.

Spa Treatments

Hot stone massage, jade facial, couples massage and more — all using Gaia, Cornwall's finest natural skincare range. Bookable separately.

Outdoor Pool

17m × 8m, heated to 26°C, depth 1–1.5m. Open in summer. Fresh air swimming with Cornish countryside views.

Gym

Air-conditioned fitness suite with Technogym equipment and free weights. Wednesday 7am circuit sessions available (£5pp).

Nourishment

90% of what you eat, grown here

The Weir Restaurant sources 90% of its ingredients from the estate and surrounding area — genuinely local, genuinely seasonal. The organic market garden supplies the kitchen directly, and welcome hampers for cottage guests are stocked from the same source.

Eating well is part of the wellness equation. At Whalesborough, it simply happens naturally.
