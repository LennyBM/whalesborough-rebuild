## Unique Selling Points
1. **Award-winning** — Multiple 5-star gold VisitEngland/Visit Britain ratings; Cornwall Tourism Awards Gold 2024/25 (Sustainability)
2. **Dog friendly** — All 27 cottages accept dogs; enclosed gardens, dog showers, pooch welcome packs, luxury dog bedding
3. **Sustainability leader** — First UK holiday resort with Cleaner Seas microplastic filters on ALL cottage washing machines; 60KW wind turbine; biomass boiler; 50 acres new woodland planted; organic market garden; EV charging
4. **Family-focused** — Farm animals, tractor rides, playroom, pools, nearby beaches
5. **Working farm** — 450-acre genuine working farm with organic produce
6. **Location** — North Cornwall coast, minutes from Widemouth Bay; South West Coast Path on doorstep; Bude Canal towpath walkable
7. **New Arvor Suites** — 22 eco-conscious self-catering apartments with curated wellness packages from £175pp/night
8. **Heritage character** — Properties include a Grade II listed 14th-century farmhouse, 17th century barn, and multiple original farm building conversions
