## COPY IMPROVEMENT NOTES
*Where Landal's copy is weak — opportunities for Whalesborough's own site*

1. **"400-plus acres"** → We know it's 450–500 acres. Use the precise "500-acre estate."
2. **Landal calls it "heated to 30°C"** for outdoor pool — our data says 26°C. Verify.
3. **Landal says indoor pool "complete with children's area and swim-in spa"** — our copy calls it "infinity pool at 29–30°C treated with UV light." Both angles worth using.
4. **Surroundings copy is dry and encyclopaedic** ("said to be birthplace of King Arthur") — massive opportunity for editorial, sensory writing ("where the Atlantic crashes against a cliff edge that has been whispering Arthur's name for eight centuries").
5. **Landal never mentions the sustainability story at all** — our Cleaner Seas filters, wind turbine, biomass boiler, 50 acres of new woodland are genuine editorial differentiators.
6. **No mention of Cornwall Tourism Awards Gold, VisitEngland 5-Star** on the Landal explore page — strong missed trust signals.
7. **Dog copy is functional** ("dog showers make it easy") — opportunity to be warmer and more playful ("four-legged guests get the full treatment too").
8. **Farm copy targets children only** — adults love farm access too. Reframe as "reconnecting with the land."
9. **Weir Restaurant described vaguely** as "local produce" — our copy has the farm-to-fork angle, the lake view, the specific Cornish provenance story.
10. **Accommodation descriptions are category-only** (2–4 person, hot tub etc.) — no personality. Individual cottage naming (Calf House, Eagles Nest, Trevelyan etc.) is a huge brand advantage.
