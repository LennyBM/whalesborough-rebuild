## In-Cottage Sustainability
- **First holiday-resort in the UK** to install Cleaner Seas microplastic filters on all cottages' washing-machines
- "Stopping 700,000 micro-fibres entering the sea every wash"
- Microfibres harvested and recycled into future products
- Ocean Saver cleaning products in welcome packs (plant-based, plastic-free)
- District heating system powered by biomass
