## Section: Discover the Farm at Whalesborough

"Nestled within our 450-acre estate, the Whalesborough farm is at the heart of what makes this place so special. Home to alpacas, goats, pigs, chickens, and donkeys, it's a haven for animal lovers and those seeking a true connection with nature.

We also offer Little Farmers feeding sessions, where children and adults alike can meet the animals and experience the joy of farm life. In the summer, these sessions include a tractor ride around the estate, offering stunning views of the surrounding countryside and coastline.

Sustainability is at the core of everything we do. Our organic market garden provides fresh produce for The Weir and our welcome hampers, while wildflower meadows and woodlands create a thriving environment for wildlife."
