## Overview
Arvor comprises **22 self-catering apartments** situated on Whalesborough Farm, Resort & Spa in North Cornwall near Bude.

The name derives from the Cornish word for "coast," reflecting the clifftop location overlooking rugged coastline and beaches.

**Target Audience:** "Designed for couples, walkers, spa-goers, and anyone in need of a restorative break."
