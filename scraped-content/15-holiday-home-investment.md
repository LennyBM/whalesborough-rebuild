## The Problem (addressed on page)
Rising costs of traditional second-home ownership in Cornwall. "Double council tax" policies now affect coastal properties. Brick-and-mortar ownership described as "increasingly complex" and "a substantial and unpredictable financial commitment."

---

## The Solution: Managed Holiday Lodges
Whalesborough offers "managed holiday lodges" classified as leisure properties rather than residential — avoiding council tax complications.

---

## Ownership Benefits
- **No Residential Stamp Duty:** "Save significantly on your initial purchase"
- **No Council Tax Bills:** "Avoid the rising costs and double tax premiums"
- **A Fully Managed Model:** "We handle the maintenance, landscaping, and logistics"
