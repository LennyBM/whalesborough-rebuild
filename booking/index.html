Booking questions

Is spa access included?

Yes — all cottage guests receive complimentary full access to The W Club Spa, including the indoor heated pool, outdoor pool, sauna, steam room and Technogym gym. No booking required.

Can I bring my dog?

All 26 cottages are dog-friendly at no extra charge. Up to 2 dogs are welcome per cottage. Each cottage has an enclosed private garden and there are 428 acres of walking trails on the estate.

What's the minimum stay?

Cottages are typically booked for a minimum of 3 nights, with 7-night Saturday–Saturday changeovers during peak school holidays. Short breaks are available outside peak season.

When can I check in?

Cottage check-in is from 4pm; check-out by 10am. Arvor Suite check-in is from 3pm; check-out by 11am. Early or late options can sometimes be arranged — call the team.

Can I book the whole resort?

Yes — Whalesborough is available for exclusive hire for large groups, family gatherings, corporate retreats and weddings. Contact our groups team for a tailored quote.

What is Landal?

Landal GreenParks is our booking partner for cottage reservations. They handle the secure payment and booking system on our behalf. Your stay itself is entirely at Whalesborough — Landal just manage the booking platform.

Still have questions?

Our team is available seven days a week to help you plan the perfect stay.
