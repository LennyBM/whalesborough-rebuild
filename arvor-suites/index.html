The Concept

Designed for restoration.

Arvor — from the Cornish — means coast. These 22 suites are positioned to make you feel exactly that proximity: the wind off the Atlantic, the salt-brushed air, the light that Cornwall does better than anywhere.

Each suite is self-catering, single-storey and eco-conscious. Designed with simplicity and purpose: a generous bedroom, a modern kitchen, a calming interior. And the entire Whalesborough estate — its spa, its farm, its restaurant, its 450 acres — on your doorstep.
