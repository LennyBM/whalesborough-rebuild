The Concept

Designed for restoration.

Arvor — from the Cornish — means coast. These 22 suites are positioned to make you feel exactly that proximity: the wind off the Atlantic, the salt-brushed air, the light that Cornwall does better than anywhere.

Each suite is self-catering and designed with simplicity and purpose: a generous bedroom, a modern kitchen, a calming interior. 20 of the 22 suites are single-storey with fully accessible layouts; the remaining two are duplex suites set across two floors. And the entire Whalesborough estate — its spa, its farm, its restaurant, its 428 acres — on your doorstep.

Questions

Frequently asked.

What are the Arvor Suites at Whalesborough?

The Arvor Suites are 22 new eco-conscious self-catering apartments at Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa in Cornwall. Named for the Cornish word for coast, they are single-storey, modern, and designed for couples or small groups seeking a wellness-focused break. Each suite includes a box-spring double bed, en-suite walk-in shower, fully equipped kitchen, Smart TV, and free WiFi. All Arvor guests receive complimentary full access to The W Club Spa.

How much do the Arvor Suites cost?

Arvor Suite packages at Whalesborough start from £175 per person per night. Stays are available for 1 or 2 nights and come as one of seven curated wellness packages — including Wild & Refined, Baby Moon, Fourth Trimester, Cornish Health Kick, Mindful Meadows, Group Gathering, and Paws & Paths. To book or enquire, call 01288 361940.

What is included in an Arvor Suite stay?

Every Arvor Suite stay includes: self-catering apartment accommodation (bedroom, kitchen, living area), full access to The W Club Spa (indoor pool 29–30°C, sauna, steam room, gym), access to all 428 acres of the Whalesborough estate, and the inclusions of your chosen wellness package — which may include treatments, hampers, guided activities, robes and slippers.

How do I book an Arvor Suite at Whalesborough?

Arvor Suites are booked directly by calling the Whalesborough team on 01288 361940. The team will help you choose the right wellness package for your stay and arrange any bespoke requirements before arrival. Packages are available for 1 or 2 night stays from £175 per person per night.

Are the Arvor Suites eco-friendly?

Yes. The Arvor Suites are built to eco-conscious standards as part of Whalesborough's wider sustainability programme. The resort is the UK's first holiday resort to fit Cleaner Seas microplastic filters on all washing machines, capturing 700,000 microfibres per wash. The resort holds a Gold Award from the Cornwall Tourism Awards 2024/25 for Ethical, Responsible & Sustainable Tourism.
