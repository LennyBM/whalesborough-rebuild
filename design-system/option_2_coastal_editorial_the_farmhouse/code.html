The Farmhouse

A 14th-century Grade II listed masterpiece, where medieval heritage meets the curated comfort of a modern retreat.

A Legacy Reimagined.

Sleeps 8 · Cornwall

Step through the heavy oak doors of our crown jewel. The Farmhouse is not merely a residence; it is a narrative of seven centuries. Thick granite walls and hand-hewn beams house a sanctuary of four expansive bedrooms, a dedicated snooker room, and a private courtyard featuring a secluded hot tub.

The Sanctuary Layout

Designed for effortless gatherings, the Farmhouse offers a perfect balance of communal vibrancy and private repose across two historic floors.
