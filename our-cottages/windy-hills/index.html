Windy Hills

Newly refurbished and sitting right by The Weir Restaurant, Windy Hills is Whalesborough's most refreshed single-storey cottage. The sympathetic refurbishment has brought contemporary comfort to a single-storey layout that is easy to live in — three double bedrooms, a generous garden, and patio doors connecting the living room directly to the outdoors.

The large kitchen and dining area seats six comfortably, making it a great choice for groups or families who like to eat together. Windy Hills has two bathrooms — a family bathroom and a shower room — and the enclosed garden with grass, patio, furniture and BBQ gives everyone space to spread out.

Being next door to The Weir Restaurant means dinner reservations are a thirty-second walk rather than a drive.

In Brief

Windy Hills is a newly refurbished Five Star single-storey cottage at Whalesborough sleeping 6 guests across 3 double bedrooms. It has a generous enclosed garden, a wood burner, and is situated close to The Weir Restaurant. Dogs are welcome. Full resort access is included.
