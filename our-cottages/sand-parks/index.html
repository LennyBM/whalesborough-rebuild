Five Star Gold · Visit Britain · Old Farm Workshop

Sand Parks

A Five Star Gold semi-detached cottage graded by Visit Britain, Sand Parks was converted from one of the original Whalesborough farm workshops. The exposed beams and low windows that characterise barn conversions are present throughout, lending the open-plan interior an honest, grounded quality that no new build can replicate.

The kitchen/dining/lounge flows out to the fully enclosed garden, which has direct access to communal grass areas with views toward the Bude Canal — a view that earns its keep on sunny evenings. Underfloor heating and tiled downstairs floors make it as comfortable in cooler months as it is in summer.

In Brief

Sand Parks is a Five Star Gold semi-detached cottage at Whalesborough sleeping 4 guests across 2 bedrooms. Converted from an old farm workshop, it has exposed beams, a wood burner, and an enclosed garden with views toward the Bude Canal. Full resort access is included.

Frequently Asked

Sand Parks — questions & answers.

How many people does Sand Parks sleep, and how many bedrooms does it have?+

See the specs panel above for sleeping capacity and bedroom count. Every bedroom has fresh linen and towels provided. Bed configurations and any sofa beds are listed in the Bedrooms section.

Is Sand Parks dog-friendly?+

Yes. Sand Parks welcomes up to 2 dogs at no extra charge. A luxury pooch welcome pack is provided on arrival, and the resort has a heated dog wash and 428 acres of walking trails.

What is included with a stay at Sand Parks?+

Every stay at Sand Parks includes complimentary access to The W Club Spa (indoor pool 29–30°C, outdoor pool 26°C in season, sauna, steam room, Technogym fitness suite), the 428-acre estate and walking trails, parking, Wi-Fi, all bed linen and towels, and a welcome pack. The Weir Restaurant is on-site; spa treatments are bookable separately.

What time is check-in and check-out at Sand Parks?+

Check-in is from 4:00pm on the day of arrival. Check-out is by 10:00am on departure day. Arrival days vary by cottage — please see the Changeover section above or call 01288 361940 to confirm.
