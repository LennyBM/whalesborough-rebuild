In Brief

Moleyns is a Five Star Gold semi-detached new build at Whalesborough sleeping 6 guests across 3 bedrooms. It has a Bose Bluetooth sound system, sunset balcony views, and bifold doors. Full resort access — W Club Spa, pools, and The Weir Restaurant — is included.

Bedrooms

Master King

King bed with ensuite shower and bath. Private balcony with sunset views — one of the finest outlooks on the resort.

Twin

Twin beds with south-facing balcony. Shares a shower and toilet with the super king.

Super King

Super king with south-facing balcony. Shares shower room facilities with the twin.

Kitchen & Living

The leather corner sofa — with lounger and separate two-seater — faces a contemporary wood burner and 42" LCD TV, all filled with the warm glow of the Bose Bluetooth speaker system. Bosch appliances, American fridge-freezer and bifold doors to the garden complete the picture.

Outdoor & Location

A private enclosed garden and patio with furniture and BBQ. The position of Moleyns on the resort means the W Club indoor heated pool and spa are just a short walk away — a genuine advantage for guests who want to make the most of the facilities.
