Gwari Spa Barn 2.0

Modern, classy, luxurious — the Gwari Spa Barn 2.0 is the ultimate in holiday chic. Two king ensuite bedrooms, upper-floor living bathed in light through floor-to-ceiling windows, and a private hot tub waiting in the garden.

In Brief

Gwari Spa Barn 2.0 is a modern luxury cottage at Whalesborough sleeping 4 guests across 2 king ensuite bedrooms. It has a Sonos sound system, floor-to-ceiling windows, and a private hot tub. Full resort access — W Club Spa, pools, and The Weir Restaurant — is included.

Bedrooms

Master King

King-size bed with Smart TV. Ensuite shower cubicle. Designed for complete privacy and comfort.

Second King

King-size bed with Smart TV. Ensuite shower over bath. Both bedrooms finished to the same exacting standard.

Living Spaces

The upper-floor living area is the heart of the Barn — stylish sofas arranged around the Sonos speaker system, a dining table for four, and floor-to-ceiling windows framing the North Cornwall sky. The kitchen is fully equipped with every appliance needed for a luxury self-catering stay.
