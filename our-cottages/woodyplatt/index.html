Five Star Gold · Visit Britain · Original Milking Parlour

Woody Platt

A double-storey barn conversion graded Five Star Gold by Visit Britain, Woody Platt occupies part of the original Whalesborough milking parlour. The character of that history is worn with pride — exposed beams and solid oak doors thread through every room, anchoring the contemporary interior in genuine agricultural heritage.

The open-plan living and dining area is one of the best on the resort, with countryside views framing two large sofas and an armchair. French doors lead directly to the west-facing enclosed garden. Both bedrooms — a master king with ensuite bathroom and a twin with ensuite shower room — reflect the same care with exposed beam ceilings and solid oak doors throughout.

In Brief

Woody Platt is a Five Star Gold double-storey barn conversion at Whalesborough sleeping 4 guests across 2 bedrooms. Converted from the original milking parlour, it has exposed beams, solid oak doors, and a west-facing garden. Dogs are welcome. Full resort access is included.
