Built in the 17th century as a working farm building, Whalesborough Cottage has been sympathetically transformed into a five-star gold award-winning retreat — retaining its historic character while offering every modern luxury.

In Brief

Whalesborough Cottage is a 17th-century barn conversion at Whalesborough sleeping 8 guests across 4 ensuite bedrooms. It has bifold doors opening to a private garden, a wood burner, and a private hot tub. Full resort access — W Club Spa, pools, and The Weir Restaurant — is included.

Four Ensuite Bedrooms

Downstairs Master

King-size bed with private ensuite. Ground-floor convenience with direct garden access — perfect for guests who prefer single-storey living.

Upstairs King Suite

Generously proportioned with private ensuite and estate views. Smart TV and high-quality linens throughout.

Upstairs Double

Double bed with ensuite. Light and airy with countryside outlook across the historic Whalesborough Estate.

Upstairs Double

Double bed with ensuite. All three upstairs rooms share the same elevated standard of finish and outlook.

Living Spaces

Three distinctive living spaces make Whalesborough Cottage perfect for groups who want to gather together or spread out.

Open-Plan Kitchen

Fully equipped kitchen with breakfast bar opening to a lounge-diner. Bifold doors fold back to seamlessly connect inside and outside.

Chill-Out Den

A dedicated relaxation space with beanbags and a 55" Smart TV — the ideal retreat for quieter evenings in.
