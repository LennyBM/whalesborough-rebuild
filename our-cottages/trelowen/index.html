In Brief

Trelowen Lodges are contemporary open-plan lodges at Whalesborough available sleeping 4, 6, or 8 guests. Each has full-height windows, a private hot tub, and stylish modern interiors. Dogs are welcome. Full resort access — W Club Spa, pools, and The Weir Restaurant — is included.

Our newest addition to the estate — the Trelowen Lodges bring laid-back luxury to the heart of Cornwall. A contemporary open-plan layout with full-height windows that frame the surrounding countryside. Multiple configurations available: 2, 3, and 4-bedroom lodges sleeping 4, 6, or 8. Each has its own private hot tub and al fresco dining space.

Bedrooms

Available in 2, 3, and 4-bedroom configurations. All bedrooms feature plush beds with soft lighting, calming décor, and high-quality linens. Smart TVs throughout. Call us to discuss the best configuration for your group.

The Living Space

The open-plan layout is designed to bring the outside in. Full-height windows frame the estate on all sides. Inside: Smart TV with soundbar, stylish sofas, and space designed for how people actually holiday together.
