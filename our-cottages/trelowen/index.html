Optional managed letting — up to 8% return

Earn up to 8% guaranteed rental income

For the first three years, owners who place their new lodge into the resort's managed letting programme can earn up to 8% guaranteed rental income, based on the original purchase price. The resort team handles everything — marketing, bookings, housekeeping, guest care and maintenance — and you receive fixed annual payments, with up to five weeks of personal use each year. Letting is entirely optional; many owners keep their lodge purely for private use.

Programme includes insurance, utility bills, TV licence & broadband, hot tub care, linen and cleaning, and ongoing maintenance. The up-to-8% figure is based on the original purchase price and applies to new sales only (not resale properties). Full terms and personal-use allowances are provided before purchase. This is not a financial-investment product or financial advice — please take independent advice.

Buyer Questions

Owning a Trelowen lodge — your questions answered.

How much does a Trelowen lodge cost, and how many are left?+

Just two lodges remain: a 2-bedroom from £425,000 and a 3-bedroom from £450,000. The 4-bedroom lodge has now sold. Each is sold turnkey and fully furnished — register your interest for the full price list and specification.

Do I pay stamp duty or council tax?+

Trelowen lodges are classified as leisure properties rather than residential dwellings, so the residential second-home stamp duty surcharge does not apply in the same way and there are no council tax bills. Your individual tax position depends on personal circumstances — we always recommend speaking to your solicitor or tax adviser before purchase.

Can I let my lodge out when I'm not using it?+

Yes. Owners can place their lodge into the resort's managed letting programme and earn up to 8% guaranteed rental income for the first three years, based on the original purchase price (new sales only, not resale). The team handles bookings, housekeeping and guest care; you block out your own dates, with up to five weeks of personal use a year. Letting is optional, not required — many owners keep their lodge purely for private use.

Can I buy with finance?+

Most lodge purchases are funded with cash or a specialist leisure-finance facility, as high-street residential mortgages typically do not cover leisure properties. We work with established leisure-lending partners and can make an introduction. Speak to the team to discuss options.

Is Trelowen dog-friendly and accessible?+

Yes. Trelowen is genuinely dog-friendly, with 428 acres of private trails on the doorstep. The lodges are single-storey with level access throughout, well suited to guests with mobility requirements. Call 01288 361940 to discuss any specific needs.
