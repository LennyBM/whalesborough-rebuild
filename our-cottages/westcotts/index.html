In Brief

Westcotts is a Five Star Gold cottage at Whalesborough sleeping 4 guests across 2 bedrooms. It has a large open-plan dining and living area with a wood burner. Full resort access — W Club Spa, pools, and The Weir Restaurant — is included.

Living & Amenities

The centrepiece of Westcotts is its large open-plan living and dining area — a generous, light-filled space ideal for couples or small families who want space to breathe. Two sofas, an armchair, a wood burner and easy access to the resort's full facilities make this a cottage where the simplest things feel luxurious.
