In Brief

Calf House is a Five Star Gold barn conversion at Whalesborough sleeping 8 guests. Originally a calf-rearing shed, it has a private hot tub, underfloor heating, and designer reclining furniture. Dogs are welcome. Full resort access — W Club Spa, pools, gym, and The Weir Restaurant — is included.

Bedrooms

Upstairs King (1)

King-size with ensuite shower room. Upper floor with natural light and countryside views.

Upstairs King (2)

King-size with ensuite bathroom with separate shower. The most spacious of the four bedrooms.

Downstairs Triple

Three single beds — ideal for children or a flexible group setup. Shares shower room and separate toilet with the fourth bedroom.

Downstairs Single

Single bed. Shares bathroom facilities with the downstairs triple. Convenient for ground-floor access.

Living Spaces

The heart of Calf House is a beautifully furnished lounge with a corner sofa, designer reclining chair and widescreen TV — all centred around a contemporary wood burner. Underfloor heating ensures the space is comfortable in every season. The kitchen opens directly to the dining area, with seating for nine.

An enclosed patio and garden surround a private hot tub — an intimate space that feels entirely separate from the rest of the resort. The BBQ and garden furniture make it as suited to long summer evenings as to crisp autumn afternoons. Parking for three cars on site.
