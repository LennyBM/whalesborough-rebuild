Questions

Frequently asked.

How many holiday cottages does Whalesborough have?

Whalesborough Farm Resort has 26 luxury self-catering holiday cottages on a 428-acre estate in North Cornwall near Bude. The cottages range from 2-person intimate retreats to large family properties sleeping 10 or more people. Every cottage holds a VisitEngland 5-Star rating.

Are all the cottages at Whalesborough dog-friendly?

Yes. Every one of the 26 cottages at Whalesborough is dog-friendly at no extra charge. All cottages have enclosed private gardens, and the estate provides 428 acres of walking terrain, a heated dog wash facility, and a luxury pooch welcome pack on arrival. Up to 2 dogs are accepted per cottage.

What is included with every cottage stay at Whalesborough?

Every cottage stay at Whalesborough includes complimentary full access to The W Club Spa — with the indoor heated pool (29–30°C), sauna, steam room, and Technogym fitness suite. The outdoor pool (17m × 8m, 26°C) is open in summer. Guests also enjoy access to 428 acres of estate, the working farm, organic market garden, and children's play areas.

Do any cottages at Whalesborough have private hot tubs?

Yes, a number of cottages at Whalesborough include private hot tubs. Use the filter on our cottages page to find properties with hot tubs, or call 01288 361940 for a recommendation based on your group size and dates.

What size cottages are available at Whalesborough?

Whalesborough's 26 cottages include properties sleeping 2, 4, 6, 8, and larger groups of 10+. The estate includes historic Grade II listed properties such as The Farmhouse, contemporary new-build barns, and everything in between — from romantic boltholes to multi-generational family retreats.

What is the changeover day for cottages at Whalesborough?

The standard changeover day for most cottages at Whalesborough is Saturday. Short breaks and mid-week arrivals may be available subject to availability. Call 01288 361940 or check availability online at landal.co.uk/parks/whalesborough-resort.
