Every Stay Includes

The estate is yours.

01

Spa & Pool Access

Free access to The W Club indoor pool (29–30°C), outdoor heated pool, Technogym fitness suite, Swedish sauna, and aroma steam room — included with every stay.

02

Superfast Fibre

Superfast fibre broadband across the estate. Stay connected for work — or use it to stream your favourite shows after a long day on the coast.

03

Welcome Hamper

Every guest arrives to a welcome pack sourced from the estate's organic market garden and handpicked Cornish producers. The flavour of arrival.

04

All Linen & Towels

Fresh bed linen and bath towels are provided for every guest. Beach towels available to hire. Arrive with a light bag and leave lighter still.

05

450 Acres to Explore

Estate trails, farm walks, and direct foot access to the South West Coast Path and Widemouth Bay — all from your front door.

06

EV Charging

Four super-fast EV charging points on site, managed via app. Arrive from London, leave on a full charge into Cornwall.

07

Dog Welcome Pack

All 26 cottages accept dogs. Your four-legged guest is welcomed with a Luxury Pooch Pack, soft furnishings and throws, large dog crate, and a dedicated dog shower.

08

Sustainability Built In

First UK holiday resort with Cleaner Seas microplastic filters on all cottage washing machines. 60KW wind turbine. Biomass district heating. Your stay is a green one.
