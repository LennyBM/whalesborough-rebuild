In Brief

Nettlecoombe is a Five Star Gold single-storey cottage at Whalesborough sleeping 4 guests across 2 bedrooms. It has a Jacuzzi bath, a private hot tub, and a vaulted ceiling. The single-storey layout makes it particularly suitable for guests with mobility considerations. Dogs are welcome. Full resort access is included.
