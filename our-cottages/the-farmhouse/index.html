In Brief

The Farmhouse is a Grade II listed, 14th-century farmhouse sleeping 8 guests across 4 king-size bedrooms with 3 bathrooms. It has a private hot tub, a full-size snooker table, a cinema snug, an inglenook fireplace, and a conservatory kitchen. Dogs are welcome at no extra charge. Guests receive full access to the W Club Spa, indoor and outdoor pools, gym, sauna, and The Weir Restaurant. Located at Whalesborough Farm, Marhamchurch, Bude, Cornwall — 2.5 miles from the beach. The Property

Few places in England combine genuine medieval architecture with the kind of unapologetic luxury that makes a holiday feel like an occasion. The Farmhouse manages both. The flagstone floors downstairs are original. The inglenook fireplace has warmed people through Cornish winters for seven centuries. The snooker table is full-size.

The boot room — rare even in luxury cottages — signals that this is a working estate as much as a holiday destination. Outside, the hot tub sits in a private enclosed garden with hillside views across the Whalesborough estate. On clear evenings, the horizon stretches toward the Atlantic.

Inside, the family entertainment room with cinema snug and the open-plan conservatory-kitchen-dining space give a large group of eight every reason to never need to leave — while access to our pools, spa, gym, and The Weir Restaurant means everything else is a short walk away.

The Highlights

Inglenook Fireplace

Original 14th-century stonework. There is nothing quite like the sound of a Cornish January fire in a room that has contained seven centuries of them.

Entertainment Room

Full-size snooker table, fully-stocked bar area, and cinema snug with beanbags and a large screen. The social heart of the house.

Private Hot Tub

Set in the enclosed garden with views across the estate hillside. A prerequisite, not a luxury, when you can see stars from the water on clear nights.

The Conservatory Kitchen

Open-plan kitchen with Bosch integrated appliances, American fridge-freezer, and a dining table that seats all eight. The conservatory floods the space with morning light.

Location

The Farmhouse sits at the heart of the Whalesborough estate, 2.5 miles inland from Bude's surf beaches and the stunning North Cornwall coast. The market town of Bude is a 7-minute drive, with independent restaurants, boutique shops, and the famous Bude Canal.
