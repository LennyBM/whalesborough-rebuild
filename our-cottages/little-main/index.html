Little Main

An end-of-terrace single-storey retreat graded Five Star Gold by VisitEngland, Little Main delivers a sense of space that belies its footprint. The vaulted ceiling floods the open-plan interior with natural light, while bifold windows erase the boundary between the living area and south-facing enclosed garden.

The master king bedroom features a walk-in wardrobe and ensuite with both bath and shower — a rare luxury at this scale. The twin room has its own ensuite shower room. Interior-designed furnishings, integrated Bosch appliances, and underfloor heating throughout complete the picture of unhurried, high-quality comfort.

In Brief

Little Main sleeps 4 (plus cot) across 2 bedrooms in a single-storey Five Star Gold end-of-terrace cottage. The master king bedroom has a walk-in wardrobe and ensuite with both bath and shower; the twin room has its own ensuite shower room. Vaulted ceilings and bifold windows open onto a south-facing enclosed garden. Dog friendly. Full resort access to the W Club Spa, pools, gym, and The Weir Restaurant is included.

Frequently Asked

Little Main — questions & answers.

How many people does Little Main sleep, and how many bedrooms does it have?+

See the specs panel above for sleeping capacity and bedroom count. Every bedroom has fresh linen and towels provided. Bed configurations and any sofa beds are listed in the Bedrooms section.

Is Little Main dog-friendly?+

Yes. Little Main welcomes up to 2 dogs at no extra charge. A luxury pooch welcome pack is provided on arrival, and the resort has a heated dog wash and 428 acres of walking trails.

What is included with a stay at Little Main?+

Every stay at Little Main includes complimentary access to The W Club Spa (indoor pool 29–30°C, outdoor pool 26°C in season, sauna, steam room, Technogym fitness suite), the 428-acre estate and walking trails, parking, Wi-Fi, all bed linen and towels, and a welcome pack. The Weir Restaurant is on-site; spa treatments are bookable separately.

What time is check-in and check-out at Little Main?+

Check-in is from 4:00pm on the day of arrival. Check-out is by 10:00am on departure day. Arrival days vary by cottage — please see the Changeover section above or call 01288 361940 to confirm.
