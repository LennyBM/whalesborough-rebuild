In Brief

Eagle's Nest is a Five Star Gold property at Whalesborough sleeping 6 guests across 3 bedrooms, all with balconies. Built in 2014, it has bifold doors opening to a west-facing garden and Bosch appliances throughout. Full resort access — W Club Spa, pools, and The Weir Restaurant — is included.

Bedrooms

Master King

King-size bed with ensuite shower room. Private balcony with open views. Smart TV.

Super King

Super king bed with its own balcony. Shares the family bathroom with the twin room.

Twin

Twin beds with balcony. Shares family shower room and toilet with the super king.

Kitchen & Living

The open-plan kitchen, dining and lounge flows effortlessly out to the garden through full-width bifold doors. Neutral porcelain tiles, a large dining table and a contemporary wood burner create a space that's as beautiful to cook in as it is to relax.
