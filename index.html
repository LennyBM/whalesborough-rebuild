The Heritage

A working estate, a luxury retreat.

Nestled within 428 acres of wild Cornish countryside, Whalesborough Farm is at the heart of what makes this place so special. Home to alpacas, goats, pigs, chickens, donkeys, and the occasional wandering duck, it is a genuine working estate where the rhythms of nature set the pace of every stay.

Our organic market garden supplies The Weir Restaurant and every guest's welcome hamper. Wildflower meadows and planted woodland create a thriving habitat for wildlife. And our trails lead you directly to the South West Coast Path and on to the beaches of Widemouth Bay — all on foot from your door.

Spring on the Estate

The estate
wakes up.

April through June brings Whalesborough at its most vivid. Wildflower meadows blush into colour, new arrivals join the farm, and the Atlantic long-period swells begin building ahead of summer at Widemouth Bay.

Pre-season rates are still in play — the quieter weeks before half-term are the estate at its most peaceful. Spa mornings with almost nobody else in the pool. Farm walks with lambs. Evenings beside a real log fire.

The Kitchen

The Weir Restaurant.

Using the freshest ingredients from the local area and our own farm, The Weir is a friendly and deeply relaxing place to enjoy breakfast, lunch, or just a coffee and a slice of fresh cake.

Seated beside the estate's lake, with an outdoor terrace and a children's play area, over 90% of what you eat here was grown or reared within a few miles of where you're sitting.

The Sanctuary

The W Club Spa.

Our onsite spa features an indoor infinity pool held at 29–30°C and treated with UV light, a children's pool, Swedish sauna, aroma steam room, and a Technogym fitness suite.

Our experienced practitioners use Gaia Natural Skincare — a Cornish range blending the finest plant actives and essential oils. Spa days and treatment packages available to book.

Bring the Pack

Your dog will love it
as much as you will.

All 26 cottages are dog-friendly. Every one has an enclosed private garden, a Pooch Welcome Pack on arrival, and dog-proof soft furnishings. The heated on-site dog wash means no muddy mess on the journey home.

The 428-acre estate and the South West Coast Path begin at your door. Year-round dog-friendly beaches — Widemouth Bay, Black Rock, Sandymouth — are minutes away.

  • Pooch Welcome Pack waiting in every cottage
  • Heated on-site dog wash station
