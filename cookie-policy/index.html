What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, and to provide information to the owners of the site. This Cookie Policy explains how Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website at www.whalesborough.co.uk, and is published in accordance with the UK GDPR, the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), and the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services. These include:

  • Google Analytics 4 — used to analyse website traffic and user behaviour in an aggregated and anonymised manner. Only set after analytics consent.
  • Google Maps — embedded maps on our Finding Us page may set cookies when you interact with the map.
  • Landal GreenParks — our booking partner may set cookies when you follow links from our site to the booking engine at landal.co.uk.

We do not control these third-party cookies and they are governed by the respective privacy policies of those organisations.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or manage your preferences individually.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that if you block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not function correctly. For more information on managing cookies in your browser, visit www.aboutcookies.org.
