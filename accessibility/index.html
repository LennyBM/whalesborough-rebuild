Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa is committed to ensuring that our website and resort facilities are accessible to all guests, including those with disabilities. We are working continuously to improve the accessibility of our digital presence and physical estate to provide an equal experience for everyone.

This accessibility statement applies to the website at www.whalesborough.co.uk and describes the current accessibility status of the site and our resort facilities.

Website Accessibility

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standard. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities. The following measures have been implemented on our website:

  • All images include descriptive alternative text
  • The website can be navigated using a keyboard alone
  • Colour contrast ratios meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA requirements for text and interactive elements
  • Navigation landmarks and ARIA labels are used to assist screen reader users
  • Form fields include visible labels and descriptive instructions
  • Pages include meaningful page titles and logical heading structure
  • Videos (where present) include captions
  • The site is responsive and functional across all modern browsers and device sizes

Known Accessibility Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some limitations currently exist:

  • Some older PDF documents may not be fully accessible to screen reader users. We are working to provide accessible alternatives.
  • Third-party booking content (provided by Landal Green Parks) is outside our direct control and may have its own accessibility limitations. Please contact Landal directly if you experience difficulties.
  • Embedded maps (Google Maps) may not be fully navigable by keyboard. The physical address and what3words reference are always available as a text alternative on our Finding Us page.

Resort Accessibility — Physical Facilities

Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa is set across a working rural estate and we are committed to making our facilities as inclusive as possible. Below is a summary of our accessibility provision by area:

Arvor Suites

All 22 Arvor Suites are single-storey with level access throughout. No steps or stairs. Wide doorways and accessible bathroom layouts with walk-in shower. Please call to discuss specific requirements prior to booking.

The W Club Spa

The spa building has level access from the car park. The indoor pool has pool hoist facilities available on request. Treatment rooms are at ground level. Please call 01288 361940 to discuss access arrangements in advance.

The Weir Restaurant

The restaurant has level access from the car park. Indoor seating is accessible throughout. The outdoor terrace is accessible via a gentle gradient. Accessible toilet facilities are available.

Car Parking

Designated accessible parking spaces are available near the main facilities. Blue Badge holders should contact reception on arrival for the nearest allocated spaces. 4 EV charging points are also available.

Cottages

Our 26 cottages vary in layout — some are single-storey, some are over two floors. We recommend calling 01288 361940 before booking to discuss which properties best suit your specific accessibility requirements.

Estate Grounds

The estate paths vary in terrain as Whalesborough is a working farm. Some paths are surfaced and suitable for mobility aids; others are natural farm tracks. Please ask our team for guidance on accessible walking routes on arrival.

Feedback and Contact

We welcome feedback about the accessibility of our website and resort. If you experience any barriers or difficulties, or if you have specific requirements for your stay that are not addressed above, please contact us directly:

  • Phone: 01288 361940
  • Email: info@whalesborough.co.uk
  • Post: Whalesborough Farm, Marhamchurch, Bude, Cornwall, EX23 0JD

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 working days.

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response to an accessibility complaint, you can contact the Equality Advisory and Support Service (EASS): www.equalityadvisoryservice.com.

The Equality and Human Rights Commission (EHRC) is responsible for enforcing the Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) (No. 2) Accessibility Regulations 2018 in Great Britain. However, as a private sector organisation, Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa voluntarily commits to meeting these standards as best practice.
