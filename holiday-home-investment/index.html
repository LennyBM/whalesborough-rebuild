The Whalesborough Lifestyle

Why buy a holiday lodge here

Rising costs and double council tax policies have made traditional second-home ownership in Cornwall increasingly complex. A brick-and-mortar property represents a substantial, unpredictable financial commitment.

Whalesborough managed holiday lodges are classified as leisure properties rather than residential — which changes everything. You get the Cornwall escape you want, without the complications of traditional ownership.

Ownership Benefits

Three compelling reasons

01

No Residential Stamp Duty

Leisure property classification means you save significantly on your initial purchase — a meaningful difference compared with buying a residential second home in Cornwall.

02

No Council Tax Bills

Avoid the rising costs and double council tax premiums that now affect many coastal residential properties. A managed lodge is simply a different — and smarter — kind of ownership.

03

Fully Managed

We handle the maintenance, landscaping, housekeeping, and logistics. You turn up, enjoy your lodge, and leave. No maintenance headaches, no tenant calls at midnight.
