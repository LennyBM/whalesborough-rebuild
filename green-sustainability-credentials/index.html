Our Commitment

Making sure this place is as beautiful tomorrow as it is today

From keeping microplastics from entering the sea, to becoming fully energy self-sufficient — our sustainability credentials are a record of what we've already done, and a commitment to what comes next.

The Cleaner Seas Initiative

The first UK resort with microplastic filters on every washing machine

A single wash releases up to 700,000 microfibres into the water system — and from there, into the sea. We installed Cleaner Seas microplastic filters on the washing machines of all 26 cottages.

No other holiday resort in the UK had done this before we did.

The microfibres we collect are harvested and recycled into future products. We use Ocean Saver plant-based cleaning products throughout — plastic-free, effective, and kind to the same water we're protecting.

Rewilding Whalesborough

50 acres of new woodland.
9,000 sunflowers.
A living estate.

We've planted 50 acres of new woodland across the estate, placed bird boxes throughout, and established organic market garden operations. The estate is not just a backdrop — it's an active ecosystem.

9,000 sunflowers planted for the pollinators. 22,000 pumpkin seeds to create rich habitats. Wildflower meadows that feed both wildlife and the kitchen.

We manage 428 acres with biodiversity as the outcome, not a by-product.

Green Energy

60kW wind turbine.
Fully self-sufficient.
Clean from the source.

Our 60kW wind turbine provides site-wide green power. A biomass boiler provides district heating across the estate. Air source technology heats our pools.

Our goal: full energy self-sufficiency. We are well on the way.

Four super-fast EV charging points are available on-site, managed via app. A network of footpaths connects to the Bude Canal towpath and Sustrans cycle tracks for guests who want to arrive — and explore — without a car.

Transparency · Our Roadmap

What we've done, what we're doing, and what comes next

We don't just report on achievements. We publish our ongoing commitments, so you can hold us to them.

Complete

Microplastic filters across all 26 cottages

UK-first installation of Cleaner Seas filters across every cottage washing machine on the estate. Microfibres are harvested and recycled — not discharged into coastal waters.

Complete

60kW wind turbine powering the estate

Our on-site turbine, combined with biomass district heating and air source heat pumps, provides clean energy across the entire estate. Grid dependency significantly reduced.

Complete

50 acres of new woodland and wildflower habitat

Working in partnership with local conservation partners, we've transformed former agricultural margins into thriving wildlife corridors, wildflower meadows, and managed woodland.

In Progress

Full energy self-sufficiency by 2027

We're currently at 65% self-sufficiency. Additional solar capacity across cottage rooflines and a battery storage system are planned to close the gap.

In Progress

Annual biodiversity audit publication

We've commissioned ongoing species surveys across the estate. Results will be published annually — invertebrate counts, bird nesting data, hedgerow health — making our impact visible and verifiable.

Planned · 2026

EV charging expansion + cycle hire scheme

Expanding from 4 to 12 EV charging bays, plus partnering with a local cycle hire operator to offer guest bike hire direct from reception — making car-free exploration genuinely possible.
