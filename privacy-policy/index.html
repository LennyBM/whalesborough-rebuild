Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Who we are

Whalesborough Farm Resort & Spa is operated by Landal GreenParks UK Ltd. Our resort is located at Whalesborough Farm, Marhamchurch, Bude, Cornwall EX23 0JD.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, make a booking, or contact us.

What information we collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, email address, telephone number, and postal address (when you make a booking or enquiry)
  • Payment information (processed securely via our booking provider, Landal GreenParks)
  • Details of your stay (cottage, dates, number of guests)
  • Communication history (emails or calls with our team)
  • Website usage data via cookies (see our Cookie section below)

How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and manage your booking
  • Communicate with you about your stay
  • Send you relevant offers and information (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Lawful basis for processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: to fulfil your booking and provide agreed services
  • Legitimate interests: to improve our services and communicate with existing customers
  • Consent: for marketing communications, which you can withdraw at any time
  • Legal obligation: where required by law

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Landal GreenParks (our booking and management partner) for the purposes of processing your reservation
  • Payment processors to handle secure transactions
  • Third-party service providers who support our operations (under strict data processing agreements)
  • Regulatory authorities where required by law

Data retention

We retain your booking data for up to 7 years for accounting and legal purposes. Marketing preferences are retained until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and analyse site traffic. We use:

  • Essential cookies: necessary for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: to understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)
  • Marketing cookies: only with your consent, to show relevant advertising

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings at any time.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at enquiries@whalesborough.co.uk or write to us at the address above.

Contact & complaints

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

Email: enquiries@whalesborough.co.uk
Tel: 01288 361940
Address: Whalesborough Farm, Marhamchurch, Bude, Cornwall EX23 0JD

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe we have not handled your data appropriately.
