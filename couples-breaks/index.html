Stay as a Couple

Your North Cornwall escape

The North Cornwall Coast is one of the most breathtaking stretches of coastline in Britain. From your private cottage at Whalesborough, you're minutes from Widemouth Bay, the South West Coast Path, and some of the finest beaches in the country.

Back at the resort, the W Club spa, heated indoor and outdoor pools, The Weir Bistro, and 500 acres of private countryside are all yours to enjoy at whatever pace suits you best.

Experiences

Made for two

01

Private Hot Tub

Many of our cottages have private hot tubs. Champagne, stars, and no neighbours in earshot — that's a Whalesborough evening done right.

02

W Club Spa

Our luxurious spa is the perfect place to unwind. Choose from indulgent couples treatments including hot stone massage and facial rituals.

03

Heated Pools

Both indoor and outdoor pools are heated to a balmy temperature year-round. The outdoor pool is a particular treat on a clear Cornish day.

04

Dining at The Weir

Our bistro overlooks the lake — a beautiful setting for a candlelit dinner. Local seafood, seasonal menus, and Cornish wines on the list.

05

500 Acres to Explore

Get lost in glorious Cornish countryside together. The estate trails lead to Bude Canal and across to Widemouth Bay — wild, private, beautiful.

06

Surf Lessons

In partnership with Free Wave Surf Academy — catch your first wave together at Widemouth Bay. Guaranteed memories, guaranteed laughs.
